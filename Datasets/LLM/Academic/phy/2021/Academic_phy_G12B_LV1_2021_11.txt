The proposed Chaos Energy Bound offers a potentially powerful tool for characterizing and classifying chaotic dynamics across a range of systems, from many-body quantum systems to classical mechanical networks.  Furthermore, we discuss implications for the relationship between OTOC behavior and the underlying Lyapunov spectrum, suggesting a tighter interplay than previously recognized.

"In the realm of materials chemistry, the adoption of FAIR (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable) data principles is paramount for catalyzing scientific collaboration and knowledge discovery. As of 2024, the integration of FAIR data practices into materials chemistry research fosters optimal resource utilization, enhances data-driven insights, and streamlines the discovery of novel materials.

"Atypical Presentation of Symptomatic Pulmonary Hamartoma: A Rare Clinical Entity. 
In 2021, a unique case of pulmonary hamartoma was diagnosed in a 44-year-old woman, presenting with unusual demographic and clinical characteristics. This benign lung tumor, typically asymptomatic, manifested symptomatically, challenging conventional clinical expectations. The case highlights the complexities in managing atypical presentations of pulmonary hamartoma. A review of current literature and clinical management strategies is presented, underscoring the need for nuanced approaches in diagnosing and treating symptomatic cases of this rare condition."

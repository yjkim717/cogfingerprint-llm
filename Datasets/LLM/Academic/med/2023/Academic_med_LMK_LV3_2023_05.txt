This study examines the updated NCCN Clinical Practice Guidelines for squamous cell anal carcinoma, incorporating recent advancements in staging classification and systemic therapy recommendations. In accordance with the AJCC staging system, the revised guidelines provide a more precise framework for managing localized and metastatic disease. The integration of chemoradiation as a primary treatment modality is reaffirmed, with refined recommendations for systemic therapy in cases of metastatic disease. As of 2023, these updates aim to standardize treatment approaches and improve patient outcomes, underscoring the importance of evidence-based practice in anal carcinoma management.

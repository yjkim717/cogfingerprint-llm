This study evaluates the efficacy of Omnipose, a novel deep neural network algorithm, in segmenting bacterial cells with varied morphologies. Leveraging advancements in image segmentation and deep learning, Omnipose achieves high-precision delineation of bacterial cells, surpassing existing methods such as Cellpose. Our analysis, conducted in 2021, demonstrates Omnipose's superior performance in accurately identifying and segmenting diverse bacterial morphologies. The results underscore the potential of Omnipose to enhance microbiological research by providing robust and reliable cell segmentation, thereby facilitating quantitative analyses of bacterial populations and informing future investigations in the field.

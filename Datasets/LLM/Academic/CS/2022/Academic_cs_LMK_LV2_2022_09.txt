Title: Examining the Nexus between Principals' Technology Leadership and Teachers' Technology Integration in Palestinian Public Schools

Abstract:
This quantitative study investigates the correlation between principals' technology leadership and teachers' technology integration in Palestinian public schools, contextualized within the 2022 educational landscape. Employing a survey-based approach, the research reveals a statistically significant positive relationship between visionary technology leadership and teachers' effective technology integration. The findings underscore the importance of professional development and digital citizenship in mediating this relationship, suggesting that targeted interventions can enhance technology integration and foster a digitally adept educational community.

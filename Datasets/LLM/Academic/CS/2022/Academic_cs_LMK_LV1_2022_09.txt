Title: Investigating the Impact of Principals' Technology Leadership on Teachers' Technology Integration in Palestinian Public Schools

Abstract (2022): This study examines the relationship between principals' technology leadership and teachers' technology integration in Palestinian public schools. Using a quantitative approach, data was collected from 250 teachers via a survey instrument assessing principals' visionary leadership, digital citizenship, and professional development support. Results indicate a significant positive correlation between principals' technology leadership and teachers' technology integration. Specifically, principals' visionary leadership and support for professional development emerged as significant predictors of teachers' technology integration, highlighting the crucial role of effective technology leadership in fostering innovative teaching practices.

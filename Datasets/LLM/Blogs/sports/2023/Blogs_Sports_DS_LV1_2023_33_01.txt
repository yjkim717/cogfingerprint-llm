There’s something special about an undefeated streak—it’s like watching a perfect sunrise day after day. For weeks, the PAC-12 rolled through the 2023 college football season untouched, stacking up 19 straight wins. As the last conference standing without a loss, it felt almost magical. But in sports, perfection is fragile. When that first loss finally came, it wasn’t just a score change—it was a moment of collective pause. It reminded us that even the strongest runs meet their match, and that’s what makes the game so compelling. Here’s to the thrill of the chase and the lessons in every fall.

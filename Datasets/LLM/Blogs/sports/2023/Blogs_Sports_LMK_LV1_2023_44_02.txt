The highs and lows of the cannabis industry are a lot like the ups and downs of being a sports fan. Just as a team can go from championship contenders to cellar dwellers in a season, a company like Lantern can be thriving one day and shutting down the next due to regulatory hurdles. As a fan, it's hard not to feel for the team - or in this case, the employees of Lantern in Massachusetts. Will federal legalization be the game-changer that helps the industry score big? Only time will tell.

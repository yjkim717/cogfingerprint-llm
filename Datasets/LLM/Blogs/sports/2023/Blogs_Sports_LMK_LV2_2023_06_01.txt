The 2023 NBA playoffs had a shocking upset - the Cavaliers' early exit at the hands of the Knicks. Despite boasting a more talented roster, Cleveland crumbled under pressure. Donovan Mitchell's impressive skills weren't enough to overcome the Knicks' gritty defense. As a fan, it's disheartening to see talent not translate to playoff success. The series will be remembered for the underdogs' triumph and the Cavs' what-ifs.

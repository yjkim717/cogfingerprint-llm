## The Jazz and the Tightrope Walk

It's 2023, and the Utah Jazz are giving us something to think about. Their win percentage this season has been… surprisingly good. But here's the thing: weren't they *trying* to tank? It’s a weird feeling, rooting for a team to lose while also admiring their play. Maybe it’s a sign that the future could be brighter than we initially thought. A little unexpected success can be a powerful thing, right?

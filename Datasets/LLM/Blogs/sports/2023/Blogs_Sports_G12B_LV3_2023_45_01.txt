## The Unexpected Spark: Marcus Morris and Clippers Grit

The Clippers have faced some tough breaks this playoff season, and watching Kawhi Leonard sidelined definitely stings. But it's been fascinating to see guys like Marcus Morris step up. His stats might not scream hero, but those plus/minus numbers? Seriously impressive. It’s a reminder that sometimes, playoff basketball isn't about the highlight reel, but about grit, hustle, and finding a way to contribute when it matters most. Ty Lue’s squad needs that.

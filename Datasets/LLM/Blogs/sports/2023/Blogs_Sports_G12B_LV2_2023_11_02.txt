## The Unexpected Joy of Watching My Team Lose

Okay, hear me out. I *love* my baseball team, the [Insert Your Team Name Here]. I’ve cheered them on through thick and thin, yelled at the TV, and worn my jersey with pride. But this season? They've been… rough. And you know what? It's been surprisingly refreshing.

**The Deal That Felt…Off**

It’s weird, isn’t it? Like you’re being offered a shortcut, a clever trick to get what you want. I recently got a car deal that had me seriously scratching my head. The dealership was pushing a lease with a rebate, then suggesting a refinance to buy it. It felt a little too smooth, a little too orchestrated.  Makes you wonder if there's more going on than just a good offer.  It’s a reminder to always dig a little deeper and trust your gut – especially when it comes to big purchases!

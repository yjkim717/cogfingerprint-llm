## The Unexpected End of a Season

It’s late October 2023, and the crisp air usually has me buzzing with excitement for high school football. But this week, seeing a heartbreaking loss on the field hit differently. It wasn't just about the score; it was the palpable disappointment, the shattered dreams on those young faces. It’s a stark reminder that even in the most competitive arenas, sometimes things just don’t go as planned, and that’s okay.

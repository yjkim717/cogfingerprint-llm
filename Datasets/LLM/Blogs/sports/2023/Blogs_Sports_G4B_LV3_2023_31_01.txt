It’s funny how fiercely we can root for people we’ve never even met, isn’t it? This year, watching Taskmaster felt like that. Frankie, Ivo, Kiell, and Jenny – a tight group battling it out with ridiculous challenges. You find yourself instinctively hoping for one over the other, like a friendly, absurd competition. It reminds you that sometimes, the most compelling drama isn't about winning, but about the shared experience of watching someone else try their best.

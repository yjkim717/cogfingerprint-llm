## The Unexpected End of the Game – and What It Means for the Field

Remember the excitement around Lantern, the Massachusetts marijuana delivery service? Its recent shutdown hit me hard, not because I'm a customer, but because it feels like watching a promising underdog stumble. It’s a stark reminder that even in booming industries, like cannabis – and, honestly, even in sports – things can change fast. 

The regulatory hurdles and the looming shadow of federal legalization, plus the potential for big corporations to swoop in…it’s a tough landscape. It makes you wonder, just like a team facing a sudden roster shakeup, how adaptable and resilient we all need to be to survive and thrive.

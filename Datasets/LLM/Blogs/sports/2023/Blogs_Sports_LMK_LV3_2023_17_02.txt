"Shifting Gears: A Pit Stop for Electric Dreams"

As I watched the latest electric vehicles zoom by at the 2023 auto show, I couldn't help but think of my own ride. Leasing a new Hyundai to snag that tempting $7500 rebate and then refinancing to make it mine was tempting. But is it a smart move? Like a pit crew fine-tuning a car, we must fine-tune our finances. Is leasing and refinancing an electric vehicle a pit stop on the road to savings or a detour to debt?

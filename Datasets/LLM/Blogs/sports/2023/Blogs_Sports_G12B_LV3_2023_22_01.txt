## The Swing and a Whole Lotta Heartbreak

Okay, fellow sports fans, let's be real. This baseball season has been…rough. I poured my heart into believing the local team could make a run, decked myself out in merch, the whole nine yards. And then… well, you know. Watching them stumble felt like a personal defeat, honestly. It's a reminder that even with all the hope and cheering, sometimes the game just doesn't go your way.

## The PAC-12's Run and the Beauty of the Unexpected

Anyone following college football this year witnessed something special – the Pac-12's absolutely electric start to the 2023 season. For 19 games straight, they were untouchable, a force dominating the scoreboard. It felt… almost destined, didn't it? 

Then, reality hit. Losses happened. Streaks ended. And you know what? It was a powerful reminder of why we love sports so much. It’s not about the guaranteed win; it’s about the journey, the drama, the unpredictable nature of competition. 

The Pac-12's undefeated run was a beautiful story, but its eventual end just underscored the thrill of college football. It’s a testament to the fact that even the most impressive momentum can be challenged, and that's what keeps us coming back for more.

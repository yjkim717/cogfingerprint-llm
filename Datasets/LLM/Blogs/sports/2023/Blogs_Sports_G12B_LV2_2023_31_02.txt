## The Missed Call and the Lingering Doubt

Remember that feeling when a crucial call goes against you? Like, *really* against you? I had one of those moments at a local basketball game last weekend. My team was down by one, seconds on the clock, and I swear, *swore*, I saw the foul. The ref didn't. 

It's more than just losing a game, you know? It’s that lingering doubt. Did I see it wrong? Was I just *hoping* to see it wrong? It's a bit like arguing a point, trying to be heard, feeling like you're presenting a solid case, and still… nothing. 

It's a tough pill to swallow.  Sports, like life, sometimes hands you a result you don’t feel you earned.  But you dust yourself off, learn from it, and get ready for the next play. Right? 2023 and still learning that lesson!

## The Unexpected Joy of Watching Him Learn

It’s been a strange 2023 watching my nephew, Leo, tackle skateboarding. He's utterly fearless, launching himself with the confidence of a seasoned pro, then face-planting spectacularly. But the *grit*! The way he dusts off, analyzes, and tries again? It’s more thrilling than any championship game. It's a reminder that growth, even messy growth, is the real victory.

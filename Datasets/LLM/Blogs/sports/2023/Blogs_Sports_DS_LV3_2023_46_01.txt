Every year, the NFL draft captivates us with the promise of a new franchise quarterback. Looking back at the 2023 class, I can’t help but reflect on the immense pressure these young men face. We chart their careers by pass attempts, each throw a tiny referendum on their potential. It’s a stark reminder that a draft pick is just the beginning; the real story is written on the field, one play at a time, under the weight of a city’s hopes.

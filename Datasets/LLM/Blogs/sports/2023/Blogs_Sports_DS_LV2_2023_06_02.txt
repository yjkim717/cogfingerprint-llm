It’s 2023, and I can’t help but wonder: what if? What if Harden hadn’t been hobbled, or Kyrie was fully healthy for that Game 7? We celebrate championship teams and their legacies, but the shadow of the "what if" is a story just as compelling. It’s a reminder that sometimes, the title of "healthy warriors" is the most crucial stat of all. The finest line in sports isn't always on the court; it's between being available and being injured.

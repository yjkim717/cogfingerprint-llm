## The Empty Stands & a Different Kind of Roar

2020 felt…off, didn't it? Watching the tennis, the football, anything really, with empty stadiums was surreal. You missed the collective gasp, the roar of the crowd. But strangely, it highlighted the *athletes* even more. Their dedication, battling on, even without that energy feeding them? That’s a level of grit we can all admire.

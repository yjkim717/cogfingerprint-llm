As I laced up my running shoes in the summer of 2020, I realized my athletic playlists needed a refresh. Migrating my Google Play Music library to YouTube Music was a breeze, but I was frustrated to find the web version didn't sort artists alphabetically. Like a perfectly organized sports locker, a tidy music library matters. After some trial and error, I finally streamlined my tunes and hit the pavement, ready to tackle new personal bests.

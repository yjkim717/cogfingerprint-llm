"Valorant Vibes: Beyond the Combat Score" 

As I dove into the world of Valorant in 2020, I found myself fixated on my combat score, convinced it held the secret to my match performance. But after a string of puzzling losses, I began to question its true worth. Was a high score really a reliable measure of my Sage gameplay? I dug deeper, analyzing my MMR rating and match stats. The truth hit me - combat score was just one piece of the puzzle. My overall performance was about strategy, teamwork, and adaptability. It was time to redefine what winning meant to me.

>## The West is West, and That Means a Fight

Remember that feeling last year? The weird, truncated season, the bubble… it felt like the entire NBA was holding its breath. But looking ahead to 2021, something feels different. The Western Conference is *stacked*. Seriously, almost every team has legitimate playoff aspirations. 

It’s refreshing, honestly. No obvious tanking attempts, no easy paths. Just a whole lot of teams battling tooth and nail. Makes you appreciate the grind, doesn't it? Bring on the chaos! The offseason moves just fueled the fire. Let the games begin!

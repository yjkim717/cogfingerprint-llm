Remember 2020? When everything felt upside down, even in college football. I miss those annual grudge matches against our ACC neighbors. Now, with scheduling grids locked tight, finding a date feels like a puzzle. We risk losing a piece of our identity for a shot at the SEC Championship. It’s a strange choice: honor tradition or chase a title. The heart says to keep the state rivalry alive, but the new conference math makes it so difficult. What’s a classic game worth these days?

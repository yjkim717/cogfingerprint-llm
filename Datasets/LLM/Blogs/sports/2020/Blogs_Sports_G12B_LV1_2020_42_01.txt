## That Little Wifi Icon Got Me Thinking...

Remember the chaos of 2020? Games were postponed, stadiums were empty. I was glued to my laptop, following every update. And honestly, even the small things felt significant. Like that ChromeOS update – the new icons, and that half-full wifi symbol when the connection dipped? It mirrored the season. Hope flickering, sometimes strong, sometimes just… half there. We were all just trying to stay connected, chasing a signal in a strange year.

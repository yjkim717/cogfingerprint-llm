"Empowering Women Through Self-Defense: A Step Towards a Safer Tomorrow. As we navigated the challenges of 2020, it became increasingly clear that women's empowerment is crucial to creating a safer society. One effective way to achieve this is by incorporating self-defense training into sports education. By teaching women the skills to protect themselves, we can reduce the risk of rape and foster confidence. It's time to rethink our approach to physical education and prioritize self-defense, empowering women to take control of their safety and well-being."

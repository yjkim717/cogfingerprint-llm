"Empowering Women Through Sports: A Step Towards Rape Prevention"

As we navigate 2020, it's clear that women's empowerment is crucial for creating a safer society. One effective way to achieve this is by incorporating self-defence training into physical education programs. By teaching women the skills to protect themselves, we can reduce the risk of rape and promote confidence. Sports like martial arts and kickboxing not only improve physical fitness but also equip women with the knowledge to defend themselves. Let's harness the power of sports to empower women and prevent rape.

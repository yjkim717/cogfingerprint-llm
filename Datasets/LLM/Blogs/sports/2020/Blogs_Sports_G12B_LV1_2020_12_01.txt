## The Unexpected Sync

2020 was… a lot. Watching the NBA bubble felt surreal, but also strangely comforting. I’d always been a casual fan, but something about the focused intensity, the way players and coaches seemed completely locked in, really resonated. It was like watching a perfectly synced system.  Suddenly, understanding the nuances of rotations and defensive schemes felt as satisfying as finally getting a new piece of hardware to play nicely with my DAW.  A weird parallel, I know, but both offered a sense of order in a chaotic year.

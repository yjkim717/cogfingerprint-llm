## That Runner on Second Feeling...and 2018 Memories

Remember 2018? The Red Sox were unstoppable, a whirlwind of offense. Looking back at the MLB stats, something always stuck with me – their incredible hitting with a runner on second. It was almost…too good. Now, with the shadow of those cheating allegations hanging over everything, it's hard not to feel a pang of unease. Did that dominance feel different? Did it *mean* different? It’s a reminder that even in sports, the thrill of victory can be tangled with complicated questions.

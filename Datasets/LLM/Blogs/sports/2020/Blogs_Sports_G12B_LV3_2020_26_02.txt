## Remembering the "What If" of the 2020 Thunder

It feels strange to think back to 2020, doesn't it? So much was upended. I was scrolling Reddit the other day and someone brought up the Oklahoma City Thunder's abruptly ended season. It really struck me. They were *building* something. While the win total might not have been spectacular, you could feel the franchise progress, even with the weirdness of COVID-19 impacting everything. It’s a real "what if" scenario – what could they have achieved with a full season?

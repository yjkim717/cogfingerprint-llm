>## The Unexpected Stillness of 2020

Remember the roar of the crowd? 2020 feels like a lifetime ago, doesn't it? I miss the electric energy of live games, the shared anticipation with strangers. My usual Saturday ritual – tailgating and cheering on my team – vanished. It was strange, this sudden quiet. But amidst the disruption, I found a new appreciation for the simple joy of the sport itself, watching from my living room, just me and the game. It wasn't the same, but it was something.

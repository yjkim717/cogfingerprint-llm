## The Quiet of the Empty Stands

Remember the roar? 2020 felt…wrong without it. Watching games on TV, the silence was deafening. My usual game-day ritual – the crowded stadium, the shared anticipation – just vanished. It made me appreciate those packed bleachers more than ever. Hoping we get back to that energy soon. It’s more than just a game, isn't it?

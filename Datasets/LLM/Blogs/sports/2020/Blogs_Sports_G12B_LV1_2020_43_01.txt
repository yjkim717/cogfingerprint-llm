## The Unexpected Joy of Badminton in Lockdown

Remember 2020? Stuck inside, craving any kind of competition? I rediscovered badminton in my tiny apartment. My sister and I, controller versus keyboard – okay, badminton racket versus sheer determination – battled it out. It wasn't graceful, it was often hilarious, and it was *perfect*. A little friendly rivalry, a lot of laughter, and a surprising workout. Who knew a shuttlecock could bring so much joy?

## The Jazz and the Road: A 2020 Reflection

It's been a strange NBA season, hasn't it? The Utah Jazz have shown flashes of brilliance, climbing the ranks and looking like legitimate playoff contenders. Yet, there's this frustrating pattern. Away games against teams with winning records? It's a real struggle. We're seeing a record surprisingly similar to the Pelicans and Raptors – not exactly what we envisioned. It makes you wonder, what’s the key to unlocking that road consistency? It's a question bouncing around every Jazz fan’s head this year, that's for sure.

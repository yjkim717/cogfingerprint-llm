**The Price of a Better Swing**

Remember 2020? It felt like everything was costing more – lessons, groceries, sanity! I was staring down at my driver, feeling hopelessly inconsistent. I’d spent a small fortune on range time, convinced more reps were the answer. Then, I tentatively booked a session with a local golf coach.  It wasn’t cheap, but the focused feedback – pointing out *why* I was slicing, not just *that* I was slicing – was a game-changer.  Sometimes, investing in a bit of expert guidance is far more valuable than simply grinding it out alone.

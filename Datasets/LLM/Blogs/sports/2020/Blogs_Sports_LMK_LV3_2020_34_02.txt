As the 2020 college football season unfolds, SEC teams are wrestling with a scheduling puzzle. With the NCAA mandating nine games, some teams are considering non-conference matchups against fellow SEC rivals. This creative solution would not only satisfy the NCAA's requirements but also deliver exciting, high-stakes games for fans. By playing each other outside of conference play, teams can create a thrilling slate of games while navigating the challenges of the ever-evolving college football landscape.

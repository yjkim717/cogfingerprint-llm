## The Quiet of the Stands

Remember the roar? The collective gasp, the joyous explosion of a crowd? 2020 feels like a lifetime ago in so many ways, but I especially miss the energy of live sports. I know, it sounds silly amidst everything that’s been happening. But there was something about being packed into a stadium, surrounded by strangers united by a shared passion.

This year, watching games on my couch just hasn’t been the same. The piped-in crowd noise feels…hollow. It lacks the unpredictable, messy, *real* feeling of thousands of people reacting together.  It made me realize how much I craved that communal experience, that sense of belonging, even if it was just for a few hours. 

It’s a small thing, I know, but it’s a reminder of all the simple joys we took for granted. I’m holding onto the hope that eventually, we’ll hear that roar again. And when we do, it'll be even sweeter.

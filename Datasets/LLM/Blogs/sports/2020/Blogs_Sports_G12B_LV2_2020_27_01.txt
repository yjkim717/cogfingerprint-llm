

## The Unexpected Joy of the Underdog

Remember 2020? A year that threw curveballs like nobody's business. Watching the Leicester City Premier League win that year felt like a tiny, beautiful rebellion against all the chaos. Nobody expected it. They were the ultimate underdog. And honestly, cheering for them, witnessing that improbable triumph, was a pure shot of joy in a year desperately needing it. Sometimes, the unexpected victories are the sweetest.

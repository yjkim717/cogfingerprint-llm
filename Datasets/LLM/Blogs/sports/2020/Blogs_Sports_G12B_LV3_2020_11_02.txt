## The Unexpected Pause and the Quiet Game

Remember 2020? The world felt… paused. Like a game put on hold. I found myself strangely drawn to watching old baseball games, the familiar crack of the bat a comforting constant amidst the uncertainty. It made me think about the beauty of routine, of dedication, and how even in stillness, there's a quiet kind of striving. It’s a lesson I’m carrying forward, even as things slowly return to play.

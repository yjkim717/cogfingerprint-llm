"Revisiting My Childhood Sports Dreams in 2020"

As I watched the sports world adapt to the pandemic in 2020, I couldn't help but think back to my own youthful athletic ambitions. Like many kids, I dreamed of becoming a pro athlete, but injuries and reality set in. Yet, watching athletes persevere through adversity reignited that spark. It reminded me that it's never too late to pick up where I left off and that sometimes, revisiting old passions can lead to new beginnings.

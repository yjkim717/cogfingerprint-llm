## Beyond the Rules: Finding Strength Where It Really Matters

It’s 2020, and we’re constantly talking about safety, about changing systems. And rightfully so. But lately, I’ve been thinking about something else – the quiet strength built in a boxing gym, the confidence forged on a soccer field. 

I remember feeling utterly powerless after a scary encounter a few years back. Then I started kickboxing. It wasn't just about learning to throw a punch, it was about reclaiming control, about knowing I *could* protect myself. 

Maybe, just maybe, alongside calls for legal reform, we need to champion women's fitness and sports. Not as a solution in itself, of course, but as a powerful tool for building resilience, self-assurance, and a sense of agency that no law can fully provide.

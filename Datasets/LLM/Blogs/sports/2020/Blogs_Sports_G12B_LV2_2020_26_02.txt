## What If? A Thunder Season of Could-Have-Beens

Remember the 2019-20 Oklahoma City Thunder? It felt…tumultuous. A weird mix of grit and uncertainty. Looking back, it’s hard not to wonder: with a full, uninterrupted season, would they have cracked 40 wins? It’s just one of those "what if?" sports moments that sticks with you, a reminder of potential unrealized.

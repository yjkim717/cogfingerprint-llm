**The Unexpected Silence of the Stadiums**

Remember 2020? It felt like everything was…shifting. Like a sudden closure, right? I was a huge baseball fan, and watching the season get canceled felt like a punch to the gut. Just as abruptly as the games disappeared, so did the buzz – the hotdogs, the chatter, the feeling of being part of something bigger. It made you realize how much we rely on those shared experiences, those familiar rhythms.

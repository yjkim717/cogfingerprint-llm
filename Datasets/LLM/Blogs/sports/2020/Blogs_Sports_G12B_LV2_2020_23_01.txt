## The Roar From the Couch vs. The Grind on the Field

Remember the energy of 2019? Packed stadiums, the communal thrill of a game… 2020 feels different, doesn’t it? Watching sports from my couch is comfortable, a pure escape. But it hit me – I’m seeing the *highlight reel*. The carefully curated moments. I used to play soccer competitively, and that was about grueling practices, aching muscles, and the quiet determination between plays. There’s a whole world of effort invisible to the viewer. It makes me appreciate both, actually.

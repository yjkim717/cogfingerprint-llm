## The Quiet of the Empty Stadiums

Remember 2020? It felt surreal, didn’t it? Even sports, usually a reliable constant, ground to a halt. I missed the roar of the crowd, the nervous energy before a big game. My usual Sunday ritual – cheering on my team with friends – was replaced by…silence. It was a stark reminder of how much we all crave connection, and how much joy a simple game can bring. It was strange, lonely, and a little heartbreaking.

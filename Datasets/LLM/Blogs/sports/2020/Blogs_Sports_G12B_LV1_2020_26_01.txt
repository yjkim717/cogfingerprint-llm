## The Empty Bleachers and the Unexpected Pause

Remember 2020? The year everything hit pause? Even sports, our reliable escape, felt… different. I vividly recall the surreal silence of the local high school games. Usually a roaring crowd, it was just the echoing swish of the ball and the announcer’s voice. It felt like a glitch in the matrix, a stark reminder of the wider world’s anxieties surrounding COVID. Like those sudden Starbucks closures everyone was talking about – supply chain snags and uncertainty everywhere. Even the thrill of victory felt muted, overshadowed by the strange, quiet reality.

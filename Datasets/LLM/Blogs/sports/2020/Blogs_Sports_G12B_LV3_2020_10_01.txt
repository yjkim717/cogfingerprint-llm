## The Empty Stands and the Unexpected Lesson

Remember 2020? The world felt… quiet. Even the roar of the stadium was gone. As a lifelong sports fan, the empty stands hit me harder than I expected. It wasn't just about missing the game; it was the sudden absence of that shared energy, that collective joy. It mirrored the isolation so many of us were feeling. It taught me that connection, in any form – cheering on a team or simply being with loved ones – is vital for our well-being.

"Perfecting Your Swing: To Coach or Not to Coach? In 2020, I found myself stuck in a rut on the golf course. Was taking lessons from a coach the answer, or could I rely on video analysis to improve my game? As I pondered this dilemma, I realized it's a question many golfers face. While a coach offers personalized feedback, video recording provides a detailed, frame-by-frame breakdown of my swing. For me, it ultimately came down to patience and dedication. By combining both approaches, I was able to iron out my flaws and hit the driving range with confidence."

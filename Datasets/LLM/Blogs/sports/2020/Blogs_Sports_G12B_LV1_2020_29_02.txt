## The Quiet Hum of Disappointment

Remember that feeling in March 2020? The world stopped, and so did my usual weekend routine – track days. My Thrustmaster T300RS GT became my substitute, a digital escape. Then, the servo motor died. A frustrating silence replaced the satisfying hum of the wheel. It wasn’t just a broken component; it felt like a little piece of that lost momentum, gone. Now, hunting for a replacement, hoping to recapture that feeling.

## The September Heisman Hype & the Players We Forget

Remember that weird feeling in September 2020? College football was back, but… different. The pandemic loomed, stadiums were half-empty, and the Heisman chatter was already swirling. It always is, isn't it? 

But this year, it felt particularly jarring. We’d crown a "September Heisman" before anyone’d really played a full season. It got me thinking: who gets lost in the early hype? The gritty linemen, the reliable defensive backs – the guys doing the quiet work that makes stars *look* good. 

It’s easy to get swept up in the highlight reels, but sometimes, appreciating the unsung heroes is the most satisfying part of the game. Who were *your* forgotten contenders this year?

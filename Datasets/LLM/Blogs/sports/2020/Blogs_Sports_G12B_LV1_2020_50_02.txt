## The Order of Things (and Why It Matters, Even in a Wild Year)

2020 has been… a lot, hasn't it? Between everything else, sports felt strangely adrift. We were watching games without crowds, celebrating victories through screens, and honestly, just trying to find some semblance of normalcy. It got me thinking about how we organize things, how we crave a little control amidst chaos.

It's funny, I was wrestling the other day with my YouTube Music library – trying to get my artists sorted alphabetically, like the app does. Seems small, but it felt *important*. In a year where schedules were upended and everything felt unpredictable, a little order brought a surprising sense of calm.  Just like wanting to see our favorite teams compete in a familiar rhythm, sometimes it’s the small organizational details that ground us. It’s a reminder that even when the world shifts, some things can still be put in order.

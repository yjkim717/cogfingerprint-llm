## The Unexpected Connection

Remember that feeling in 2020 when everything felt…disconnected? I had a similar moment watching a local basketball game. Seeing a player’s familiar stance, a move I'd practiced countless times as a kid, felt surreal. It was a tiny echo of my past, a reminder that even amidst lockdowns and uncertainty, those foundational passions remained, quietly shaping who I was. It was a strangely comforting realization.

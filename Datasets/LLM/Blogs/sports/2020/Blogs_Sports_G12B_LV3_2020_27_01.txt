## The Quiet Joy of the Comeback

Remember 2020? A strange year for sports, to say the least. But watching the Lakers claw their way back to the championship, after all the uncertainty, felt… different. It wasn’t just about the win; it was about witnessing resilience. Seeing LeBron and company overcome adversity, battling through injuries and a disrupted season, reminded me that sometimes, the most rewarding victories are the ones earned through sheer grit and determination. It’s a lesson that resonated far beyond the court.

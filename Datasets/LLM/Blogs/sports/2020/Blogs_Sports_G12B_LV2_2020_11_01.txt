## The Ghosts of Saturday Mornings

It's funny the things you hold onto, isn't it? Lately, I've been sorting through some old boxes, and stumbled across a stack of VHS tapes. Mostly home movies, a few birthday parties… and a surprising number of recordings from *Saturday morning cartoons* back in the 90s. 

Remember those? Before streaming, before everything was on demand, you *had* to be there. It felt like a ritual. Now, watching them feels like peering into a past self, a simpler time. I'm thinking about finding a way to share these little time capsules – maybe just with a few friends who remember the magic too. It feels right to let those Saturday morning ghosts live on. 2020's been a strange year, and a little nostalgia feels pretty good.

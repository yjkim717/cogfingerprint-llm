**The Empty Stadium**

That 2020 season was the strangest of my career. We played for a championship in a silent, empty arena. No roaring crowd, just the echo of squeaking sneakers and our own labored breaths. It taught me a profound lesson: you don't play for the noise. You play for the person next to you, and for the love of the game itself.



## Even the GOATs Have Off Nights

It's funny how quickly things change in sports, isn't it? Just saw a headline pop up – Kawhi and LeBron, both scoreless in the fourth quarter! Makes you realize even the absolute greats have those nights. We all do, really. It’s a good reminder that perfection is a myth, and sometimes, even *they* just have an off period. Keeps things real, you know? It’s what makes the comeback that much sweeter.

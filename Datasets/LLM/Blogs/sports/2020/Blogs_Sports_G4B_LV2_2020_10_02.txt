**The Year We Stopped Running**

2020… honestly, just thinking about it feels surreal. I was *loving* my running – a solid 5k five times a week. Then, lockdown hit. Suddenly, the track was closed, and my carefully planned routine vanished. It wasn’t just about missing the exercise; it was the loss of that little daily ritual, that feeling of accomplishment. 

It made me realize how much we anchor ourselves to routines, and how quickly they can be snatched away.  It was a tough year, but also a strange reminder to find joy in the small things – a walk around the block, a good book – and appreciate the simple act of moving.

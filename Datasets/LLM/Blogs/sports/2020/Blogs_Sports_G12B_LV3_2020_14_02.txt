## The Unexpected Nostalgia of a Loading Bar

Remember waiting for dial-up? That agonizingly slow progress bar felt like a lifetime. Seeing that Reddit post about ProgressBar 95 getting a "browser evolution" game mode sparked a strange wave of nostalgia. It reminded me of watching sports back then, too – delayed broadcasts, buffering streams. We’ve come so far, but sometimes I miss that shared patience, that communal anticipation before the action finally unfolded. 2020 felt like a long loading screen, didn't it?

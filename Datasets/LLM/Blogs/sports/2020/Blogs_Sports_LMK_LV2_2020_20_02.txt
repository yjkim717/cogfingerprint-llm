"Leveling Up Sports Quizzes: A Call to Action for Sporcle. As I navigated Sporcle's minefield quizzes in 2020, I realized that their badge system, while engaging, has a flaw. Currently, badges are awarded based on total plays, making them easily attainable. I propose that Sporcle gamify their platform further by counting unique plays, adding a new layer of challenge and excitement to their quizzes. Let's take sports quizzes to the next level."

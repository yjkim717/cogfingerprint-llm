As I watched the 2020 NBA restart, I was struck by the players' adaptability. Just months prior, the season was on the brink of collapse due to COVID-19. Yet, they adjusted to a "bubble" environment and delivered thrilling performances. It got me thinking - how often do we resist change in our own lives? Embracing uncertainty, like the players did, can lead to unexpected growth and success. What "bubble" are you navigating, and how can you thrive within it?

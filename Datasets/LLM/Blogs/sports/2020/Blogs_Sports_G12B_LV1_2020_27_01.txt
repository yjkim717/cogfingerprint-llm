## The Unsung Heroes of the Bench

Remember that feeling in 2020, watching the Lakers claw their way through the bubble? Everyone focused on LeBron, AD, but I kept thinking about those role players. Guys like Kentavious Caldwell-Pope, Alex Caruso – consistently reliable, grinding out crucial minutes. 

It felt like they deserved some kind of bonus, a little extra recognition for their steady presence. Like Crusader Kings, where twins get a buff, shouldn't teams acknowledge the quiet, essential contributions of the bench? They aren't the headline grabbers, but they’re the backbone of a championship run.

**The Unexpected Timeout**

Honestly, 2020 felt like one long, drawn-out timeout for a lot of us. I was a pretty dedicated runner – always chasing that next personal best, pushing through the early morning miles. But around March, everything just...stopped. Races were canceled, gyms closed, and my usual routine crumbled. 

It was jarring, to say the least. I started feeling this weird frustration, like I was constantly *trying* to do things, but not really achieving anything. Like a runner stuck on a loop. 

It took a while to realize I needed a different kind of pace, a different kind of goal. It wasn’t about the stopwatch anymore. It’s funny how a global pause can force you to re-evaluate what truly matters – and sometimes, the biggest win is simply accepting the timeout.

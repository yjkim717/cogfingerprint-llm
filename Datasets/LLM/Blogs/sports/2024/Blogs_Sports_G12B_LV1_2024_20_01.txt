## The Magic of Never Saying Never

It's wild, isn't it? Watching playoff hockey, you're constantly reminded that anything can happen. This year's ALCS had me on the edge of my seat, thinking back to those incredible comebacks – teams clawing their way back from a seemingly insurmountable 3-0 hole. It proves that in sports, and maybe in life, giving up just isn't an option. That resilience? That's what makes these championship chases so captivating. You truly never know what’s around the corner.

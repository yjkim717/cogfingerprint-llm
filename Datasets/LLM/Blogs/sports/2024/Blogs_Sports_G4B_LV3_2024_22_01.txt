**The Silent Screams on the Sidelines**

You know those moments in youth sports – the frustrated tears, the slammed helmets, the sheer, overwhelming *noise*? Last season, my son, a fiercely competitive little soccer player, started reacting violently to the other kids’ cries during games. It wasn't just a little discomfort; it was full-blown aggression, fueled by his sensitivity to loud sounds – something we were just beginning to understand as ASD. 

It felt like a silent scream echoing across the field.  We learned coping strategies – deep breaths, quiet zones – but it was a tough reminder that sometimes, connection looks different, and managing those sensory triggers is a constant, beautiful challenge.

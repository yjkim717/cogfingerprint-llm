That final buzzer hit differently tonight. After twenty years, my old knees have finally called time. No more pre-game rituals or that electric roar of the crowd just for me.

The shared struggle, the unspoken trust. The game gives you everything, until it’s time to give it back. Here’s to the next chapter.

## The Tough Calls We Face as Fans

It’s hitting different this year, isn't it? Seeing teams grapple with tough decisions. I was reading something the other day suggesting the Heat might consider moving on from Jimmy Butler. Wild, right? Part of me screams "no way!" But another part understands the logic – age, injuries… it’s just brutal to watch a player decline. It makes you wonder, how much loyalty do we owe when the wins start slipping?

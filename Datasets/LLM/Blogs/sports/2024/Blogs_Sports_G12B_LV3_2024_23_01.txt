## The Linemen We Need

It's 2024, and Iowa State football feels… pivotal. I saw a thread on Reddit that really hit home: our success hinges on the offensive line. Seriously! We’ve got the talent, but a history of O-line struggles keeps holding us back. Seven wins? Big 12 Championship? Playoff run? It all comes down to those guys up front. Let's hope they're ready to rise to the challenge.

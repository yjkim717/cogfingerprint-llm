## The Rim Protector's Dilemma: Can You Really Do Both?

Coaching in 2024 feels like a constant chess match, doesn’t it? Lately, I’ve been wrestling with a particular challenge: how to defend this increasingly popular 5-out offense while sticking with our man-to-man scheme. It’s a real tightrope walk. 

We’ve always prioritized rim protection – our big man is the anchor, the last line of defense. But with five players constantly spacing the floor, pulling him away to chase shooters leaves our post vulnerable. It’s a classic dilemma: do you sacrifice perimeter coverage to keep your big man close, or risk leaving paint protection behind? There's gotta be a balance, a smart strategy that lets us protect the rim *and* contain those shooters. Any thoughts out there? Let's talk hoops!

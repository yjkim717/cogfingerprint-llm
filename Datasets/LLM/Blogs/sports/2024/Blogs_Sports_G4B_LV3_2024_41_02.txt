**The Endless Scroll & the Echo Chamber**

You know that feeling when you’re scrolling through Twitter – or, let’s be honest, X now – and you see *another* link to the same viral video? It’s…familiar.  It’s like a digital echo, bouncing back and forth. Lately, I’ve been noticing a weird glitch on Reddit too – links to X aren’t automatically flagged when similar ones have been posted before. 

It’s a tiny detail, sure, but it highlights something bigger.  We crave connection, and social media promises it. Yet, are we really *connecting*, or just endlessly reposting the same thoughts and images?  It feels like a massive waste of everyone’s time, and honestly, a drain on moderators trying to keep things moving.  Maybe a little digital mindfulness is needed – a conscious choice to seek out genuinely *new* voices and perspectives.

**Remembering the Mud**

We’d always complain about the conditions, but honestly, those muddy games were the best. You *knew* every tackle, every scramble, every moment mattered. It wasn’t about winning; it was about the grit, the teamwork, and that shared, messy experience.  What’s a memory that brings you right back?

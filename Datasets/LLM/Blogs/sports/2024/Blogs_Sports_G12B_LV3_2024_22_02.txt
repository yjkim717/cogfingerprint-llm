## That Feeling When You Just *Are* the Game

Remember that feeling during a game – not the pressure of winning, but when everything else melts away? The world narrows to the ball, your teammates, the play unfolding. It’s almost… blissful. As a manager in 2024, I’ve been chasing that same sense of complete immersion, that "flow state" we hear so much about. It’s a reminder that even amidst challenges, finding that zone – wherever it exists – is worth the pursuit.

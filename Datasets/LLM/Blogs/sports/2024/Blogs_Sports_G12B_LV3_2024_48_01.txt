## The Ghosts in the Stands (and Online)

Remember that feeling after a huge win? Pure exhilaration! But lately, even celebrating feels… complicated. It’s 2024, and every cheer, every highlight, seems to be meticulously tracked. It got me thinking: just like a favorite team protects its plays, shouldn't we be safeguarding our digital footprint? Maybe it’s time to explore ways to enjoy the game – and the online world – with a little more privacy.

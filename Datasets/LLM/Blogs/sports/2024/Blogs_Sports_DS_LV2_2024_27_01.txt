They say you can’t serve two masters, but Travis Hunter never got the memo. Watching him in 2024 was like watching a magic trick in cleats. One snap, he's making a diving catch as a receiver; the next, he's locking down the opponent's top corner. To excel so completely on both sides of the ball? It’s a raw display of athletic genius that makes you wonder if we’ve been limiting our definitions of what’s possible in this sport all along.

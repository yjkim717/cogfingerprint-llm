Watching Becky Lynch’s recent three-week storyline unfold, I couldn’t help but think back to Mick Foley’s retirement arc. It makes you wonder: what truly defines a great story in sports? Sometimes, it’s not the epic, season-long sagas but the potent, compact mini-arcs that hit hardest. They’re like a perfect, brief play—intense, memorable, and they leave you wanting more, a refreshing change of pace in our long-term narrative landscape.

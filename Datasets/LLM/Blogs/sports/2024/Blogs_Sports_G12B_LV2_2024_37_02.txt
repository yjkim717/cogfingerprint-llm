## The Clock's Always Ticking, Isn't It?

Something's been nagging at me this recruiting season. It feels… arbitrary. Like, one year a program gets a flood of contact hours, the next it’s a trickle. Is there a secret formula? A cosmic lottery? 

We obsess over on-field performance, rightfully so. But these behind-the-scenes hours, the tireless work building relationships – it all feels so vital, and yet, the rules seem to shift. Makes you wonder what truly moves the needle in 2024, doesn't it?

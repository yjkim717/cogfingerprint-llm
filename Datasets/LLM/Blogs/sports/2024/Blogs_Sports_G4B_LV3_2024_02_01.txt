**The Price of Passion**

Remember that top-of-the-line running watch I splurged on last year? It was supposed to be my performance tracker, my motivation guru. Now, after just twelve months, it’s stubbornly frozen, and the repair quote… well, it’s basically the cost of a *new* one. It’s frustrating, to say the least. It makes you wonder if chasing peak performance sometimes means accepting a hefty price tag for equipment that’s destined to fail.  Maybe it’s a reminder that passion doesn’t always equal longevity.

## The Unexpected Comeback at the County Fair

Remember that horseshoe tournament at the 2022 county fair? I was *sure* I had it in the bag, playing a slow, steady game. Then, old Mr. Henderson, known for his surprisingly aggressive style, launched a furious counter-attack. Suddenly, he was stealing ringer after ringer! It was a brutal reminder that sometimes, the best defense is a well-timed, unexpected rush. Humbling, but definitely entertaining.

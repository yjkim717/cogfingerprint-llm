## The Unexpected Comeback That Reminded Me Why We Play

Remember the Cardinals’ season in 2022? Honestly, it felt like a slow burn of disappointment for much of the year. But then, something shifted. A scrappy resilience emerged, fueled by unexpected heroes and a genuine fight. It wasn't a championship run, certainly, but watching them claw their way back in those final weeks… it reminded me why I love sports. It’s not always about the win; it's about the grit, the perseverance, and the beauty of a team refusing to quit.

**The Unexpected Cheers**

2022 was a weird year for my local hockey team. We weren’t winning, and honestly, the mood was…flat. Then, at a game against a rival, something shifted. A young kid, maybe eight years old, started shouting encouragement – not just for *our* team, but for the opposing goalie. It was a genuinely heartfelt, messy cheer. It reminded me that sports aren’t always about victory. Sometimes, it’s about the feeling of connection, of rooting for someone, anywhere.  It was a small thing, but it totally lifted the energy.

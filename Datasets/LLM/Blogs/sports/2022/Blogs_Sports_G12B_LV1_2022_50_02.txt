## 2022: A Year of Small Victories

Scrolling through Reddit the other day, I stumbled upon a thread buzzing with runners celebrating their 2022 PRs. It got me thinking about my own running journey this year. No marathon finishes for me, but I shaved a surprising amount of time off my 5k! It wasn't about breaking records, but consistently pushing myself, feeling stronger, and truly enjoying the miles. Those little accomplishments? They felt pretty monumental. Here's to chasing those personal wins, big or small!

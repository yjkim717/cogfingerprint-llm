As the 2022 NBA offseason heated up, rumors swirled around potential blockbusters, including a possible trade for Kevin Durant. While having a superstar like KD on your roster sounds enticing, I couldn't help but think it's not a guarantee of success. Teams willing to trade assets like Rudy Gobert for Durant are putting all their eggs in one basket. The risk is that Durant's injury history could derail a team's championship aspirations. Is the high cost worth the uncertain reward? For contenders, the answer isn't as clear-cut as it seems.

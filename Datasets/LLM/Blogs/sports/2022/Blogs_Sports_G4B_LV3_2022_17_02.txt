**The Long Game of Patience**

2022 felt like a year of frustrating plateaus in my squash. I was slowly, painstakingly improving my game, but constantly battling a wave of negativity after matches – little jabs disguised as ‘helpful’ advice. It’s incredibly disheartening when you’re putting in the work and someone just seems intent on stirring up frustration. Honestly, it’s a type of ‘trolling’ that can really derail your progress.  You learn that sometimes, the best win is simply continuing to play, to learn, and to ignore the noise.

"College Football's Voter Vagaries: Unpacking the AP Poll's Inconsistencies"

As I dove into the 2022 AP Poll data, I was struck by the inconsistencies in voter rankings. College football fans know how passionately invested voters are in their favorite teams, but does that passion translate to consistent voting patterns? I analyzed the poll ballots to find out. The results were fascinating - and sometimes frustrating. Some voters showed remarkable loyalty to their top teams, while others seemed to change their tune from week to week. The data revealed intriguing insights into the minds of AP Poll voters. By examining these inconsistencies, we can gain a deeper understanding of what drives voter decisions and how that impacts the rankings. Let's take a closer look at the numbers and explore what they reveal about the ever-unpredictable world of college football.

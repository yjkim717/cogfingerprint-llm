"Recovering from a Foul Play: Lessons from a Lost iMovie Project"

As I frantically tried to free up space on my MacBook before the 2022 sports season highlight reel deadline, I accidentally deleted my iMovie project files containing last year's game footage. The panic of data loss was reminiscent of a crucial fumble on the field. Thankfully, I recovered most of the files. This experience taught me to be more mindful of my digital storage, just as athletes are cautious of their moves on the field. Now, I'm back to editing, wiser and more focused.

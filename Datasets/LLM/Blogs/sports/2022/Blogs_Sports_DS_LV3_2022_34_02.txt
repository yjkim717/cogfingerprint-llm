There’s something electric about the calm before the storm—the quiet anticipation of a new college football season. When ESPN dropped their 2022 way-too-early All-America team, it wasn’t just a list of names; it was a promise. A promise of breakout stars and unforgettable Saturdays. I remember scrolling through, imagining these players stepping onto the field, carrying the hopes of their teams and fans. It’s more than stats or rankings—it’s about the stories waiting to unfold, the grit, the glory. That early glimpse made the wait for kickoff feel both endless and thrilling. Here’s to the players ready to leave it all on the field.

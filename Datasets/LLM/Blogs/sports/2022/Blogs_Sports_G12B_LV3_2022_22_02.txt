## The Unexpected Lesson from My Kid's Eating Habits

Remember that feeling of pushing yourself to the absolute limit in a game? My toddler’s mealtime reminded me of it. We’re battling the over-stuffing issue – even with small pieces! It’s frustrating, watching him cram so much in. It got me thinking, are we sometimes like that, piling our plates (and schedules) too high? Maybe slowing down, savoring each bite, applies to life as much as it does to dinner. 2022 has been a year of learning that less can truly be more.

## It's Not Always About the Score, Is It?

Anyone who's been to a college football game knows the atmosphere is electric. But lately, I've been thinking about *why* that energy gets so intensely negative sometimes. A recent Reddit thread got me laughing – turns out, some of the biggest rivalries stem from the most ridiculous things! Like, a player wearing the "wrong" color socks, or a team’s mascot being slightly…off. It's a reminder that fandom is a weird, wonderful mix of passion and petty grievances. We invest so much, and sometimes, it manifests in the silliest ways.

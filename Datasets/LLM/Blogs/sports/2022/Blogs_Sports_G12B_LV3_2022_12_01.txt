## The Unexpected Comfort of Routine (Even in a Chaotic Game)

Remember that feeling of frustration when you’re dropped into a Warzone match and scrambling for gear? It hit me hard last night. I was hunting for a specific Automaton blueprint – purple, 75-round mag, the works! It felt like a quest. It made me realize, even in a chaotic game like that, the thrill of finding that perfect loadout, that defined attachment, is strangely comforting. It's a little routine in the middle of the mayhem, and sometimes, that's exactly what you need.

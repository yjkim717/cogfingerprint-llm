As the 2022 NBA playoffs heated up, one player's limitations were under the microscope: Ben Simmons. His struggles with shooting have long been a topic of debate. While Simmons excels in many areas, his inability to knock down shots from beyond the arc severely hinders his team's chances. As fans, we can't help but wonder - can he adapt and evolve to become a more well-rounded player? Simmons' situation serves as a reminder that even top athletes have areas for improvement, and the playoffs are where those weaknesses are put to the ultimate test.

**October’s Rush**

Remember that feeling when you finally nail a shot, a play, anything in sports? For me, 2022 was all about trying to recapture that feeling on the tennis court. I’d signed up for a fall league, picturing quiet evenings and steady improvement. Instead, it was chaos! The courts were packed, especially in October, with families and a surprising number of competitive players. It just reminded me that sometimes, the best moments aren’t about solitary practice – they’re about sharing the energy and the challenge with others.

## The Weight of Expectations & the Joy of Rising Above

It's funny how baseball can feel like history repeating itself. Watching Jeremy Peña’s 2022 rookie season felt reminiscent of Troy Tulowitzki’s electric 2007. Both faced enormous pressure, yet delivered – Peña with those 22 homers and stellar defense, Tulo with his undeniable talent. It’s a beautiful reminder that even under the brightest lights, young players can truly shine.

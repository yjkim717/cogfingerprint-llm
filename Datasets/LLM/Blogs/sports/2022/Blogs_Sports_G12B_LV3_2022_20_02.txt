## That Heartbreak Feeling: Remembering the 2022 World Cup Final

Okay, let's be honest, who *didn't* feel a gut punch watching the 2022 World Cup final? Argentina vs. France… what a game! Even as a casual fan, the rollercoaster of emotions was intense. Messi finally getting his moment, then Mbappe's incredible comeback… It was a sporting spectacle that transcended the sport itself. That final truly highlighted why we love (and sometimes suffer through) the beautiful game. Still processing, honestly!

**The Weight of a Perfect Throw**

It’s funny how something as simple as a baseball can hold so much weight, right? This summer, I spent a *lot* of time at the park with my nephew, Leo, trying to teach him to throw. He’d grip the ball, wind up, and… well, it usually ended up a good foot to the left. 

We spent weeks on it, really. I’d meticulously analyze his stance, the angle of his arm, everything. But honestly, there were moments I felt like I was imposing *my* idea of a perfect throw, instead of letting him find his own. 

It made me think about my own sports memories – the pressure I put on myself to perform, to win.  2022 was a year of pushing myself, of striving for ‘better,’ but sometimes the joy was lost in the pursuit. Leo’s earnest effort, his willingness to just *try*, was a reminder that the real victory isn’t always about the outcome. 

It’s about the process, the grit, and the feeling of a good throw – even if it’s a little off-target.

As the 2022 NBA playoffs unfolded, one player continued to be under the microscope - Ben Simmons. His struggles with shooting, particularly from beyond the arc and at the free-throw line, raised eyebrows. Can a player be so impactful on the court yet struggle with such a fundamental aspect of the game? Simmons' athleticism and defensive prowess are undeniable, but his inability to knock down a jumpshot or sink a free throw has called into question his true value. As the league continues to evolve, will Simmons adapt and find a way to overcome his shooting woes, or will it remain his achilles heel?

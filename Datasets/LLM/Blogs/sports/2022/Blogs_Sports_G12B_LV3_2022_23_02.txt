## The Changing Game: Is College Sports Losing Its Soul?

Remember the days when college athletes played for the love of the game, for their school, and the thrill of competition? Lately, watching college sports feels… different. The rise of NIL deals, while offering athletes a deserved opportunity, has also widened the chasm between powerhouse programs and smaller schools. It’s tough to ignore the growing sense that the amateurism we cherished is fading, and the financial realities are reshaping the landscape in ways that feel unsettling. Are we witnessing the end of an era?

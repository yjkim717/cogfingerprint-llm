## The Faceoff Fizzle: When Best-Laid Plans Go Sideways

Anyone who's watched an NHL game in 2022 knows faceoffs are a *thing*. But I was pondering something recently – those moments where a center clearly intends to win the draw and send the puck left, yet *bam*, the other team snatches it. It’s frustrating, right? It highlights how much can change in a split second, and how even the most calculated plays can unravel. Just a reminder that even the pros don't have it all figured out!

The whistle blew, and my season was over—another injury. In 2022, sidelined with a torn ligament, I felt my identity slipping away. No more dawn practices or game-day adrenaline. But forced stillness taught me resilience isn’t just pushing through pain; it’s embracing rest, too. Healing became my new sport. Now, back on the field, I carry that lesson: sometimes, the strongest move is to pause and listen to what your body truly needs.

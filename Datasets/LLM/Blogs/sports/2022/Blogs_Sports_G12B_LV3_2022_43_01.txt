## The Silent Stadium and the Echo of What Might Have Been

Remember that gut-punch feeling after the World Series in 2022? The one where you just… sat there? I did. The silence of the stadium, even watching from my living room, was deafening. It wasn't just about losing; it was the weight of all the hope, all the build-up, suddenly evaporating. It’s a reminder that even in the most thrilling games, sometimes the narrative just doesn't go your way. And that's okay, right? It’s part of the beautiful, brutal game.

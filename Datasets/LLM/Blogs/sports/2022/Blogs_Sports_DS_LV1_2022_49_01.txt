**The Voice of the Fight**

Back in 2022, my two passions—mixed martial arts and my voice—finally collided. An MMA YouTube channel with 60,000 subscribers took a chance on me. Suddenly, I was the narrator for weekly combat sports breakdowns, my words giving life to flying knees and slick submissions for $100 per three videos.

## The Unexpected Pause: When Your Body Says "Not Today"

This year has been… a lot, hasn't it? We're all still finding our footing after the pandemic, trying to rebuild routines and reclaim some sense of normalcy. For me, that’s meant finally getting serious about running again. I was feeling *good*, seeing progress, actually enjoying the early morning miles.

Then, bam. A nasty cold hit me last week. Just knocked me flat. And it got me thinking – beyond the misery of feeling awful, how much does this kind of disruption *really* impact things? I mean, I'm barely eating, creating a pretty serious calorie deficit. Is my body going into some kind of preservation mode? Will I be fighting an uphill battle when I finally feel well enough to get back out there?

It’s a good reminder, I guess, that even with the best laid plans, our bodies are still in charge. Sometimes, the best training is just *rest*. Hoping to be back on my feet soon!

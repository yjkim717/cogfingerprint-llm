"Losing Stings: My Chess.com Conundrum"

As I clicked "resign" on chess.com in early 2022, a familiar frustration resurfaced. At my level, online chess can be a rollercoaster. Wins are fleeting, and losses are laced with trolls' jabs. I wondered, is it about the game or the validation we crave? I reflected on my journey, realizing that improvement is a personal quest, not a competition. Perhaps it's time to mute the noise and focus on my next move.

## The Unexpected Joy of Watching My Kid Learn to Fall

It's 2021, and my little one's just started soccer. Honestly, I expected to be all about the goals and the wins. But what's truly stuck with me? Watching them learn to fall. That wobble, the scramble, the eventual, slightly tearful, rise. It's a powerful lesson in resilience, isn't it? More valuable than any trophy, I reckon.

## The Quiet of the Empty Bleachers

Remember the roar of the stadium in 2021? Feels like a lifetime ago, doesn't it? My son, barely walking, used to love being hoisted up to see the baseball game. Now, he’s chasing his own ball in the backyard. It’s a bittersweet shift. The energy of those packed stands is missed, but watching him create his own little world… that’s a different kind of winning.

## Beyond the Baseline: Finding Leadership in Unexpected Places

It's 2021, and we're seeing a lot of upheaval. But lately, I've been thinking about Steve Simon, the WTA chief. An LA Times piece really struck me – his unwavering resolve in advocating for what’s right, even when it’s difficult. It’s a powerful reminder that true leadership isn’t always about wins and losses on the court, but about standing up for values. It's inspiring to see that kind of courage in sports.

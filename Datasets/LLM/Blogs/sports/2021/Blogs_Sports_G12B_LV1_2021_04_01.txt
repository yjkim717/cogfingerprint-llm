## The Unexpected Battle for the Play-In

It’s April 2021, and the NBA’s Eastern Conference is shaping up to be wild. Honestly, who saw the Toronto Raptors, a team once championship hopefuls, battling Charlotte and Washington for a *play-in* spot? It's a fascinating shift. Pascal Siakam’s been carrying a heavy load, and you can practically feel the desperation in every game. The thought of Toronto losing home-court advantage? Unthinkable just a few years ago. This late-season scramble is a great reminder that parity can make even the most predictable seasons utterly captivating.

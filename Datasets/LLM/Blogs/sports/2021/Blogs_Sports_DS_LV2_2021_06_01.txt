It’s 2021, and I can’t help but feel a pang of sadness watching the Pelicans. Remember the electric hope when Zion Williamson arrived? We dreamed of a new era, a franchise reborn. But here we are, stuck in a cycle of injuries, questionable front-office moves, and squandered draft picks. It’s like watching a sports car with a full tank of gas and a flat tire—so much potential, going nowhere. As fans, we invest our hearts in these teams, and seeing such talent consistently underdeliver is just tough. What could have been, right?

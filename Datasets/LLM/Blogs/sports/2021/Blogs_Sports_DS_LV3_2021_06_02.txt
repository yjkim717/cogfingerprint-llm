There’s something special about watching greatness unfold, even when we’ve seen it before. In 2021, Giannis faced the familiar whispers—voter fatigue, playoff doubts. But then you watch him play. The raw stats are one thing, but it’s the relentless defense, the way he anchors those Bucks, that truly tells the story. It’s a reminder that consistent, hard-nosed excellence, leading your team to third in a tough East, deserves its flowers. Sometimes, the MVP case isn’t just in the numbers; it’s in the undeniable will to elevate everyone around you.

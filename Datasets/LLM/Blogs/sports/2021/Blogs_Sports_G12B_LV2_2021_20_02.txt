## The Ghost of Empty Stadiums

Remember 2021? It felt…off, didn’t it? Even with the games back, the roar was missing. Watching the Olympics, seeing those incredible athletes perform in near-silent stadiums, it hit me hard. Sports are about the collective gasp, the shared joy, the energy of the crowd. It was a stark reminder of what we’d lost, and how much those connections truly matter. A strange, quiet year for a sport I love.

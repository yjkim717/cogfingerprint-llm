"Oklahoma State's Recruiting Riddle: A Coach, Top-Notch Facilities, and Still a Struggle"

As a die-hard Oklahoma State Cowboys fan, I've been scratching my head over the team's recruiting woes. In 2021, it's baffling to see a program with Mike Gundy's steady leadership and state-of-the-art facilities still struggling to land top talent. The Cowboys have invested heavily in their recruiting infrastructure, with top-notch amenities that should be a major draw for prospective players. Yet, year after year, they seem to be just missing the mark. Gundy's consistency is a major plus, but it's clear that more needs to be done to attract the best recruits. As the Cowboys head into the next recruiting cycle, it's time to rethink their strategy and figure out what they're missing. The question is, what's holding them back?

## The Voices That Make the Game

Remember when sports felt… more? Lately, scrolling through Reddit threads has me thinking about the personalities *around* the game – the announcers, the podcasters, the journalists who add layers beyond the score. It's funny how much a great commentator or a thoughtful analysis can elevate an experience. Back in the 2000s, Bill Simmons basically pioneered this space, and it’s fascinating to see how his influence echoes even now. These voices, they become part of the memory of a game, don't they?

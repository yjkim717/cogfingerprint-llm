## The Season That Taught Me About Letting Go

Remember 2021? It felt like the world was collectively holding its breath. For me, it was also the year my son, Leo, was *convinced* he was going to be the star shortstop for his little league team. He practiced relentlessly, dreaming of game-winning hits. 

But… it just wasn’t happening. He struggled, and the frustration was etched on his face. As a parent, it was tough to watch. I kept wanting to step in, offer tips, *fix* it. But his coach wisely encouraged me to let him experience it.

And you know what? He did. He learned resilience. He learned that sometimes, despite your best efforts, things don't pan out the way you hoped.  We didn't win the championship that year, but Leo gained something far more valuable than a trophy. It was a lesson in grace, in accepting outcomes, and appreciating the joy of playing, regardless. It's a lesson I'm trying to remember, off the field too.

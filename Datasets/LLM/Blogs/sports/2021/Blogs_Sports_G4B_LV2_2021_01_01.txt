**The Unfinished Game**

Anyone else feel like sometimes you’re just… stuck? I was thinking about my little brother’s baseball season last year – 2021. He was finally getting some real playing time, really starting to find his groove. But then, just as things were clicking, a shoulder injury sidelined him. 

It felt like an unfinished game, a frustrating pause. You invest so much energy, build up that momentum, and then… nothing. It’s a weird feeling, that sense of potential just out of reach.  It reminded me that sometimes, even in sports – and life – you have to accept that the game isn’t always going to go your way.  What about you?  Have you ever felt that way?

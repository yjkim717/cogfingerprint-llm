## The Unexpected Lesson from a Lost Game

Remember that gut-punch feeling watching the Braves lose the World Series in 2021? Brutal. But honestly, amidst the disappointment, something stuck with me. It wasn't about the win or loss, but the unwavering support, the shared heartbreak, the *togetherness* of it all. It reminded me that life, like a baseball season, is full of ups and downs, and navigating them with someone by your side is what truly matters.

"Scoring More Than Just Points: My Journey to Embracing the True Spirit of Sports"

As I stepped onto the field in 2021, I realized it wasn't just about winning or losing - it was about the journey. Like a worn leather jacket that needs adjustment, I had to fine-tune my mindset to truly enjoy the game. The thrill of competition still drove me, but now I appreciated the camaraderie and perseverance that came with it. I learned that sometimes, you need to adjust your approach to score more than just points - you need to score memories, friendships, and personal growth.

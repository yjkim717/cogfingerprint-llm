## That Gut Feeling About the Playoffs

Remember that feeling in 2021? The Chiefs looked shaky, the Packers had a weird slide… it was playoff season, and something just *felt* off. We’d all been so sure about certain teams, built up expectations. But sports, right? They have a way of reminding you that even the most dominant streaks can crumble. It’s a humbling, thrilling ride, isn’t it?

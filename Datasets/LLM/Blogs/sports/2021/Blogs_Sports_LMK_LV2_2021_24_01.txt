"Stepping Off the Court: A Lesson in Letting Go"

In 2021, I had to confront a tough reality - my competitive basketball days were behind me. It was a hard pill to swallow, just like when I struggled to remove my previous employer's OneDrive from my device after they shut down my account. I felt stuck, like a part of me was still tied to something that no longer existed. But, just as I finally found a way to delete that lingering cloud storage, I learned to let go of my athletic identity and discover new passions beyond the court.

## The Unexpected Joy of Picking Up a Paddle (Again)

Remember that feeling of being effortlessly good at something as a kid? I’d almost forgotten it. Then, last summer (2021), I dusted off my old paddleball set. Turns out, rediscovering a simple sport like that – no pressure, just fun – has been surprisingly restorative. It’s a little pocket of joy in a busy life, a reminder that learning something new, even something seemingly small, can be incredibly rewarding.

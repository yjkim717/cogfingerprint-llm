

## The Play-In Push: More Than Just a Game

It's April 2021, and the NBA's play-in tournament feels *different* this year. Watching Charlotte, Washington, and Toronto battle for that last spot? It's not just about wins and losses. It's about the heart, the fight, the sheer will to prove yourself. Reminds me of those underdog moments in life, doesn't it? When you're scrapping for every inch, every opportunity. Rooting for these teams feels a little more personal than usual.

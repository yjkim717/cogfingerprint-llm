**The Obsession with Numbers**

It’s weird, isn’t it? 2021 and I was *obsessed* with my Garmin Venu 2. Tracking every stride, every heart rate zone.  I’d spend hours tweaking watch faces and obsessing over those training metrics.  Then I read about potential updates for existing Venu 2 owners – a little unsettling, realizing how much I relied on that specific version. It made me think about the future of fitness tracking... and maybe, just maybe, letting go a bit.

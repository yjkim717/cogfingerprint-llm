**The Silent Watch**

You know that feeling when you’re really locked in during a game – completely absorbed? I remember last year, streaming some Valorant, and suddenly I noticed a flurry of chat messages.  It was unsettling. Twitch’s rules are super clear about underage accounts, and I felt this immediate responsibility.  It’s a weird thing, being a public face, constantly scanning for potential issues.  It’s a reminder that even in the digital world, we have a duty to protect those younger viewers, and to keep the energy positive.

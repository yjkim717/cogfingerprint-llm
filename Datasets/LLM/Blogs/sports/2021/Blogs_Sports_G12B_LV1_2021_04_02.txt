## The Roar We're Missing

It’s wild, isn't it? Watching the Lakers battle in the playoffs this year and knowing…it’s just not the same. Scrolling through Reddit the other day, someone asked about full capacity at Staples – a simple question that hit me hard. Seeing other teams packed, the energy palpable, really highlights what we’re missing. The roar of a full crowd? That's part of the game, and 2021 feels incomplete without it.

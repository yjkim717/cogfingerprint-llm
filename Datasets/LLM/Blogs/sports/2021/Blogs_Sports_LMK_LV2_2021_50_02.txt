As I watched the Tokyo Olympics in 2021, I was reminded that even the world's top athletes face setbacks. Simone Biles's withdrawal sparked a mix of shock and empathy. It struck me that mental toughness is just as crucial as physical strength. Just like Biles, we all face our own 'Olympic trials' in life. How we respond to adversity defines our true character, and it's a lesson that resonates far beyond the sports arena.

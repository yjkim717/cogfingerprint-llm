>## The Roar of the Crowd (Even From My Couch)

Remember that electric feeling of being *in* the stadium? 2021 felt different, a lot of games watched from home. But honestly, catching the winning goal with my family, popcorn in hand, on a massive 75-inch screen? It wasn’t so bad. The 120Hz refresh rate made every play feel real, and suddenly, my living room felt pretty close to the action. Sports, connection, and a good TV – a winning combo.

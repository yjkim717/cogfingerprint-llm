"Stepping Off the Field: How Sports Taught Me About Life's Referees" 

As I hung up my cleats in 2021, I realized sports had prepared me for more than just the next game. The wins, losses, and tough coaches had taught me about perseverance, teamwork, and adapting to life's unexpected calls. Just like in sports, life has its own referees - experiences that shape us, sometimes fairly, sometimes not. But it's how we respond that defines the game.

The Dark Side of Sports Fandom in 2021: When Online Harassment Takes Center Stage. As sports enthusiasts, we're no strangers to heated debates and rivalries. However, the rise of social media has taken this to a disturbing new level. False accusations and cyberbullying have become all too common, damaging reputations and fostering social distrust. It's time for fans, athletes, and governing bodies to take a step back and reassess our online behavior. Let's strive to maintain a positive, respectful sports culture, both online and offline, and ensure that our passion for sports doesn't become a breeding ground for harm.

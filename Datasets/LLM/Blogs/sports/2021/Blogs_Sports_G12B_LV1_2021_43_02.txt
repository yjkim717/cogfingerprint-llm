## Did Anyone Else Notice…? The Subtle Weight of a Championship

Rewatching the 2021 Super Bowl last night, something struck me. It wasn't just Brady's brilliance, but the almost *burden* he carried. That weight of expectation, the years of chasing another ring – it felt palpable. Like Bilbo Baggins slowly realizing the Ring's power, I wondered if we, as viewers, fully grasped the pressure on him and the entire Buccaneers team. It was more than just a game; it was a legacy.

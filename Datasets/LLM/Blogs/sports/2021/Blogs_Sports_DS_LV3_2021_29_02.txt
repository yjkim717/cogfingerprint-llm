That 2021 season, our team was like choosing between the 75-inch Sony and sleek 65-inch LG. We debated: go big with a power-running game or sleek with a fast-passing offense? At a 12-foot viewing distance—or in our case, a tight budget and a must-win game—the bigger screen, like our ground game, just felt more immersive. Sometimes, presence matters more than pure finesse.

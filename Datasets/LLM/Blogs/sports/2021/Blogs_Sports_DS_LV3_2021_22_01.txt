As I dug through my garage in 2021, that old black fabric sparked a memory. It wasn't destined for Halloween monsters, but for my makeshift home gym during lockdown. Those solo workouts—just me, some cheap weights, and sheer determination—taught me resilience. Sports became less about competition and more about personal endurance. Funny how a simple piece of opaque fabric can remind you that sometimes, the toughest battles are the ones you face alone, and conquer.

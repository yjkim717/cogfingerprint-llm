It’s 2021, and the MVP chatter is heating up. Sure, voter fatigue is real—we’ve seen Giannis dominate before. But let’s not overlook what he’s doing now: monster win shares, top-tier VORP, and that relentless defense. His team is winning, and in the playoffs, he elevates even further. Sometimes, greatness becomes so routine we risk taking it for granted. But true value? It doesn’t fade—it deserves its due, year after year. Giannis has earned his place in this conversation, no doubt.

## The Quiet Thrill of the Comeback

There's something uniquely captivating about a team battling back from defeat, isn't there? Watching the Dodgers claw their way through the 2021 playoffs, down multiple times, reminded me that resilience isn't about avoiding falls, but about *how* you rise. It’s not the flashy home runs that stick with you, but the quiet determination, the incremental gains, the refusal to quit. It's a beautiful, slow burn, like Chromatics covering Springsteen – intense, but with a soothing undercurrent of hope.

I remember those backyard games, counting down in my head before launching a perfect spiral for the imaginary overtime win. It’s a fantasy shared by so many of us who grew up with a football in our hands. Yet, as I looked at the stats after the 2021 playoffs, it hit me: not a single elite quarterback has ever thrown that legendary sudden-death touchdown pass when it truly matters. It’s a humbling reminder that even for the greats, the dream scenario is often just that—a dream.

**The Weight of a Stolen Smile**

Remember scrolling through Reddit last year, seeing this bizarre story about a stolen Boba Fett toy ending up in a pawn shop? It got me thinking about how easily things – and people – can get caught in the undertow of the secondhand market.  It’s unsettling, isn’t it, the idea of a manufacturer’s employee potentially involved?  It felt like a small, strange mirror reflecting a larger question about trust and responsibility in a world obsessed with ‘new’ and ‘used.’  Sometimes, a simple toy holds a surprising amount of baggage.

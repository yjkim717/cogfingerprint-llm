## The Anxiety of the Little Dot

Anyone else find themselves *obsessed* with tracking packages these days? It's 2021, and the live map has become a strange source of both comfort and anxiety. I'm waiting on a small shipment – AirTags, to be exact – and UPS is telling me they were "delivered" three days ago. Delivered where? To another dimension, apparently! It’s a tiny frustration, but a reminder of how reliant we are on these digital promises.

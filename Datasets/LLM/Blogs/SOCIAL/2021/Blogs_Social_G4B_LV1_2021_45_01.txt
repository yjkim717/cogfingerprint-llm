**The Phantom UPS Stop**

It’s weird, right? 2021 was *all* about tracking. AirTags were everywhere, obsessively monitoring our stuff. I was waiting for a package from UPS, and my little Apple chip showed the truck just… stopped. For like, twenty minutes. No update, no explanation. It felt strangely social – a shared frustration bubbling up on Reddit, everyone wondering what was happening. It highlighted how much we rely on these systems, and how unsettling it is when they fail.

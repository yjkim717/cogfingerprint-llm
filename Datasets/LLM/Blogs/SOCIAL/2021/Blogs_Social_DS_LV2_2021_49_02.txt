It’s 2021, and I still feel that familiar twinge of social anxiety in crowded grocery aisles. Watching *My 600-lb Life*, I wondered: why not just order online? But then I remembered—sometimes we crave normalcy, even when it’s hard. The pandemic taught us to hide behind screens, but human connection, however fleeting, is a need we can’t delete. Maybe those shopping trips aren’t just about food; they’re about refusing to disappear completely.

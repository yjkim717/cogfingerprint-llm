"After the Crash: Navigating the Unseen Impact of Head Injuries". In 2021, a concerning trend emerged - the often-hidden consequences of car accidents on our brains. For a 19-year-old, the aftermath can be overwhelming. Concussions and head injuries can be tricky to diagnose, and the road to recovery is often long and uncertain. As we reflect on the fragility of our health, we're reminded that some wounds aren't visible. Let's break the silence around head injuries, sharing stories and supporting those affected. By doing so, we can work together to create a more compassionate and informed community.

"As we navigated the pandemic in 2021, I noticed a trend - blockbuster films that pushed the 2.5-hour mark were captivating audiences worldwide. Christopher Nolan's masterpieces, like 'Dunkirk' and 'Interstellar', were prime examples. These lengthy epics not only enthralled viewers but also raked in over $200 million at the box office. It made me wonder, are we craving more immersive experiences in a world where our attention span is constantly being tested?"

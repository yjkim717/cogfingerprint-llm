"Embracing the Comfort Zone: My Lazy College Days"

As I navigated college life in 2021, I found myself stuck in a rut. Online learning made it easy to coast through classes, and I often felt lazy and unmotivated. I'd put off assignments until the last minute, and my procrastination became a habit. But as I looked back, I realized that my lack of motivation was a symptom of a deeper issue - a fear of pushing beyond my comfort zone. By acknowledging this, I took the first step towards breaking free from my laziness and finding a more fulfilling college experience.

## The Unexpected Housing Question

Navigating grad school is tough enough, right? This week, a fellow Marquette grad student posed a question that hit close to home: can a spouse live in on-campus housing? It sparked a thought – how do we balance pursuing our dreams with maintaining our relationships? And, surprisingly, it all boiled down to a simple question: is rent per person or per unit? It’s a small detail with a big impact on so many of us.

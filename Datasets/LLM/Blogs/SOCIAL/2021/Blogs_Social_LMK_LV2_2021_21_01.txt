As I sailed through Sea of Thieves in 2021, I felt a pang of disappointment - where was the progression? The thrill of discovering hidden treasures was exhilarating, but the lack of upgrading my ship left me feeling stuck. I'm not alone in craving more depth in ship games. I'm on the hunt for games that let me chart my own course, upgrade my vessel, and experience the joy of progression on the high seas.

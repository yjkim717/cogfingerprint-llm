**Title: The Echo Chamber & Finding Your Own North Star**

Remember back in 2021 when we were *all* glued to our phones, scrolling endlessly? I definitely was. I was constantly seeking advice online – career shifts, relationships, even what color to paint my apartment. It felt like everyone had an opinion, and a very loud one at that.

It’s a little scary, but so much more rewarding.

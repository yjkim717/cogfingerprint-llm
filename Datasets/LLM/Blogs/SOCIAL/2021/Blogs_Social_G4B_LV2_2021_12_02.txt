**The Quietest Introductions**

Remember back in 2021, when everyone was frantically refreshing their feeds, trying to *appear* busy and connected? It felt… exhausting, honestly. I realized I was so focused on broadcasting my life that I was missing the truly important moments. 

Recently, I adopted a tiny kitten, and she’s been incredibly nervous since she came home. It’s made me think about introductions – not just to a new pet, but to new experiences and new people. Sometimes, the quietest moments, the ones where we’re simply *present*, are the most valuable.  It’s about letting people – and kittens – settle in at their own pace.

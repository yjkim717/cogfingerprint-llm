"Summer Blues: When Sunshine Feels Suffocating"

As I watch the world around me bask in the warmth of summer, I'm hit with a wave of anxiety. The pressure to be carefree and happy feels suffocating. Social media is filled with sun-kissed selfies and beach vacations, making me feel like I'm not measuring up. My body image issues resurface, and I feel self-conscious in my own skin. It's ironic that a season meant to be joyful can trigger such sadness. I'm not alone in feeling this way - let's break the silence around summer anxiety and seasonal sadness.

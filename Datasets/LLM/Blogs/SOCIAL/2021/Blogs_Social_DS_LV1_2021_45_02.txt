It’s 2021, and my Apple Watch battery is draining faster than my motivation on a Monday. Turns out, WatchOS 7.2’s new handwashing detection is the culprit—constantly listening with its mic and sensors. It got me thinking: even our tech is hyper-aware of hygiene now, a quiet reflection of our pandemic-altered lives. We’re all just trying to stay safe, but sometimes, it feels like the world itself is on high alert, batteries and all.

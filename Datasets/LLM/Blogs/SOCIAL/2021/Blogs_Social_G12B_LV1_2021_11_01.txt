## The Unexpected Joy of Giving Back (and Building a Portfolio!)

It’s wild to think it’s already late 2021! I’ve been feeling a pull to connect more, and decided to shake things up. I’m offering free headshots and portraits right now – a chance for me to build my portfolio, and a gift to folks in the Katy area. Seeing people’s faces light up when they get those first few edited photos? That’s a feeling you just can't buy. It’s been incredibly rewarding.

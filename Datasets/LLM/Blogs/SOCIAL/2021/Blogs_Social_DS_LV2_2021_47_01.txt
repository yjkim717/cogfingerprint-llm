Remember that beautiful bullet journal I started in January, full of hope and colorful pens? For weeks, it sat on my nightstand, a silent monument to my ambition. I was so caught up in making it Pinterest-perfect that I forgot the whole point: to actually *use* it.

My mistake was trying to build the perfect habit tracker before I’d even built the habit. This year, I’m starting over. No fancy layouts, just me, a pen, and one task: write one thing, every day. The organization can come later. Right now, it’s just about showing up.

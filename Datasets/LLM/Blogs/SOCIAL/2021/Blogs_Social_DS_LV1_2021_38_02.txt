It’s wild to think about that unreleased Boba Fett toy turning up on *Pawn Stars* back in 2021. It got me reflecting on how many unique items—maybe even stolen prototypes—pass through pawn shops, often without anyone asking the tough questions. In a world where so much is bought and sold anonymously, it makes you wonder: what’s the real story behind the treasures we chase, and who might be missing a piece of their history?

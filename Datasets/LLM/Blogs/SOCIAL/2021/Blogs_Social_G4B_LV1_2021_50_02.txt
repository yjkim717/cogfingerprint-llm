**Remember When We *Actually* Talked?**

Honestly, scrolling through my feed in 2021 felt…weird. Everyone was sharing curated highlights, but the genuine, messy conversations felt scarce. I remember feeling this desperate urge to connect beyond a like or a comment.  Like, did anyone *really* hear what the other person was saying? 

I started making a conscious effort to pick up the phone – a real, live voice. It felt so much more grounding.  It made me realize that social media can be a fantastic tool, but nothing beats a face-to-face chat or a shared laugh.  Anyone else feeling this way?

>## The Unexpected Lesson in My Driveway

Remember that feeling of being totally out of your depth? I had it last weekend. Attempting a DIY oil change – fueled by pandemic-era frugality – quickly turned into a sweaty, anxious battle with my car jack. Turns out, YouTube tutorials don’t always translate to reality. It was a humbling reminder that sometimes, asking for help (and admitting you don't know everything) is the safest and smartest route. Now, I'm scheduling a professional appointment!

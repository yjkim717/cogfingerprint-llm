"Uncovering the Weight of Family Secrets: My Journey to Healing"

As I navigate 2021, I'm confronted with a harsh reality - a family secret that's shaken my mental health. The trauma lingers, threatening to undo my progress. I've turned to counseling, seeking a safe space to process my emotions. It's not easy, but I'm determined to cope and heal. With each session, I'm slowly unlearning the pain and finding solace in vulnerability. I'm not alone, and I'm learning to be kinder to myself. This journey is mine, and I'm taking it one step at a time.

"Uncovering the Unspoken: When Family Secrets Shake Your Mental Health"

As I navigated the complexities of my mental health journey in 2021, I thought I was making progress. But the revelation of a long-held family secret sent me reeling. The familiar feelings of depression crept back in, threatening to undo all I'd worked for. I felt like I was back at square one, struggling to cope with the trauma that had been hidden behind closed doors. It was a harsh reminder that healing is not linear, and that sometimes, the most unexpected twists can test our strength.

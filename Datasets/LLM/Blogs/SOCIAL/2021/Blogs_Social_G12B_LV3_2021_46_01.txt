## The Weight of "What If?" in 2021

There’s a quiet ache settling in for those of us born in the late 80s/early 90s, isn’t there? Scrolling through TikTok, seeing Gen Z effortlessly embrace experiences we only dreamed of, a little pang of regret hits. Concerts, festivals, a certain carefree vibe... it’s not jealousy, exactly. More like a wistful "what if?" It’s a strange generational feeling, this quiet disappointment about missed moments, amplified by a world still navigating 2021's uncertainties.

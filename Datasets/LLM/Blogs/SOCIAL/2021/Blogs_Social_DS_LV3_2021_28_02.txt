Looking back at 2021, my leap from a sleek Mac to my first custom PC—built around an AMD Ryzen processor—felt like more than just a tech upgrade. It was a social awakening. Suddenly, I wasn’t just consuming content; I was sharing my build specs online, bonding with fellow gamers over peripherals, and joining communities I’d only ever observed. That tower of components became my passport to genuine connections, transforming my solitary screen time into a vibrant, shared digital life.

## What If Halo's Stories Were Told Differently?

Remember diving into *Halo 2* and *Halo 3* back in the day? Lately, I've been pondering something sparked by a Reddit discussion: Should a novelization of those games have woven in *Halo 3: ODST*'s story? It's fascinating how interconnected these narratives feel now, but imagining a single, sprawling novel encompassing everything… it’s a lot! What do you think – standalone brilliance or interwoven epic?

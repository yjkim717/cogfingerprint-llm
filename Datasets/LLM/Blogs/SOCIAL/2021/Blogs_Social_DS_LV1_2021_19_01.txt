It’s 2021, and honestly, my social feeds felt like an endless scroll of bad news. Then I stumbled on Pete Beard’s YouTube channel. Instead of another hot take, here was this calm, deep dive into the lives of historical illustrators. It was my little digital sanctuary. Sharing those videos became a new way to connect—not about the latest outrage, but about beauty and craft. It reminded me that social media can still be a place of discovery and genuine connection.

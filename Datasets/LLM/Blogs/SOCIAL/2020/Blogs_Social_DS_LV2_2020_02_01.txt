It’s 2020, and I’m hiding in a virtual ruin in *Half-Life: Alyx*, trying to line up a shot with this sleek pistol reflex sight. But my aim’s all over the place. Then it hits me—I have to use my dominant eye, something I never think about in real life. It feels awkward, almost unnatural, like trying to write with my other hand.

It’s funny how a VR game can hold up a mirror to our own quirks. In a year when so much feels disconnected, here’s this tiny, personal challenge reminding me that sometimes, you just have to adjust your perspective to hit the mark.

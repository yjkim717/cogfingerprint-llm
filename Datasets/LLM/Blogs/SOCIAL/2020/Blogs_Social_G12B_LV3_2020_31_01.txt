## The Cuddle Question: Is Platonic Touch Really Possible?

Remember 2020? We all craved connection, but physical touch felt… complicated. Lately, I've been pondering something a Reddit post brought up: does cuddling *always* lead to more? I genuinely wonder if platonic physical intimacy – a simple, comforting hug – can exist without the expectation of something else. Am I the only one who finds this a surprisingly tricky line to navigate in relationships? It feels like a vulnerability, doesn’t it?

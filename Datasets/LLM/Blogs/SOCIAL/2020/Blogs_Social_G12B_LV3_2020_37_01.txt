## The Current of Creation: Why Worldbuilding Feels So Real Right Now

It's 2020, and honestly, building a fantasy world feels oddly…relevant. I'm wrestling with a continent shaped by a massive, clockwise current – everything from trade to war dictated by its flow. It's mirroring how *everything* feels right now, doesn’t it? Like we're all just trying to navigate a powerful, unseen force. Maybe creating these worlds helps us understand our own a little better. Thoughts?

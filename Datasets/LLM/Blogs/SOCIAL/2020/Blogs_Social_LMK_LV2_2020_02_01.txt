"Lost in the Crosshairs: A Gamer's Frustrating Reality". As I dived into Half Life Alyx in 2020, I was stoked to experience VR gaming like never before. But, my excitement was short-lived. The pistol's reflex sight, touted as a game-changer, became a source of frustration. It dawned on me that the sight was calibrated to a dominant eye - a trait I don't possess. Suddenly, my aiming was off, and my gaming experience suffered. It's astonishing how a simple assumption can alienate players like me, highlighting the importance of inclusivity in game design.

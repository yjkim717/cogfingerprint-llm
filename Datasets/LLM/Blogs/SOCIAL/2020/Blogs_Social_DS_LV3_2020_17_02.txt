The gurgles have been my awkward soundtrack since childhood. In 2020, as my anxiety and emetophobia grew, I learned my inability to burp even had a name: R-CPD. The proposed Botox treatment feels like a light at the end of the tunnel, a chance to feel normal. But the irony isn't lost on me—the very cure might involve my greatest fear, vomiting. It’s a terrifying crossroads between a lifetime of discomfort and confronting the panic that has held me captive for years.

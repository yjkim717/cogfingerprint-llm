**The Uncomfortable Echoes of Connection**

Remember 2020? Scrolling through endless feeds, feeling this… unsettling echo of everyone else’s lives? Small, anxious bursts of updates, followed by larger waves of worry. It was a strange time for our guts – and our social connections. I started noticing these digestive quirks – a little burp of frustration, a gurgle of loneliness – mirroring the chaotic feeling of being so *connected* yet so isolated.  It made me realize how deeply our internal state reflects what’s happening externally.

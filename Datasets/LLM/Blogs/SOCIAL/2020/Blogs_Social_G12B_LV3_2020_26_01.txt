## Planting the Seeds of Logic: Why It Matters Even *Before* the Code

Remember back in 2020, when everyone was suddenly learning to video call? It felt chaotic, right? That's kind of how introducing coding can feel. Lately, I've been focusing on building logical thinking and algorithms with my 7th graders *before* they even touch a line of code. It’s about laying the groundwork for problem-solving – a skill that’s useful far beyond the screen.

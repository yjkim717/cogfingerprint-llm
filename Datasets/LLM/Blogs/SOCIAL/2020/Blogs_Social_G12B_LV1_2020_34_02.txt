## The Uncomfortable Silence of Zoom

Remember 2020? Endless Zoom calls. It was bizarre, right? I’d be mid-presentation, trying to sound professional, and suddenly… a rumble. A gurgle. Once, a truly unfortunate burp. The silence afterward felt monumental. It was a tiny, physical discomfort amplified by the digital spotlight. We were all so *exposed*, even in our own homes. It made me realize how much unspoken communication we miss when we’re not physically together.

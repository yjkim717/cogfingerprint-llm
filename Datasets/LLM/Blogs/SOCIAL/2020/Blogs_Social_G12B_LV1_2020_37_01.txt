## The Way the Water Turns

Been noodling on a worldbuilding idea lately – a continent ringed by a massive, perpetual clockwise current. Imagine: all seafaring relies on understanding its rhythm. Ships *have* to follow it, a constant, predictable circle. It’s wild to think about the cultural impact, right? Like, coastal cities would be defined by where the current flows strongest. It feels… limiting, in a fascinating way. Makes you wonder how societies adapt when the ocean itself dictates your trade routes. Just a thought! What do you think?

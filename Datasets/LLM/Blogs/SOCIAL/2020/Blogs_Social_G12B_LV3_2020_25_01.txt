## The Unexpected Joy of Connecting Online

Remember 2020? Stuck inside, feeling a little lost? For me, it was rediscovering the power of online communities. Suddenly, sharing snippets of my day – a goofy pet video, a half-finished painting – felt…good. It wasn't about chasing likes, but about those little moments of connection, a reminder that even across screens, we weren't truly alone. It was surprisingly comforting.

## The Frustration of Digital Connection

Anyone else feeling the digital disconnect lately? I’ve been trying to keep up with friends through Civilization VI multiplayer, a silly little way to stay connected during all this 2020 craziness. But lately, our games keep… breaking. Desyncs are *constant* since that last update. We’ve tried everything, and it’s just so frustrating! It makes you realize how fragile even our online relationships can feel sometimes.

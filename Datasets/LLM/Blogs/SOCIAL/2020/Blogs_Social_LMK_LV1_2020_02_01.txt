As I dived into Half Life: Alyx in 2020, I encountered an unexpected hurdle - the pistol's reflex sight. It assumed I had a dominant eye, a feature many of us take for granted. But I'm part of a small group without one. Simple gaming mechanics can be exclusionary. The designers' choice highlighted the importance of accessibility in game design. It sparked a conversation about inclusivity, and how a few tweaks can make a big difference for players like me.

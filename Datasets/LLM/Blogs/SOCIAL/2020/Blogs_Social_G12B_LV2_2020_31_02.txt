

## The Unspoken Expectation

It’s 2020, and even in spaces seemingly built for liberation, unspoken expectations linger. Recently, a Reddit thread really resonated with me – a guy struggling with the assumption that anal sex is a given in gay hookups. It’s a surprisingly common story, isn't it? We all deserve to navigate intimacy on *our* terms, free from pressure and judgment. Boundaries are sexy, folks. Let's normalize communicating them.

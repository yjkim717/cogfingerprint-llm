"Scrolling through Reddit in 2020, I caught myself about to comment on a thread, only to pause. It reminded me of Calvin's infamous 'transmogrifier' - how easily we can transform our thoughts into reckless online statements. I reflected on how consumerist culture fuels this impulsivity. A wave of nostalgia washed over me, recalling the carefree days of reading Calvin and Hobbes. It made me ponder: are we using the internet to genuinely connect, or just to consume and react?"

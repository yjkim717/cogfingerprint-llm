**Scrolling Through a Strange Year**

Remember 2020? We were all glued to our screens, trying to connect amidst the chaos. I spent so much time on social media, seeing everyone’s “perfect” isolation – the sourdough starters, the home workouts, the carefully curated sunsets. It felt…performative. Like everyone was competing for validation while we were all just trying to survive.  I started noticing a weird disconnect, a longing for *real* connection, not just digital likes.  It was a strange, unsettling shift.

Remember that frantic week in 2020, trying to get the last online trophy for *Battlefield 4* before the servers shut down? I spent hours in empty lobbies, chasing a ghost. It hit me then—these digital accolades aren't about skill, but luck and timing. Why are they lumped with our hard-earned platinum trophies? They’re a record of a game’s fading heartbeat, not our dedication. It’s time we separated the permanent from the perishable in our collections.

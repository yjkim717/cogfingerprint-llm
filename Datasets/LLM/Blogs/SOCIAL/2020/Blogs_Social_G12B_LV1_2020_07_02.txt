## Finding My Anchor in a Year That Felt Like a Whirlwind

2020. Just saying the year feels heavy, doesn't it? It felt like the world was spinning, and I was desperately trying to hold on. Between the news, the uncertainty, and the sheer strangeness of everything, I realized I needed something… solid. Something *mine*.

Then, I started swapping out some takeout for simple, home-cooked meals – salads, roasted veggies, things that actually made me *feel* good.

It wasn’t about drastic changes or a perfect physique. It was about carving out a little pocket of control in a world that felt completely out of it. It sounds simple, but those little routines – the exercise, the healthier food – became my anchor. They reminded me that even when everything else was chaotic, I could still take care of myself. And that, in itself, felt like a victory. Maybe it can be yours too.

**The Strange Comfort of Zoom**

Remember 2020? It felt like everyone was stuck underwater, desperately waving their hands for connection. I spent so much of that year staring at a screen, attending Zoom meetings – awkward silences punctuated by the frantic clicking of mute buttons.  It wasn't ideal, but there was a weird comfort in it, like watching someone else swing an axe, a little distanced and safe.  We were all just trying to find a way to embed a piece of ourselves into each other’s lives, even if it was through pixels.  It made me realize how fragile and precious those digital moments felt.

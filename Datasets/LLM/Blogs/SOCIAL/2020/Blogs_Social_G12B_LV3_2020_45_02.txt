## Hotel Keys and Unexpected Magnetism: A 2020 Travel Lesson

Remember 2020? Travel felt… different. I was cautiously optimistic about a quick weekend getaway, and everything felt wonderfully normal until I got to the hotel. Turns out, my iPhone 12’s MagSafe magnet and my AirPods case were a surprisingly disruptive duo! A quick Reddit thread revealed others had the same issue – demagnetized hotel keys. Who knew tech could throw a wrench in a relaxing escape? A good reminder to be mindful of those magnetic stripes!

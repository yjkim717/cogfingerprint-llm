Asking someone out can be nerve-wracking, especially when they're just visiting from out of state. You're wondering if it's worth the risk, and preparing for a "let's just be friends" response. As we navigate our social lives in 2020, it's easy to get caught up in romantic expectations. Take a deep breath, be genuine, and ask them out - you never know what might happen, and either way, you'll gain clarity on your feelings.

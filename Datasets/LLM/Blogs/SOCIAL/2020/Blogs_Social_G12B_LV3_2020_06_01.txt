## The Weight of It All (and 2020's Part in It)

Remember 2020? It feels like a lifetime ago, and yet, the echoes linger. For me, it wasn't just about lockdowns and uncertainty; it unearthed a deeper feeling – a quiet, persistent dread. Suddenly, the things I’d chased felt…hollow. The hustle, the striving, the carefully curated online persona – it all seemed pointless.

There was a simmering frustration, a sense of being a small cog in a machine that didn't care. Worthlessness crept in, whispering doubts I’d long buried. I know I wasn’t alone in feeling this disillusionment. Maybe 2020 just stripped away the layers, revealing a raw vulnerability we all share: the unsettling question of *what’s it all for?*

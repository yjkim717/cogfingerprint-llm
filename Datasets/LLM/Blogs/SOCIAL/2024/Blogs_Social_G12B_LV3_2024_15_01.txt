## The Nickel Hunt Continues...

It's funny, isn't it? Some hobbies become all-consuming. For years, I’ve been on a quiet quest: finding 1962-64 D nickels. The thrill of the hunt, sifting through change, imagining the history held in those small coins… it’s captivating. Now, with online marketplaces booming, the question lingers: do I buy them, or keep digging? What are your collecting dilemmas?

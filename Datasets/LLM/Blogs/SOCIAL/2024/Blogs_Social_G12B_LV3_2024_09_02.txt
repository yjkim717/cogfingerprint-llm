## Should You Jump on This Refinance Wave?

Okay, so mortgage rates dipped – a welcome surprise in 2024! It's got me thinking about a lot of homeowners wondering the same thing: should I refinance? It’s tempting to chase those lower interest rates and shave some money off your monthly payments. But it’s also a big decision. Are rates likely to drop even further, or is now the moment to act? It’s definitely worth exploring your options!

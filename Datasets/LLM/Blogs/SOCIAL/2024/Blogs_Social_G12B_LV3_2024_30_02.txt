## The Endless Scroll and the Question Mark

Anyone else feel like 2024 is a deep dive into online rabbit holes? I was just wrestling with a car repair – new wheel bearing hubs, you know the drill – and found myself lost in Rock Auto’s product descriptions. Do they include the axle nut? The ABS line?

## Little Joys & Growing Collections

Isn't it funny how small things can just brighten your day? I recently snagged a Yeti tumbler – a little departure from my usual, but I’m loving it! It’s got me thinking about all the little collections I’ve amassed: vinyl records steadily growing, a few bottles of favorite wine tucked away… It’s the simple pleasures, right? Finding joy in tangible things feels extra grounding in 2024.

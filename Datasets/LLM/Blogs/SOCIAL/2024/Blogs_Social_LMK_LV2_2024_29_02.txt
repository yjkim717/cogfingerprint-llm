"Finding My Voice, Losing My Ground: A Journey Through Adversity. In 2024, my world was turned upside down when my house burned down, forcing me to navigate the harsh reality of the housing crisis. The financial strain was overwhelming, and I was forced to make impossible choices. One of the hardest was quitting voice therapy, a crucial part of my transgender journey. The struggle to afford basic needs while fighting for mental health is a harsh truth many face. I'm not alone in this fight, and sharing my story is my first step towards healing."

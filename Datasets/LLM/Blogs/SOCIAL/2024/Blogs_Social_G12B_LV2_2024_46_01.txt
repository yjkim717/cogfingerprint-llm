

## The Eternal Goodreads Struggle (Still!)

Okay, fellow bookworms, let's talk Goodreads. It’s 2024, and I’m *still* banging my head against the mobile site's UI. Marking a book "currently reading" is nice, but what about those chapters I devoured on the train? That feeling of actually tracking progress? It’s such a small thing, yet it impacts the whole reading experience. Anyone else feel me on this? Maybe I’m just asking for too much…

"Feeling Frustrated by False Accusations: A Pre-recorded Interview Nightmare"

In 2024, universities are embracing innovative assessment methods, but what happens when technology fails you? I was accused of cheating during a pre-recorded interview, despite my meticulous preparation. The technical issues that arose during the process were misinterpreted as a breach of academic integrity. My frustration grew as I tried to prove my innocence. This experience highlights the need for clearer guidelines and empathy in the digital assessment landscape, ensuring that students aren't unfairly penalized for technical glitches beyond their control.

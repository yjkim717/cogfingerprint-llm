Pulling out of that grocery store lot today, I had to inch my car halfway into the road just to see past the overgrown bushes. It hit me how often we're forced to make decisions with only half the picture. Isn't that true socially, too? We judge others based on a sliver of their story, our view obstructed by our own biases. In 2024, maybe the real exit strategy is to consciously seek a clearer, more compassionate vantage point before we proceed.

"Stuck in Neutral: A Frustrating Drive in 2024"

As I sat idling in a parking lot, I couldn't help but feel frustrated. The tall bushes surrounding the exit obstructed my view, making it a gamble to pull out into traffic. It's 2024, and you'd think road safety would be a top priority. But in this instance, it felt like a game of chance. I wondered, "Is it too much to ask for a clear line of sight?" A simple trim to the bushes could make all the difference.

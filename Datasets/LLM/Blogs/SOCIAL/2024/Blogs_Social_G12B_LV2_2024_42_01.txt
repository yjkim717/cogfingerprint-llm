## My Frankenphone & A Little Workplace Magic

Okay, so I did something a little… unconventional this year. My phone was constantly taking a tumble at work. Instead of replacing it, I MacGyvered a protective case out of some unexpected materials! It looks a bit wild, but customers *love* it. Go figure – a little quirky ingenuity sparked some surprisingly genuine connections. It just proves, sometimes embracing the unusual can be surprisingly rewarding.

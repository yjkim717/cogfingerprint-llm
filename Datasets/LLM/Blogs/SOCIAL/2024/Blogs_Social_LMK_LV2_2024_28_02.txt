"Let's Get Real About Drinking: My Journey to a Healthier Me"

As I stepped into 2024, I realized my weekly drinking habit was getting out of hand. With a family history of alcohol addiction, I knew I had to cut back. But it's hard when drinking is deeply ingrained in our social culture. I'm on a mission to reduce my drinking, not give it up entirely. Join me as I explore the challenges and triumphs of navigating social events with a newfound moderation.

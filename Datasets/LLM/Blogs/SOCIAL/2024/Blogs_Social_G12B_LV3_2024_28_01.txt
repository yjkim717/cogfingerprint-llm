## The Weight of the Block: When Work and Family Collide

It's 2024, and the hustle is real. We’re all chasing that next delivery, that extra dollar, trying to make ends meet. But what happens when life throws you a curveball? Recently, I've been wrestling with that very question as my dad’s health has taken a turn. 

Being an Amazon Flex driver means freedom and flexibility – usually. But needing to be present, to soak up these precious last moments with him, meant taking time off. And that, unfortunately, came with point deductions. 

It's a heavy feeling, that pressure. Trying to balance the demands of work with the undeniable pull of family, especially when facing loss. It’s a reminder that even in the gig economy, sometimes the most important deliveries aren’t packages, but moments. It makes you question, what truly matters?

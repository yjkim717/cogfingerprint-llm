"Stuck on Repeat: The Frustration of Prerecorded Interviews. As I navigated my university's online assessment in 2024, I encountered a prerecorded interview that left me seething. Forced to respond to questions without human interaction, I relied on pre-prepared notes - only to be judged for it. The technical issues that plagued the process only added to my frustration. Is this really fair? As online assessments become the norm, I worry that we're sacrificing the human element for convenience. Am I just being old-fashioned, or is there a better way?"

**Lost in the Threads of Connection**

It’s funny how sometimes, even in a sprawling fantasy world like *Baldur's Gate 3*, the most compelling stories are about the small, tangled threads of human connection. My third run focused heavily on Grymforge, wrestling with the Dreugar and the weight of the Nere’s influence. It reminded me of online communities – those unexpected alliances, the betrayals, the sheer effort to build something meaningful amidst the noise.  Are we truly connecting, or just shouting into the void?

Thinking about this woman’s experience, it reminded me that pain – physical or emotional – is something everyone deals with.  And frankly, our attempts to hide it or minimize it on social media can actually be isolating. 

I think we need to be a little kinder, a little more open, about acknowledging the messy parts of life.

## The Great WiFi Hunt of 2024

Anyone else feel like staying connected on a cruise is a *luxury* these days? I just got back from an Alaskan adventure and was consistently surprised by the internet prices. Specifically, I was hunting for a deal on NCL’s upgraded WiFi – hoping for a flash sale, anything! – but it seemed stubbornly fixed. It really makes you wonder if those “deals” are ever *real* or just clever marketing. Thoughts?

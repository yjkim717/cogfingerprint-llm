**The Parking Lot Paradox**

Anyone else feel that familiar surge of frustration when trying to navigate a parking lot? Last week, I was wrestling with limited visibility – a thicket of bushes blocking my view, and honestly, just a stubborn desire to avoid a certain store. It’s funny how a simple parking situation can trigger this unexpected wave of anxiety, especially in a small car. It made me realize how much we build these little anxieties around everyday moments, and how important it is to just… breathe.

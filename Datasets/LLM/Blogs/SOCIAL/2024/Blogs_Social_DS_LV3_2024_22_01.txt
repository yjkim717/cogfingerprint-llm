After more than a year of sleeping on couches and in shelters, I finally have my own apartment. The first thing I did was run my hand over the cool, smooth surface of the oven—a real kitchen, all mine. Now, I’m staring at this blank canvas, my heart full of hope. I want to fill it with warmth and the smell of good food. What are your favorite simple recipes? This isn't just about cooking; it's about rebuilding a home, one meal at a time.

## The Unexpected Connection: Modding & My Hands

It's 2024, and my latest obsession? Console modding. Seriously, diving into the guts of my Switch has been surprisingly therapeutic. But there's a catch – my hands get *so* sweaty! I'm researching gloves now, something I never thought I'd be doing. It’s funny how a hobby meant to be solitary can lead you down rabbit holes of online forums and gear recommendations. Who knew battling controller slip would be this social?

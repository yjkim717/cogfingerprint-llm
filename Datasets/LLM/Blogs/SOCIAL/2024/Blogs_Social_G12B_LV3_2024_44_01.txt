## Does the Venue Steal the Show? A Recent Opera Experience

Something’s been on my mind since seeing an opera last week. The acoustics were…challenging, to say the least. It got me thinking: how much does amplified sound impact our ability to truly appreciate a performance? It's 2024, and technology is everywhere, but does it sometimes overshadow the raw talent and artistry we’re there to witness? A fascinating, if slightly unsettling, question!



## Did Anyone Else Notice Something... Off About the Klingons?

Okay, Star Trek fans, let's talk! I stumbled across a fascinating thread on Reddit the other day – someone pointed out that the Klingons in *Strange New Worlds* Season 2 seemed… different. Shorter, less booming voices. It got me thinking. We're so used to the established lore, the towering figures and guttural roars, that even subtle shifts feel jarring. 

It's a small thing, right? But it highlights how deeply ingrained these details are in our fandom.  And it's a reminder that even with the best intentions (and those platform boots!), reimagining beloved characters is always going to spark conversation. Anyone else pick up on it? Let's discuss!



## The Unexpected Logic of a Six-Year-Old

My son dropped a truth bomb on me the other day. Apparently, he didn’t share his snack at school because, and I quote, "There weren't enough boogers." He explained, with utter seriousness, that sharing requires a certain… *something* extra. It’s 2023, and kids are navigating sharing in ways we never anticipated. Suddenly, I'm questioning everything I thought I knew about generosity! Anyone else experiencing this delightful chaos?

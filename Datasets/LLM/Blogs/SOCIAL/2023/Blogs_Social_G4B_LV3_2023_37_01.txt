**The Strange Comfort of Online Hypotheticals**

It’s funny, isn’t it? Scrolling through Reddit and stumbling upon a “Would You Rather” post about, like, controlling spiders or accelerating your hair growth? It feels… bizarrely comforting.  2023 is full of anxieties, of course, but these little digital thought experiments – superpowers applied to mundane problems – offer a weirdly safe space. 

Maybe it’s because they’re so utterly detached from reality.  Choosing between a nose-hair-whipping ability or the power to stop mosquitos? It’s a distraction, a gentle reminder that sometimes, a little fantastical escape is exactly what we need.

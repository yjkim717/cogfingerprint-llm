It’s 2023, and I’m still struck by how much we crave real connection in a digital world. Just last week, I met up with an old friend for coffee instead of our usual text thread. We laughed, shared stories, and left feeling truly seen—something a hundred emojis can’t replace. It reminded me that, despite all our tech, the simple act of being present with someone is what truly nourishes our social souls. Let’s not forget that.

Ever since starting Accutane, my anxiety has a new favorite game: “Dehydration or Actual Flu?” I’ll finally commit to the gym, feel that post-workout high, then—bam—wake up shaky and feverish. Is my body fighting something, or did I just not drink enough water? In 2023, we’re all hyper-aware of every ache, but this guessing game is exhausting.

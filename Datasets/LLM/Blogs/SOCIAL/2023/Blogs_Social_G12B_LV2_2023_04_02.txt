

## The Curious Case of the Unknown Bug (and Why I Get It)

Anyone else ever feel like a tiny, green, striped-tailed insect in the vast landscape of the internet? A Reddit thread popped up today – someone in Florida completely stumped by a bug they found. It struck a chord! We’re *all* searching for understanding sometimes, posting our blurry photos and vague descriptions hoping someone, somewhere, can shed some light. It’s a digital echo of that universal need to be seen and identified.

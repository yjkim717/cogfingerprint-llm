I’ve been thinking a lot about this lately, especially when observing online discussions around certain communities (you know, the ones with… *complicated* histories). It’s frustrating to see accountability become a moving target.

The idea of better documentation – really, a clear and accessible record of what’s been said and done – keeps circling back in my mind. It's about building a shared understanding of how these online spaces evolve, and preventing the constant rewriting of history. It’s a big ask, but isn't clarity worth striving for?

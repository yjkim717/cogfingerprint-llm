## The Little Things That Throw You Off (and Why It Matters)

Okay, fellow creatives, anyone else having those frustrating tech moments? I just spent a good ten minutes wrestling with Clip Studio Paint – specifically, my trusty shift key refusing to create those beautiful, free-moving straight lines. Seriously, it's 2023, shouldn't this be automatic?! 

It’s a tiny thing, right? But it’s a reminder that even the tools we rely on can have quirks. These little hiccups can derail your flow and honestly, just make you question everything. Anyone else relate to the digital artist struggle? Let's commiserate!

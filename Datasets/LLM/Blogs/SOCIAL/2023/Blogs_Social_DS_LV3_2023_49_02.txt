There I was, diving back into Baldur’s Gate 3 for a second run, determined to uncover every secret. But Art Cullagh’s quest had me stumped—those Fists just wouldn’t hand over the crucial note! Instead, I was trapped in a loop of the same dialogue, my fists metaphorically clenched in frustration. It’s 2023, and isn’t this just like real life? Sometimes, no matter how many times we ask, the answers don’t come, leaving us wondering if we should just “reload” and start fresh. It’s a quirky reminder that persistence, even in games, mirrors our own social struggles and the patience we must muster.

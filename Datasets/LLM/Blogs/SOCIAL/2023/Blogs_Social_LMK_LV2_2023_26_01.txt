"Feeling Disposable: My ADHD Struggles in the Modern Workplace"

As I sat in my manager's office, I couldn't shake the feeling of dread. Being told I was being replaced due to my struggles with workload and productivity was a harsh reality check. As someone with ADHD, I've always worked harder to prove myself, but it's clear that in today's fast-paced work environment, being different isn't always accepted. My story is a reflection of the struggles many face, and it's time we start talking about creating a more inclusive and supportive workplace culture, where everyone can thrive.

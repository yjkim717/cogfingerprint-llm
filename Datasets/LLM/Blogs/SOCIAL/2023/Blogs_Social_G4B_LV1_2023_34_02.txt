**Threads of Connection**

It’s funny how a simple request can really shift your perspective. Last week, a friend messaged me about a transgender friend experiencing homelessness and struggling to find clothes – not just any clothes, but things that felt *right*, that affirmed their identity. It struck me hard. 

We often think of homelessness as a purely practical issue, but it’s layered with so much vulnerability, especially for trans folks who may be still figuring out their expression and battling internalized shame.  The need for clothing isn’t just about warmth; it’s about feeling visible, feeling safe, feeling like *you*. 

I realized I’d been so focused on grand gestures, but sometimes, a simple donation of a few carefully chosen items, combined with genuine listening and social support, can make a world of difference.  It’s a reminder that connection, in all its forms, is the most powerful resource we have. #transgender #homelessness #socialsupport

## The Weight of a Dollar (and a Suitcase)

Anyone else feeling the pinch of inflation lately? It’s got me thinking about little things, like tipping on our upcoming Royal Caribbean cruise. Those baggage handlers work *hard*, hauling our overstuffed suitcases! Traditionally, I’ve tipped $2-3 per bag. But with prices rising across the board, does that still feel right? It’s a small gesture, but one that feels increasingly meaningful in 2023. What are your thoughts?

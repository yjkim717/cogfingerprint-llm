As I scrolled through Reddit in 2023, I stumbled upon a poll that made me chuckle. Users were asked to choose their ideal everyday superpower from four quirky options: teleporting to anywhere while staying clean, having flawless personal hygiene without effort, instantly growing out your hair, or making others appreciate their own hygiene. The results were telling - most users opted for the teleportation power.

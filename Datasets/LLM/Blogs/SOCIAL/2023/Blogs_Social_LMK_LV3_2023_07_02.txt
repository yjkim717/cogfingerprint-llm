The Nostalgia Factor: Why We Still Miss Discontinued Costco Favorites. 
In a recent Reddit thread, a user's simple question about beloved discontinued items at Costco Canada sparked a wave of nostalgia among fellow shoppers. The discussion highlights the power of product loyalty and how it can be deeply rooted in emotional connections.

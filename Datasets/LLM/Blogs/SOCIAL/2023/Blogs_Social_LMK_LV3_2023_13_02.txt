As I navigated pregnancy in 2023, genetic carrier screening became a pressing consideration. Despite no known family history of genetic disorders, health anxiety lingered. Prenatal testing offered reassurance, but also sparked unease. I grappled with the uncertainty, weighing the benefits of screening against the fear of what it might reveal. Ultimately, understanding my genetic health empowered me to take control, easing pregnancy anxiety and allowing me to focus on the joy of expecting.

**The Unexpected Science of a Good Chat**

Anyone else feel like social media lately is… a lot? I was wrestling with it the other day, actually, while trying to make a really good batch of broth. I was stubbornly following online advice about pot size, completely ignoring a tiny voice whispering about physics. It felt silly, right? 

But then it hit me – it’s kind of like a conversation. You need the right space (the pot!), and you need to manage the heat (the connection). Overcrowding – or forcing a conversation – just leads to a messy, bitter result. Maybe we need to be more mindful of *how* we engage, not just *where* we do it.  What do you think?

"Can't a Guy Get Some Sleep? My Frustrating Quest for a Quiet Night" 

I've always been a light sleeper, but living in a densely populated neighborhood in 2023 has turned bedtime into a frustrating battle. My neighbor's dog seems to bark at every. single. thing. I've tried everything to drown out the noise - earplugs, a white noise machine, and even noise-cancelling headphones. Still, I'm stuck wide awake. It's not just about me; I'm worried about being a grumpy neighbor. I'm calling on those with similar struggles: how do you cope with noisy neighbors? Share your tips - I'm desperate for a good night's sleep!

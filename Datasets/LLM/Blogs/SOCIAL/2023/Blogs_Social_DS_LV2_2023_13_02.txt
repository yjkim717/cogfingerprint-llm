The pamphlet said “carrier screening” in cheerful blue letters. I’m young, healthy, with no family history of genetic disorders. So why was my heart pounding? It felt like a choice between blissful ignorance and a potential Pandora’s box of ‘what-ifs’. My health anxiety, a constant companion, whispered all the worst-case scenarios. But then I thought of my future, our family. Knowledge, even the scary kind, is power. I took a deep breath and picked up the phone to schedule the test.

## The $25 Million Question & Our Love for the Weird

Okay, so I saw this Reddit thread buzzing about A24's *Beau is Afraid* and it got me thinking. $25 million is a *lot* of money to pour into an arthouse film, especially when the box office numbers aren't exactly screaming success. It’s 2023, and we’re so used to blockbuster formulas. But isn’t there something beautiful about a studio championing something genuinely… strange? It makes you wonder if financial viability should *always* be the ultimate measure of artistic value. What do you think?

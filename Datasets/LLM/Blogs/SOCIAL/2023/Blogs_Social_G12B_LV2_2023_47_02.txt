## The Echo in the Algorithm

Isn't it funny how trends work? Lately, I've been noticing a pattern online – a build-up, a surge of voices, and then…a response. Like a call and answer. It reminds me actually of that funk song I was searching for the other day - a driving beat, a male vocal climbing higher, and a powerful female choir answering back.

Social media feels a bit like that sometimes. We shout our opinions, our anxieties, our joys, and then wait for the echo.  Do people agree? Do they challenge?  It’s a constant cycle. And while the sequencer of likes and shares can be addictive, I'm trying to remember to listen for the *real* responses, the ones that resonate deeper than the algorithm allows. What about you?

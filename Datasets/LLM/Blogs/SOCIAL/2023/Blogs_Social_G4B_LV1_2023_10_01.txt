**The Quiet Frustration of Seeing Something Disappear**

It’s funny, isn’t it? How something you *know* is there can suddenly just… vanish. I’ve been wrestling with this feeling lately, mostly on social media. I’ll post something – a thought, a picture, a little piece of myself – and it gets a few likes, maybe a comment. Then, poof. It's gone from the main feed, swallowed by the endless scroll. 

It’s not about the numbers, really. It’s the feeling of that little digital ghost, that trace of a connection, disappearing. Like a module import error in Flask – you *know* the code exists, you’ve used it before, but the system just can’t find it. 

I'm trying to be more mindful of sharing, of acknowledging that not everything needs a spotlight. Maybe, just maybe, some things are better left quietly present, instead of chasing the fleeting validation of a disappearing post.

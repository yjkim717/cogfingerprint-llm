Ever since starting HRT, I’ve been staring at my reflection, wondering how these tiny pills will reshape me. As someone who’s always been skinny with a lightning-fast metabolism, the promise of "feminine curviness" feels both thrilling and distant. Will my body finally cooperate and soften into those gentle slopes? It’s a strange, hopeful waiting game in 2023, learning to trust that change takes time, and that my own shape is still being written.

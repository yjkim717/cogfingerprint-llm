For three years, my ADHD brain and I tried to keep pace with the relentless demands of the workplace. The constant struggle to prioritize, the forgotten deadlines piling up—it created a storm of silent stress I thought I was managing.

Today, I found out they’re hiring my replacement. The news hit with a strange mix of devastation and grim validation. In 2023, we talk so much about mental health, yet the reality for many of us is still a quiet, desperate race we’re set up to lose.

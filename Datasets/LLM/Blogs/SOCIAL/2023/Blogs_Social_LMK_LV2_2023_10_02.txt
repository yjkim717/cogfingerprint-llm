As we juggle multiple expenses in 2023, managing our finances can be overwhelming. I turned to RShiny to create a personalized budgeting app that updates my data frame in real-time. But, deploying it was a challenge. I explored free and affordable options to share my app with the world. In this post, I'll share my journey of deploying my RShiny budgeting app on shinyapps.io and other platforms, and the lessons I learned along the way.

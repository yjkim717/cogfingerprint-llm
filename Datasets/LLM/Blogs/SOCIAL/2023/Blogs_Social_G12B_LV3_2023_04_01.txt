## The Unexpected Discomfort of Grounded Sleep

Anyone else ever experience a bizarre shift in comfort? I recently moved my mattress to the floor – a temporary solution, I thought – and suddenly, sleep became a battle. For years, this same mattress has been my cozy haven. Now? Tossing, turning, and a distinct feeling of…unease. 

It's a strange reminder that our environment profoundly impacts us. Even something as seemingly simple as a sleeping surface can trigger insomnia and disrupt what felt like a solid routine. Anyone else relate to the unexpected ways our surroundings can throw us off balance?

"Feeling replaceable at work is a harsh reality many of us face, but for those with ADHD, it's a constant fear. I've lost count of how many times I've been told I'm not meeting expectations, only to be replaced without warning. It's 2023, and we're slowly breaking down the stigma around neurodiversity, but there's still a long way to go. As I navigate the aftermath of being let go, I'm reminded that my worth isn't defined by my job - and that's a hard pill to swallow."

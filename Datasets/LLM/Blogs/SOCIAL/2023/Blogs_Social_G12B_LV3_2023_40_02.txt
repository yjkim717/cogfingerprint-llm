## That Little Bump and the Big Worry

It’s funny how a tiny thing – a small, soft lump I found just the other day – can spiral into a whole avalanche of worry. In 2023, with everything instantly searchable online, it's so tempting to self-diagnose. But the internet can also amplify anxiety, right? I’m caught in that familiar loop of "what ifs" and medical questions. Anyone else ever feel completely consumed by a minor health concern? Just putting it out there – hoping a little shared experience can ease the uncertainty.

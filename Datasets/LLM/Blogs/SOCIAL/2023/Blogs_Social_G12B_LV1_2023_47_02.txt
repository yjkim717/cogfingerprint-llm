## The Echo in the Algorithm

It’s funny, isn’t it? How a random search for a groove – a frantic funk song with that driving guitar riff and a building male vocal, punctuated by a female choir’s response – ended up making me think about social media. That call-and-response, that push and pull… it’s *exactly* how we interact online. We shout our opinions (the male vocal), and the algorithm, a kind of digital choir, chimes back with what it thinks we want to hear. “Come on,” it seems to say. “Engage! React! Repeat!” Sometimes, I just want to turn it all off and lay down a solid bassline.

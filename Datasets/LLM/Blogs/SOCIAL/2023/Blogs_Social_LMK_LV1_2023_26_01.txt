Navigating the Modern Workplace with ADHD: A Personal Struggle. 
In today's fast-paced work environment (2023), I'm not alone in struggling to keep up. As someone living with ADHD, I've faced my fair share of challenges at work. Recently, my manager's comment that they're "looking for my replacement" hit hard. It made me reflect on how my brain works differently and the impact on my productivity. I'm now seeking strategies to thrive, not just survive, in my role and break down stigmas around mental health in the workplace.

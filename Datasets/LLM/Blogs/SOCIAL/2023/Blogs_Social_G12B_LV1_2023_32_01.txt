Honestly, I've been avoiding a certain thought. The 'D' word. Depression.  It feels… monumental.  Like admitting something is wrong would somehow make it *more* real.

So, I'm tentatively, cautiously, starting to consider talking to someone. Actually *saying* the words "I think I might be depressed." It feels vulnerable, exposing.  But maybe, just maybe, acknowledging the hum is the first step to silencing it, or at least understanding where it’s coming from.  Anyone else ever felt this hesitant to seek help?  I'd love to hear your thoughts.

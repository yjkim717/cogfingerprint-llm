"Juggling the Unpredictable: My Life as a Part-Time UPS Worker"

As a part-time UPS worker in 2023, I'm no stranger to uncertainty. The on-call schedule can be grueling, making it tough to plan my life. To make ends meet, I'm considering a second job at FedEx. It's a big decision, but the promise of a more stable income is tempting. I'm weighing the pros and cons, wondering if taking on more work is worth the strain. Is it time to diversify my income streams?

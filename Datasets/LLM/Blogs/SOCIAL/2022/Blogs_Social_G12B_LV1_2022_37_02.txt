>## The Everyday Magic of Our Feeds

Remember when social media felt… simpler? Back in 2010, it was sharing vacation photos and catching up with old friends. Now, scrolling feels like a constant low-level spellcasting. We're summoning validation with carefully curated posts, levitating our anxieties with filters, and brewing a potent potion of comparison with every perfectly-lit influencer pic. 

It makes you wonder, what’s the most frequently used "spell" of 2022? Probably a quick edit followed by a nervous tap of the 'post' button. Anyone else feel like they’re just trying to get by with a few basic charms in this digital world?

## The Delicious Discomfort of Opera's Downfall

Okay, hear me out. I stumbled across a Reddit thread the other day about finding catharsis in opera – specifically, watching characters like Don Giovanni or Tosca meet their end. It’s a weirdly satisfying feeling, isn't it? In 2022, amidst all the chaos, there's something comforting in witnessing dramatic, operatic justice, even if it’s just on stage.

It was 2022, and I thought I was the hunter. Armed with a notorious mod, my mission in Willow Creek was grimly simple. But in a digital world, even the most calculated plans can shatter. Eliza Pancakes, a background character in my own story, became an autonomous force of nature. She didn't just fight back; she ended me. My screen faded to black, and a cold, virtual lesson settled in: no one, not even the player, is truly safe from the chaos they unleash. Revenge suddenly felt so personal.

## The Hidden Costs of Getting That First Job

It’s 2022, and you’d think landing a job would be the biggest hurdle for a young adult. My son, eager to start his career at Walmart, thought so too. What we *didn’t* anticipate were the layers of unexpected expenses. The physical exam alone? A hefty bill. Then there’s the need for specific identification – another cost we hadn’t factored in. 

It really hit me: even getting *into* the workforce can feel financially prohibitive. We're constantly talking about the cost of living, but rarely about the upfront costs of simply securing employment. It’s a frustrating reminder that the path to independence isn't always as straightforward as we hope it will be.

The curious case of 'fine'. In 2022, we often use this word to convey opposing emotions. Say you're asked how you're doing, and you respond with a casual 'fine'. It can mean you're genuinely okay or, conversely, that you're struggling to cope. Linguistics experts attribute this to etymology and context. The meaning of 'fine' shifts depending on the situation, highlighting the complexities of language and the importance of understanding the nuances behind the words we use daily.

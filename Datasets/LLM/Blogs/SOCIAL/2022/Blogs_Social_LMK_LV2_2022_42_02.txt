"The True Cost of Getting Hired: A Mother's Frustration"

As a mother, I've seen my son struggle to get a job at Walmart. In 2022, it's astonishing that the costs of employment aren't just about the paycheck. My son had to renew his expired ID and undergo a physical examination, adding up to a hefty expense. It's disheartening to think that the job requirements are pricing out eager workers. Is it fair to expect job applicants to bear these costs? As we navigate the job market, we must consider the unseen expenses that come with getting hired.

## The Quiet Longing of Online Worlds

It's funny, isn't it? How in 2022, we’re *more* connected than ever, yet sometimes feel utterly alone. I stumbled across a post the other day – a teenager looking for someone to share a fantasy roleplay with. Giants, angst, escape… a whole world built between two people.

It struck a chord. That desire to create something, to lose yourself in a shared narrative, feels so incredibly human. We chase it in books, movies, games… and sometimes, in these little corners of the internet.

There’s a vulnerability in putting yourself out there, admitting you want to build a world with someone else. A hope that someone, somewhere, feels that same pull, that same need to explore the "what ifs" and the deeply felt emotions. 

It reminded me that even in a world of constant scrolling and fleeting connections, the search for genuine connection – for someone to share a story with – remains a powerful, and beautiful, thing. Maybe we all need a little giant-sized escape sometimes.

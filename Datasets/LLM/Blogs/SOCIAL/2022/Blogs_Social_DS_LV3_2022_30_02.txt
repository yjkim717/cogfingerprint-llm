There are days when the world feels like it’s got it out for you personally. You try your best—hold doors, listen patiently, offer kindness—yet it all seems to boomerang back as exhaustion. Meanwhile, others glide through life effortlessly. It’s 2022, and I’m more alienated than ever, wondering if anything truly matters. This creeping fatalism is a heavy coat to wear in a crowd. Maybe you’ve felt it too: that quiet, draining certainty that nothing really changes, no matter what you do.

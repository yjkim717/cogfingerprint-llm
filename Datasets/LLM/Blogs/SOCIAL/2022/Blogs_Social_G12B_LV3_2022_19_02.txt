Lately, a discussion's been brewing on Reddit about Shotdeck – a site offering screenshots from films, often used for "photobashing" and artist referencing. It’s got me thinking. As artists, we all pull inspiration from somewhere, but where's the line? Can monetizing *those* inspirations, even in a transformative way, truly be fair use?

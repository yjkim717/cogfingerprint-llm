It’s 5 a.m. on Christmas morning, 2022, and my living room is full of flying broccoli. Inspired by Bluey’s "Veranda Santa," my kids decided our family needed a new tradition—tossing veggies onto the porch at dawn. The thuds and giggles woke the whole house, and honestly? It was magical. In a world that often feels divided, there’s something grounding about these simple, silly moments of connection. Who knew a cartoon could bring us closer, one thrown carrot at a time?

**The Unexpected Cost of Fighting: Cancer & The Financial Fallout**

It’s funny, isn't it? You hear “cancer diagnosis” and you think of treatment, appointments, fighting for your life. You *don’t* necessarily think of spreadsheets, frantic calls to insurance, and the slow, creeping dread of financial ruin. But that's been my reality for the last year.

I was diagnosed with malignant melanoma last spring – 2022 felt like it just exploded.  Thankfully, the treatment seemed to be working, but the bills? They were relentless. Even with insurance, the co-pays, deductibles, and the sheer volume of medical expenses started to feel insurmountable. We tapped into our savings, which felt like a punch to the gut, and I’m still grappling with the weight of what we’ve lost.

It's isolating. You're battling a serious illness, and simultaneously, you're navigating a system that feels designed to squeeze every penny out of you.  It makes you question everything – your priorities, your future, and the fundamental fairness of it all. I’ve connected with others online who understand, and that’s been a lifeline. Knowing you're not alone in this silent struggle is…well, it’s everything.

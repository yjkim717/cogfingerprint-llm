"Behind the Wheel: The Unseen Struggles of a Delivery Driver"

As I navigated the busy streets in my UPS truck in 2022, I couldn't help but feel the weight of my job. The constant pressure to deliver packages on time, coupled with the stress of managing a demanding schedule, was taking a toll on my well-being. To make ends meet, I had taken on a second job, but the irregular hours as a delivery driver made it challenging to balance both. The real struggle, however, wasn't the physical demands of the job, but the management issues that seemed to overshadow our hard work. I often found myself wondering if there was a way to advance in my career without sacrificing my sanity. As I looked around at my fellow drivers, I realized I wasn't alone in this struggle. It's time we shed light on the unseen challenges faced by delivery drivers like me.

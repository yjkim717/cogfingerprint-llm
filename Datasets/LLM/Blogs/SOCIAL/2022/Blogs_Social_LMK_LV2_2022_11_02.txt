"Dealing with a Melanoma Diagnosis and a Frustrating Insurance Claim"

Receiving a melanoma diagnosis is life-altering. On top of navigating treatment, I had to fight for my short-term disability claim with MetLife. Their handling of my case was infuriating. In 2022, I wasn't alone in this struggle. Many face similar battles with insurance companies. I felt lost and alone, but sharing my story can help others. If you're going through a similar ordeal, know you're not alone. Let's stand together and advocate for fair disability coverage. Our health, not insurance red tape, should be the priority.

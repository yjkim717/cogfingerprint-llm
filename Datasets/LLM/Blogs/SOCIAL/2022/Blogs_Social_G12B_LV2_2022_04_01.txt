## The Weight of a Lyric

Okay, hear me out. I was deep down a TikTok rabbit hole (aren't we all?) when a lyric from Danny Gonzalez’s song stopped me cold. It playfully favored Nick Jonas over Kevin. Seemed lighthearted, but it sparked something. Do we really dissect every detail of online personalities? It’s a reminder to maybe, just maybe, apply a little critical thinking – and lighten up!

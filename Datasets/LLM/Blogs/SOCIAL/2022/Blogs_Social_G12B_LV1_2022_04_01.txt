## The Oddest Rabbit Hole of 2022

Okay, hear me out. I stumbled down a *wild* Reddit thread last night. It was all about a lyric in Danny Gonzalez’s new song – something about wishing he could ghost Kevin or Nick Jonas. Seriously! It sparked a huge debate about interpretation and, honestly, made me question how much we overthink social media art. It's a funny reminder that sometimes, things are just…weird.

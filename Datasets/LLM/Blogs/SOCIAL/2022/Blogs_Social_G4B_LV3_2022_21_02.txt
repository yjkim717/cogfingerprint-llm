**The Pressure Cooker of Online Connection**

Remember 2022? Scrolling felt…intense. Social media felt like a constant pressure cooker. Like *Risk of Rain 2*'s Void Fields – a relentless barrage of damage – every comment, every like, felt like a challenge. It’s easy to get caught up in chasing validation, desperately trying to heal with the ‘right’ emojis. 

But honestly, the constant need to perform, to measure up, left me drained. I started to realize that true connection wasn’t about racking up numbers, but about finding spaces where I could just *be*, without feeling the urgent tick of the clock.

## The Unexpected Rocket League Economy

It’s wild how Rocket League’s blueprint system has become its own little economy. I was scrolling through the trading subreddit last night and saw someone offering to clear out entire inventories for credits – just to get rid of the random wheels and toppers! It feels strange, this digital decluttering, but hey, it’s fueling the game and sparking some surprisingly creative trades. Who knew Rocket League would become so…socially complex?

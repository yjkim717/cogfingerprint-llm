## That Sinking Feeling: When a Job Just…Disappears

Remember that unsettling feeling in 2022 when things felt so uncertain? Well, it hit hard for one Oklahoman recently. They poured three hours into a job, only to be “ghosted” by the employer – no communication, no paycheck. It's a stark reminder that even in today's world, labor laws and fair wages aren't always a guarantee. Anyone else ever felt that sting?

**The Weight of a Word**

Remember scrolling through social media in 2022? It felt… intense.  I was genuinely taken aback by how often seemingly small, casual insults – *beleidigungen* – were actually carrying a criminal charge. It made me think about how easily words can inflict real *schaden*, even if they don’t seem terribly serious at first. 

It’s easy to dismiss online negativity, but the potential for *sprachliche hass* to spread and deeply wound someone is terrifyingly real.  We talk about freedom of speech, but where do we draw the line when words become weapons?  It’s a complex question, and one we really need to keep wrestling with as a *gesellschaft*.

There I was in 2022, staring at my Crown Victoria’s silent engine and the mocking dashes on the odometer. It was turning over slower each day, like my own motivation. I’d just serviced it, yet here we were—both of us stalled. It reminded me how life’s batteries drain quietly, often when we think we’re fully charged. Sometimes, the issue isn’t neglect but something deeper, needing more than a quick fix to truly start again.

## Where Are They Now? The Lingering Question of Hell's Kitchen's Sade

Remember Sade from *Hell’s Kitchen* Season 13? I do! She was seriously talented, and I was *convinced* she should have taken home the win. Gordon Ramsay even hinted at a potential job offer, and it got me thinking – what happened? It’s fascinating how reality TV contestants vanish after the cameras stop rolling. Anyone else ever obsessively wonder about their post-show journeys? It’s a testament to the impact these personalities have, even fleetingly.

**The Odd Comfort of Shared Goals**

Remember 2022? Scrolling through Reddit, I stumbled across a guy offering Xbox credits for Rocket League blueprints – a little shortcut to rank up. It felt…weird, almost transactional. But seeing people willing to help each other, even in a small way, reminded me how much we crave connection.  We’re all just trying to level up, in games and in life, and sometimes a little shared push feels surprisingly good.

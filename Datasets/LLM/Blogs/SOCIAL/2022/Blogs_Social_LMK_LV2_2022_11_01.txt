The Weight of Medical Bills: A Cancer Survivor's Financial Nightmare.

Receiving a cancer diagnosis is devastating, but the financial strain that follows can be just as debilitating. As I navigated my treatment in 2022, I was shocked to discover that even with insurance, the medical bills were crippling. Living paycheck to paycheck, I struggled to make ends meet. The stress of mounting bills was a constant companion, making it hard to focus on healing. My story is not unique; many cancer patients face similar financial struggles. It's time we acknowledge the financial toxicity of cancer treatment and work towards creating a more supportive and equitable healthcare system for all.

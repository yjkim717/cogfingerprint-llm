**Title: The Echo Chamber and the Expert in the Room (It's Usually Me)**

Okay, let's be honest, hasn't anyone else noticed the internet has become… a bit much lately? I've been lurking in some gaming forums (as one does), and it’s just a constant stream of people declaring themselves the ultimate authorities on everything. And if you dare to suggest a different perspective, brace yourself.

It’s frustrating, right? You’re just trying to have a conversation, share a thought, maybe even learn something, and you’re met with this wall of, “Well, *actually*…” and a complete refusal to consider anything outside their established viewpoint. It’s like everyone's built their own little echo chamber, and any dissenting voice is immediately dismissed. 

I've definitely been guilty of it myself, clinging to my opinions with a surprising level of defensiveness. Maybe we all need to take a breath, remember we’re all just figuring things out, and actually *listen* to each other a little more. 2022 needs a little more empathy, don’t you think?

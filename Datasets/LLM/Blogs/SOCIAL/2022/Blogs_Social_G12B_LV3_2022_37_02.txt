## Beyond "Expelliarmus": What Everyday Magic Do We *Really* Use?

Remember that Reddit thread about wizarding world spells? It got me thinking. We often focus on the flashy magic – the big battles, the dramatic saves. But what about the *mundane* magic? The spells that make daily life easier? I bet a lot of us wizards are silently using charm-like shortcuts all the time, aren’t we? Just a little bit of everyday enchantment to get through the week. 2022 felt like we all needed a little extra of that!

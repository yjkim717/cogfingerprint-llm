"Stuck in Neutral: When Your Trusty Ride Won't Start. I still remember my old Crown Vic refusing to budge one morning in 2022. The odometer was on the fritz too! I was frustrated, but it got me thinking - how many of us take our reliable cars for granted until they leave us stranded? Let's troubleshoot together and explore the possible causes behind this issue, and maybe, just maybe, we'll learn to appreciate our trusty rides a little more."

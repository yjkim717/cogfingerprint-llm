"Investing in the Underdogs: A 2022 Reflection"

As I looked back on my investment journey in 2022, I couldn't help but think about the allure of bankrupt companies. Specifically, those that filed for Chapter 11 protection. Their stocks, often relegated to penny stock territory, can be tempting targets for contrarian investors. I considered indexing these underdogs, betting on potential turnarounds or acquisitions. While it's a high-risk strategy, the payoff could be substantial. As I navigated the volatile market landscape, I wondered: could a carefully curated index of Chapter 11 stocks be the key to unlocking hidden value?

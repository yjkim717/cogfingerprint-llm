## The Echo of a Melody I Can't Quite Grab

It's funny how fleeting music can be. I saw a post on Reddit the other day about someone desperately trying to recall a song – cello prominent, haunting vocals – after attempting to recreate it in a sequencer. It hit a nerve. We’ve all been there, haven't we? That phantom melody, just out of reach, a frustrating echo in the mind. 2022 has been a year of fleeting moments, and this felt like another one.

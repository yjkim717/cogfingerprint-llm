"The Laughing Pill: A Cultural Misadventure at Work". I still chuckle thinking about the day my Vietnamese colleague, with limited English, prescribed me 'medication' for my new job jitters. The potent 'vitamin' left me feeling...interesting. As I navigated the side effects, I realized the importance of clear communication in the workplace. In 2022, our diverse workplaces demand more than just language skills - they require empathy and understanding to avoid such humorous, yet awkward, misadventures.

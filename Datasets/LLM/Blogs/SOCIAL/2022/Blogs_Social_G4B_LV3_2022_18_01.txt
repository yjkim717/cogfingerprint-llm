**The Weight of Early Adventures**

Remember those first few D&D sessions in 2022? I was playing a monk, desperately trying to get him airborne with a quirky kenku companion. It felt…slow. Like wading through mud.  There’s a real pressure to deliver exciting gameplay, especially when you’re starting low-level.  It made me think about how we sometimes feel obligated to *immediately* entertain, forgetting that genuine connection and a shared story are built on a slower pace.

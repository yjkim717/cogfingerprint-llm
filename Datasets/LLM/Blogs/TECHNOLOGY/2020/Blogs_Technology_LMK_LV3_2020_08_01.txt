As I navigated the challenges of 2020, I realized that 'digital detoxing' was more than just a trend - it was a necessity. With the pandemic forcing us to be glued to our screens, I found myself feeling overwhelmed. Just like some people are sensitive to fragrances, I became sensitive to the constant notifications and social media updates. Taking a digital break was my path to sobriety from the tech world, and it brought a sense of calm I hadn't experienced in months.

## The Wild West of Online Shopping (and My Canoe Quest)

Remember when online shopping felt… straightforward? Now, it’s a jungle! I'm trying to find a decent canoe to explore some rivers in Southern Europe this summer, and I'm drowning in a sea of ads and questionable Alibaba listings. It’s a stark reminder that sometimes, the best tech solutions still require a little old-fashioned research and maybe, just maybe, a local shop.

## Watching the Game From the Sidelines

It's 2020, and Fortnite's still *huge*. I don’t actually play – the learning curve felt a bit steep! – but I’ve been fascinated by the competitive scene. It’s wild to watch these pros strategize and react at lightning speed. It makes you wonder, can you truly understand a world without fully participating? Anyone else find themselves observing from the outside, appreciating the spectacle?

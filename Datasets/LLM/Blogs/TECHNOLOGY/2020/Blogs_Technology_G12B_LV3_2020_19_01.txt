## The Ghosts of Atlantis and Lost Tech

Remember *Stargate Atlantis*? A Reddit thread got me thinking – those brilliant Atlanteans had ZPMs, incredible power sources, and a vast database. Yet, why no schematics? It’s a strange paradox, isn't it? In 2020, as we grapple with data security and rapidly advancing AI, it highlights a crucial question: how do we balance sharing knowledge with safeguarding against misuse? Maybe some secrets are best left lost.

"As I scrolled through social media in 2020, I felt my frustration and anger boiling over. The Covid pandemic, rising racial tensions, and divisive politics were taking a toll. It seemed like every post was a trigger, fueling my anxiety and sense of helplessness. I realized I needed a break from the constant stream of information. By stepping back from social media, I reclaimed my mental space and found a sense of calm. It was a small step towards regaining control in a chaotic world."

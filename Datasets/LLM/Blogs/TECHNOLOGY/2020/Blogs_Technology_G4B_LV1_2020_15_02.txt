**The Slow Burn of Connection**

Remember 2020? Everyone was chasing the *shiny* – instant gratification, viral trends, that perfect Instagram filter. I was too, initially. Trying to build my online presence overnight. But then, I started gravitating towards the quiet connections. Like a stealth archer, patiently waiting for the right moment to strike – a thoughtful comment, a genuine share. It's a slower, more deliberate approach to building relationships online, and honestly? It feels *way* more rewarding.

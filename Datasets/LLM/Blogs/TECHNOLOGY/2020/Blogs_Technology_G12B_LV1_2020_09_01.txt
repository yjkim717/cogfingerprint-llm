## Do You *Really* Know What's Going On?

It's wild, isn't it? I've been watching Twitch streams of competitive Fortnite – the intense strategies, the lightning-fast reflexes – and I don't even *play* the game regularly. It makes you wonder, can you grasp the depth of a digital world just by observing? Like, do you *get* it without being *in* it? It's a strange disconnect in 2020.

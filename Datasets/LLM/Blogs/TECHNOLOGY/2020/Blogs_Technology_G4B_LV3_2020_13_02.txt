**The Great Digital Purge of 2020**

It felt…necessary. As the year wore on, I realized I was drowning in InDesign files – a chaotic collection built up over a Creative Cloud subscription. The thought of canceling felt daunting, but also strangely liberating. I started seriously researching exporting to Scribus, a free alternative. It’s a reminder that sometimes, letting go of the tools we cling to can be the best way to reclaim our space, both digital and mental.

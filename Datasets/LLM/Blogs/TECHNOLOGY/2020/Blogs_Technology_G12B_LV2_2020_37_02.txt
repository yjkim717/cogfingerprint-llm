## The Unexpected Comfort of Pixelated Precision

Remember 2020? Stuck inside, many of us found solace in digital worlds. Lately, I've been lost in Warframe, specifically tweaking my "Kitgun." It’s a crazy system where you build your own weapon! Right now, I’m wrestling with a choice: pinpoint hitscan accuracy or chaotic, satisfying projectile bursts. It’s surprisingly… therapeutic. Finding that perfect balance, even in a game, feels like a tiny victory these days.

## The Lost Manuals of Our Future

It’s funny, isn’t it? We’re obsessed with documenting *everything* now. Cloud storage, endless backups, digital footprints trailing behind us… yet, sometimes I wonder if we're truly capturing what matters.

I was reading something the other day – a wild sci-fi theory, admittedly – about an ancient civilization (the Atlanteans, apparently!) who powered their city with these incredible energy crystals. The thing is, they had a massive database, but *didn’t* include the instructions on how to *make* those crystals.  

It got me thinking. We're building incredible AI, developing groundbreaking medical tech… but are we diligently documenting *why* we made these choices, the potential pitfalls, the ethical guardrails? What happens when the original architects are gone, and all that’s left are the tools, but not the wisdom to wield them responsibly? It’s a question that feels particularly relevant in 2020.

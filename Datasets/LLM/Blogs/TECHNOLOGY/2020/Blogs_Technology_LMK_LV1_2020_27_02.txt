"Scrolling through social media in 2020 felt like watching a never-ending nightmare. Covid-19 ravaged the globe, racial tensions boiled over into protests against police brutality, and Trump's divisive rhetoric fueled the fire. As I stared at my screen, I felt my frustration grow. The same platforms meant to connect us were now amplifying the chaos. I couldn't help but wonder: are we using technology to bring about positive change, or are we letting it tear us apart? The answer, I realized, lies in how we choose to use it."

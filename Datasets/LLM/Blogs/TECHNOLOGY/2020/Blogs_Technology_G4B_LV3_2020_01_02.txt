**The Echo in the Screen**

Remember 2020? Scrolling through endless feeds, desperately seeking connection while physically isolated? I found myself building walls with notifications, a digital fortress against the loneliness blooming after the breakup. It felt safer to avoid a direct confrontation – a simple ‘unfollow’ – than face the raw disappointment.  The irony wasn’t lost on me: seeking solace in a world that amplified my isolation, fueled by resentment and the fear of rejection, all reflected back at me from the screen.

## Lost in the Algorithm, Searching for Something Real

Remember the thrill of discovery? Now, it feels like everything's curated. I was just trying to find a decent canoe for a trip next summer, and ended up drowning in Alibaba listings. Thousands of options, all shouting for attention. It made me miss the days of flipping through a catalog, actually *talking* to someone about gear. Is this hyper-choice really progress?

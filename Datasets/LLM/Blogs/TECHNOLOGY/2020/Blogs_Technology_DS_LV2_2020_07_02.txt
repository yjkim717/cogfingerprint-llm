Remember those frantic 2020 days, endlessly scrolling for a distraction? I’d add items to my cart, then abandon it. Days later, a 10% discount code would arrive. It wasn’t just a coupon; it was a digital tap on the shoulder. A simple algorithm remembered me when I’d forgotten myself. In a year of isolation, that tiny, automated gesture felt surprisingly human—a little nudge saying, “Hey, we see you.”

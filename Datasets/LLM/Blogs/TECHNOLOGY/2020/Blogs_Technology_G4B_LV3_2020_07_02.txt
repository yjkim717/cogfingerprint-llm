**The Ghostly Discount Code**

Remember 2020? We were all glued to our laptops, trying to make *something* of online retail. I had a frustrating experience – a beautiful Shopify store, a perfect discount code, and then… forgotten! Customers would add everything to their cart, hit checkout, and *poof* – the discount vanished. It felt like a ghostly little thief, stealing potential sales and leaving a trail of frustrated customers. It highlighted how seamless the online experience needs to be; technology should *help*, not hinder.

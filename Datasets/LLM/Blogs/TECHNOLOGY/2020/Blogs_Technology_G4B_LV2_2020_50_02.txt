**Lost in Translation (and Loving It)**

Remember 2020? Zoom calls were *everything*. I was desperately trying to nail down my English accent, connecting with a native speaker from Canada through a language exchange program. It felt so strange, chatting through a screen, but honestly, it was a lifeline.  Those awkward video chats, the shared laughter – technology unexpectedly brought me closer to a better understanding of the language and, more importantly, a new friend.

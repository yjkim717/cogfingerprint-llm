**The Ghost in the App**

Remember 2020? Everything felt…digitally mediated. I was obsessing over stocks, specifically Rolls Royce on Trading212. The app showed a fantastic dividend yield, but then I’d cross-reference with financial sites, and it just didn’t match. It felt like a ghost in the system – a discrepancy that sparked a weird anxiety. Was the app lying? Were dividends actually being processed correctly? It highlighted how easily we trust these interfaces, and how vital it is to question the information we’re presented with, even when it’s glowing on our screens.

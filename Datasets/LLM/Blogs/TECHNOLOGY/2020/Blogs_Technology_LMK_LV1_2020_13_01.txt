"Lost in a Sea of Options: My Frustrating Canoe Hunt in 2020. I was excited to buy a canoe while traveling in southern Europe, but my Google search was hijacked by Alibaba results. As I scrolled through page after page of Chinese suppliers, I felt like I was drowning in a sea of options. In that moment, I realized that our reliance on technology can sometimes lead to more problems than solutions - and a good old-fashioned local shop might be just what we need."

**Pixel Problems & FreeBSD Frustrations**

It’s weird, right? You build this incredible network with pfSense, thinking you’ve conquered the digital world. Then, you discover a tiny, stubbornly blank LCD display on your Supermicro server. I spent a frustrating week in 2020 wrestling with FreeBSD, LCDproc, and a whole lot of online forums. Turns out, getting that display to show anything beyond a solid black was a surprisingly deep dive.  Sometimes, the biggest challenges aren’t the grand network design, but the little details.

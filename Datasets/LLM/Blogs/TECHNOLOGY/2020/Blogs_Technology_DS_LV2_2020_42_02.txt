It’s 2020, and I’m sitting here thinking about how technology has become our lifeline. Remember when video calls were just for formal meetings? Now they’re how I see my grandma’s smile, share a coffee with friends, and even attend yoga classes. It’s strange—these screens that once felt isolating are now stitching our lives back together. In a year of distance, tech quietly bridged the gaps, turning pixels into presence. Who knew our most human moments would be mediated by Wi-Fi?

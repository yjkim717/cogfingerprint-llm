## The Upgrade Dilemma: Does Newer *Always* Mean Better?

Remember that feeling when a new phone came out? The urge to upgrade, even if your current one *mostly* worked? I’m wrestling with that right now, but with a Honda Odyssey instead of a smartphone. Should I grab the slightly older, higher-mileage one for the savings, or bite the bullet and get the newer model? It’s a reminder that "new" doesn't always equal "better," especially when real-world value is on the line. What do *you* prioritize?

**Shiny Doesn’t Always Mean Better**

Remember when we all thought the ‘Hero Edition’ of games was the *ultimate* upgrade? I was really excited about Minecraft Dungeons’ Hero Edition back in 2020. It looked slick, right? But honestly, it felt a bit… underwhelming.  It was a nice polish, sure, but not a fundamentally different experience. 

It made me think about how we get caught up in the hype.  Do we really need the newest, fanciest version, or is the core game already good enough?  Value for money is important, absolutely, but sometimes the best games are the ones that just *work* – not the ones that try hardest to impress.

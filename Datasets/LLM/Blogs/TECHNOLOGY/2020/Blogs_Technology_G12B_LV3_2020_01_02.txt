## The Ghost in My Notifications

2020 has been a year of distance, hasn’t it? And for me, it amplified a heartbreak I thought I was moving past. Today was supposed to be our anniversary. Instead, I'm staring at a phone screen, haunted by the ghost of notifications that used to flood in. It’s a strange kind of loneliness – a digital echo of a relationship lost, reminding me of what isn't, and what likely never will be.

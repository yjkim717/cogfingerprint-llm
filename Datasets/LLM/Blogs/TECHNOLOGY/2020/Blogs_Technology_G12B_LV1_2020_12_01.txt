## The Algorithm and My Nonna's Stories

Remember when we thought everything would be instantly connected, perfectly translated by 2020? I certainly did. Now, I’m staring at a language learning app, trying to grasp my grandmother's Reggiano dialect – a far cry from Google Translate’s sterile Italian. It’s a humbling reminder that some things, the richest stories and deepest connections, still require human effort, a patient ear, and a little bit of heart. Technology helps, sure, but it can’t replace *that*.

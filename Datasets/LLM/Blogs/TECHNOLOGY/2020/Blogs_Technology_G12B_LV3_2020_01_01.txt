## The Unexpected Comfort of Disconnecting

Remember 2020? It felt like we were all glued to our screens. I was definitely guilty! Lately, though, I've been thinking about the joy of *not* being online. It's a strange feeling, almost like rediscovering a forgotten hobby. Putting my phone down, even for an hour, brings a surprising sense of calm. Maybe it's time we all explored a little digital "bundle" of offline moments.

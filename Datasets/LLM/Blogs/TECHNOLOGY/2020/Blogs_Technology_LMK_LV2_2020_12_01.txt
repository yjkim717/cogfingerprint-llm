"Lost in a Sea of Screens: My 2020 Digital Detox Journey. 
As 2020 unfolded, I found myself mindlessly scrolling through social media, a pandemic-induced habit. But with the world in lockdown, I realized I was trading depth for breadth. I took a digital detox, swapping screens for books and conversations. The result? A renewed sense of connection and a more meaningful online presence. I rediscovered the joy of presence, and I'm still reaping the benefits."

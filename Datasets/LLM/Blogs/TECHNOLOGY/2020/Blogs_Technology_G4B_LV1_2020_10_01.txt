**Remember When Everything Was…Slightly Less Connected?**

It feels weird looking back to 2020. Suddenly, everyone was glued to Zoom, and the idea of *not* being online felt… strange.  It was a frantic, beautiful mess, and a stark reminder that technology, for all its convenience, was suddenly *everything*.

## The Unexpected Comfort of Digital Coordinates

Remember 2020? Stuck inside, finding solace in…Minecraft? I did. Lately, I’ve been diving back into PvP modpacks, and it's wild how much a simple mod – displaying ping and coordinates on the screen – can impact the experience. It’s more than just gameplay; it’s a strange comfort, knowing exactly *where* you are, even in a digital world feeling increasingly chaotic.

## My Old Paperwhite & The Joy of Unexpected Discoveries

Remember that feeling of finding a perfectly good deal? I snagged a used 7th gen Kindle Paperwhite last fall – cellular too! – and I'm rediscovering the simple joy of reading. I was curious if Goodreads would play nice and, surprisingly, the built-in translator is a neat little bonus. It's a reminder that even older tech can offer unexpected delights.

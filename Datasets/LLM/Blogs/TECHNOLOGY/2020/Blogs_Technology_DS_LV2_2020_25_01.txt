Remember those frantic 2020 Zoom calls? My Chromebook, my lifeline, decided videos just wouldn't load. It was maddening! I’d bypass the school’s Lightspeed filter, but nothing. I even poked around ChromeOS developer tools, feeling like a hacker.

It hit me then: we were all so dependent on these little machines, and a single glitch could completely unravel our new virtual lives. That silent screen was a stark reminder of our fragile digital world, all from my kitchen table.

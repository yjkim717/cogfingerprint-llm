**Lost in Translation (and Kindles)**

Remember 2020? Everyone was glued to their Kindles, desperately seeking an escape. I was too, trying to devour a Spanish novel. My older Paperwhite – a trusty 7th gen – had this cool built-in translator, but I kept wrestling with it.  Goodreads was my go-to for recommendations, of course, but syncing the translation felt clunky. It highlighted the frustrating gap between wanting to read *everything* and actually *understanding* it.

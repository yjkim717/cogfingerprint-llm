Remember those early 2020 days, endlessly scrolling through 2b2t’s chaotic history? I kept thinking, what if we could build something more—a living archive, not just a list. So we built an interactive timeline, a place where every player could embed their own stories. The feedback from the community was incredible. Suddenly, it wasn't just my blog; it was our shared memory, a digital campfire where we all gathered to piece together the legacy of the oldest anarchy server in Minecraft.

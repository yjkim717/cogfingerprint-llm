## The Algorithm and My Anxious Heart

Remember 2020? Everything felt… uncertain. I was staring down the barrel of graduation and the job search felt like battling a complex algorithm. Every rejection email chipped away at my confidence. I spent weeks tweaking my resume, paralyzed by the fear it wouldn't be *enough*. Finally, I just *did* it. And you know what? The relief, the learning – it was worth pushing past that digital anxiety. It reminded me: humans, not algorithms, make the final decisions.

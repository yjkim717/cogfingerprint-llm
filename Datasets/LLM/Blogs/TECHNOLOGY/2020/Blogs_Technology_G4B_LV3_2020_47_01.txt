**The Unexpected Leak in My Digital Life**

Remember 2020? Everything felt…precarious. I was upgrading my entire home office setup – new monitor, fancy keyboard, the works. Then, a tiny trickle started behind the desk. Turns out, a loose cable and a little condensation were causing a slow, insidious leak. It wasn’t dramatic, but it mirrored that feeling of things slowly crumbling. 

Like a neglected bathtub with failing tiles, we all have digital “grout” – the connections and systems we rely on. Addressing those small issues *now* – updating software, securing passwords – felt crucial to prevent bigger problems down the line. It’s a reminder that even the newest tech needs a little care.

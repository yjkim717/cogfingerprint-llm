## The Facebook Ads Paradox I'm Still Wrapping My Head Around

Okay, so here's a weird thing I've noticed with Facebook Ads this year. You'd think higher click-through rates (CTR) always equal better results, right? Turns out, campaigns hovering around 1-2% are *crushing* it in terms of ROAS and CPA. It's a reminder that chasing vanity metrics isn't everything. Sometimes, a little mystery is part of the game.

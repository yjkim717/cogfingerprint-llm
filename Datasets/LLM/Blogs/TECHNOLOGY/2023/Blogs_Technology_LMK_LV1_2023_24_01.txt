As we navigate the complexities of 2023, it's hard not to wonder: what drove European militaries to dominate the global stage for centuries? Was it merely a product of circumstance, or was there something more at play? I believe it was a potent mix of innovative technology, strategic geo-politics, and a dash of historical luck. From the development of gunpowder to advancements in radar and drones, European powers consistently pushed the boundaries of military tech. This relentless pursuit of innovation allowed them to stay ahead of the curve, often tipping the scales in territorial conflicts and shaping the course of history.

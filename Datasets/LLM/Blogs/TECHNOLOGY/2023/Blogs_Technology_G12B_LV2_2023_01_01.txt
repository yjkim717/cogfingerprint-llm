

## The Quiet Comfort of a Watching Eye

It’s funny, isn’t it? Just a few years ago, the idea of cameras watching our homes felt like something out of a sci-fi movie. Now, I’m seriously considering adding a few. Not out of paranoia, but a quiet desire for peace of mind. Knowing I can check in, see the porch light on, the dog asleep… it's a strangely comforting feeling in this ever-changing world.

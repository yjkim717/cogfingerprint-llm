As I crouched my rogue before yet another intricate lock in Baldur’s Gate 3, I couldn't help but draw a parallel to my own digital life. In 2023, we're all constantly picking locks—whether it's a forgotten password, a two-factor authentication prompt, or a tricky software bug. That same dexterity and sleight of hand, both in-game and out, is about patiently solving puzzles to access what’s hidden. It’s a quiet, satisfying skill that reminds me that some of the best rewards require a little finesse to uncover.

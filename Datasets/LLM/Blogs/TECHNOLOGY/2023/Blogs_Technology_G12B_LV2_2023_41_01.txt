## The Algorithm Knows My Disappointment

It's funny how technology, designed to connect us, can sometimes highlight our own little frustrations. I spent a good chunk of last night chasing a rare card in a game, burning through raid tickets with zero luck. It felt… familiar. Like endlessly scrolling, hoping for *that* post, *that* recommendation. We're all chasing something, aren’t we? And sometimes, the algorithm just laughs.

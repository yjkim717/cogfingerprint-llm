As we navigate the digital landscape of 2023, the themes of Thomas Pynchon's 'Gravity's Rainbow' remain eerily relevant. The novel's exploration of technology's darker side, intertwined with sex and the power of images, feels prophetic. Like J.G. Ballard's 'The Atrocity Exhibition', it highlights the ways in which technology can both captivate and control us, raising important questions about our relationship with the digital world.

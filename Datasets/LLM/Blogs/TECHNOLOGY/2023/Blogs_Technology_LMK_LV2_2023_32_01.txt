"Lessons from a Faulty UPS: The Dangers of DIY Electronics"

As I watched a recent YouTube video showcasing a teardown of a faulty APC UPS unit, I was struck by the potential risks of DIY electronics. The issue? Copper wires were soldered to an aluminum transformer core, a recipe for disaster. In 2023, it's surprising that such a fundamental mistake can still occur. This incident serves as a cautionary tale for tech enthusiasts and professionals alike, highlighting the importance of electrical safety and proper craftsmanship. It's a reminder to always double-check our work and respect the intricacies of electronics.

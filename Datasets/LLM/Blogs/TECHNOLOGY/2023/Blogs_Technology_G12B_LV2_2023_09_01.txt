## That Feeling of Inevitability Online

Anyone else ever feel like some online games are just… decided early on? I was playing "Capture the Object" in Gunsofboom the other day, and it hit me: unless you're way ahead, a comeback feels impossible. It’s a weird echo of life, isn't it? Sometimes, momentum just carries you, or it doesn’t. 2023 and still pondering digital parallels to reality!

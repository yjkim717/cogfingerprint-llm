## The Allure of the Shortcut

I used to be all about chasing those shortcuts. Stack the buffs, optimize the flow, shave off every possible second. But I’ve noticed something: the satisfaction isn't really there. Maybe it’s just me, but I’m leaning into the slower burn now. The deliberate, steady climb. Because ultimately, isn't the journey more valuable than just reaching the destination quickly? What do you think?

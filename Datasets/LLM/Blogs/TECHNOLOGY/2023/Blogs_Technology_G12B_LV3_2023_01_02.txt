## The Algorithm and the Unexpected Detour

Isn't it funny how technology, designed to streamline our lives, can sometimes create new frustrations? Lately, I've been thinking about navigation apps. They promise the fastest route, yet a sudden road closure or unexpected construction can send you spiraling down a maze of backstreets – much like those collection carts blocking alleys in Lethbridge, I read online! It’s a reminder that even the smartest systems can't predict everything.

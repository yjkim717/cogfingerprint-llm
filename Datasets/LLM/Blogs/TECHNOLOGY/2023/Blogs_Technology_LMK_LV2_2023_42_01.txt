"Revisiting the Laptop Scene: Flexibility on a Budget"

As I scoured the market for an affordable laptop in 2023, I found myself torn between ChromeOS Flex and traditional laptops. I wanted a device with a sturdy hinge and decent performance. The Asus Vivobook and Lenovo Duet caught my eye, offering alternatives to Chromebooks. I'm now exploring non-Chromebook options that can run ChromeOS Flex, seeking the perfect blend of flexibility and affordability.

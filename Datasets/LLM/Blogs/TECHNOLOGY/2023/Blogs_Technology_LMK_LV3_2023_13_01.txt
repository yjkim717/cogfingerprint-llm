"Disconnecting to Reconnect: My Yukon Canoe Trip Revelation". I recently returned from a Yukon canoeing adventure with Whitehorse outfitters, and it was a digital detox like no other. As I paddled down the serene river, I realized that leaving behind technology allowed me to truly connect with nature and my family. In 2023, we're constantly connected, but this trip taught me that sometimes, disconnecting is the key to a more meaningful outdoor adventure.

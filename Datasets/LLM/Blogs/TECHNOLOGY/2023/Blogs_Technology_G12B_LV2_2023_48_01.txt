## The Quiet Hum of Things Going Wrong (And Why I Miss Simple Websites)

Remember when the internet felt… simpler? Just pages and links, mostly? Lately, I've been feeling a weird disconnect online. Things *look* fine, but there’s this subtle feeling of something off, like a slightly out-of-tune note. 

I was browsing for a gift the other day, and kept getting inexplicably redirected. It was frustrating, and honestly, a little unsettling. It got me thinking about how much complexity we’ve layered on top of the basic idea of sharing information. CDNs, intricate backend systems, acquisition dramas… it’s a lot!

I read something on Reddit the other day about a potential hiccup with a recent acquisition and how it might be impacting online product links. It’s probably nothing huge, but it’s a reminder that even the things we take for granted – a straightforward link to a product – rely on a massive, interconnected web of technology.

Sometimes, I just crave the feeling of a website that *just works*, without the invisible layers of optimization and potential points of failure. Anyone else feeling this lately?

## The Unexpected Nostalgia of a Hardware Swap

It's funny how technology moves. I saw someone selling an older i7-6700, ASRock H110M-ITX board, and 16GB of DDR3 – a mini-ITX combo! It sparked a wave of nostalgia. Remember when those specs felt cutting-edge? Now, it’s a reminder of how far we’ve come, and a little piece of computing history up for grabs.

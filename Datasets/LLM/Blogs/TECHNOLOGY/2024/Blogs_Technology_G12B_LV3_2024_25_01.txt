## Password Policies: When Rules Collide (and What It Means for Us)

Ever feel like tech rules are a bit…confusing? I was wrestling with it just yesterday! Apparently, in Windows domains, a broad Group Policy Object (GPO) and a more specific "fine-grained" password policy can clash on minimum lengths.

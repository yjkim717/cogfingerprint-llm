>## The Missing Miles & the Promise of Seamless

It’s 2024, and we're *supposed* to be living in a world of frictionless financial integration. Revolut's RevPoints sounded amazing – earn points on spending, redeem for… well, almost anything! But I noticed something glaringly absent: Lufthansa miles. As a frequent flyer, it felt like a missed opportunity. Are we still chasing loyalty programs, or is this truly the future of rewards? It leaves me wondering what else is still just out of reach.

It’s 2024, and I’ve been thinking a lot about Perplexity AI. It’s incredible for quick answers, but I worry. If these platforms summarize the entire web, how will my favorite independent blogs and journalists keep the lights on without ad revenue? We can’t let convenience starve the diverse voices we rely on, pushing us toward a few, polished, proprietary sources. What happens to truth when only the loudest, best-funded platforms remain?

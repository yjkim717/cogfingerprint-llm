There I was, prototyping a fullstack NextJS app, marveling at how seamlessly server logic integrated into my components. It felt like magic—until deployment day. Suddenly, scaling became a puzzle, monitoring felt opaque, and I realized my frontend and backend were holding hands a bit too tightly. In 2024, this convenience is a double-edged sword: rapid development can lead to tangled architectures. It’s a lesson in balancing speed with sustainability, ensuring our digital creations can grow without breaking.

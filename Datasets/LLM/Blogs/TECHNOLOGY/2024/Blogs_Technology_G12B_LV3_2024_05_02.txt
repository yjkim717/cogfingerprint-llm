## The Unexpected Calm of Disconnecting Before Dawn

There's something uniquely stressful about those pre-dawn travel scrambles, isn't there? A recent Reddit thread reminded me – someone wrestling with a 6am flight from Ithaca! It got me thinking about our constant connectivity. In 2024, we're *always* on. But maybe, just maybe, intentionally disconnecting a little before a big journey – silencing notifications, putting the phone down – offers a surprising pocket of calm. Like that 5am arrival time, it’s a small investment in a smoother start.

It’s funny—in 2024, we’re surrounded by AI that can predict our next word, yet I still crave a little unpredictability. It reminds me of that overpowered checkered deck in Balatro: too much consistency made the game feel hollow. Sometimes, technology smooths out the friction so much that we lose the thrill of the climb. Maybe the bugs, the glitches, the happy accidents are what keep our digital journeys human.

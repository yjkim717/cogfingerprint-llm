## Remember Dial-Up? Seriously.

It's wild, isn't it? We stream 4K video now, effortlessly, yet I still occasionally catch myself thinking, "Do I need to disconnect so someone else can use the internet?" Growing up with dial-up, that screeching symphony of connection, feels like a lifetime ago. It fostered a patience we seem to have lost. Sometimes I wonder if that deliberate slowness, that waiting, actually made us appreciate the digital world a *little* more. Anyone else feel that pang of nostalgia?

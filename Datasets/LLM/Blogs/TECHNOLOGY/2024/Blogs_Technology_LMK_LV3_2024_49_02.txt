As we navigate 2024, I'm reminded of a recent Reddit post that got me thinking - is our enthusiasm for technology putting our digital wellbeing at risk? Just like spinning a dog in the air while playing tug-of-war, our tech habits can be both fun and hazardous. Are we prioritizing our online safety and pet care for our mental health? Let's take a step back and assess our screen time, just as we would consider a dog's playtime, to ensure we're using technology in a way that promotes, rather than hinders, our overall welfare.

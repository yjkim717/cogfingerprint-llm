## Digital Ghosts and Familiar Faces

It’s funny how nostalgia works, isn't it? Lately, I've been down a rabbit hole thinking about *Mega Man X* and a Reddit thread proposing human designs for those robotic heroes. It got me pondering: do our digital worlds imprint on us, shaping archetypes we recognize even in unexpected places? Like, is Sigma just a very complex, zero-sum game boss, or a digital echo of something…familiar?

Remember when we thought flying cars were just around the corner? It’s 2024, and instead, my phone just reminded me to drink water. We’ve traded sci-fi dreams for smart fridges that shame us for eating leftovers at 2 a.m.

I love technology, but sometimes I miss the simplicity of just being present. The constant pings and updates pull us in so many directions. Maybe the real innovation isn’t the next gadget, but learning when to power down and just be.

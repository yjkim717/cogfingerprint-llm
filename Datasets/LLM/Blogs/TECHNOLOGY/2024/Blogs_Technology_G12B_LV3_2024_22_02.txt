Okay, so you're diving into Rubrik, a powerful data management platform – exciting! But sometimes, like when setting up a new cluster, you hit a snag. I remember that feeling! Sometimes, the cleanest path forward is a factory reset on a node, essentially a full restart of the cluster configuration.

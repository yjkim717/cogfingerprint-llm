## The Ghosts in My Digital Toolkit

It's funny how echoes linger in tech. I was just poking around in an old game, searching for a specific, fleeting voice line – a character referencing a tough year. It reminded me of how quickly digital experiences fade, even ones we cherished. Now, in 2024, so much has shifted. Are those little digital moments, those specific lines and interactions, truly lost, or just buried deep within the code’s memory?

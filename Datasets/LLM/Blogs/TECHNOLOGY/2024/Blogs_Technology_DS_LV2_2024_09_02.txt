It’s 2024, and I’ve been thinking a lot about how quickly the tech landscape shifts—especially in gaming. I remember when Fortnite was the undisputed king, and every pro player seemed set for life. But then Valorant came along, and suddenly, the rules changed.

Watching so many talented players struggle to make that leap was eye-opening. It wasn’t just about skill; it was about adapting to a whole new world. Most ended up streaming instead of competing, finding their niche in building communities rather than chasing trophies.

It reminds me that in tech—and in life—pivoting isn’t failure. It’s survival. Whether it’s AI, VR, or the next big thing, staying flexible keeps us in the game. Here’s to embracing change, one click at a time.

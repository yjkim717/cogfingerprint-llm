**The Snowstorm of Notifications**

It’s funny, isn’t it? We’re surrounded by tech designed to *connect* us, but sometimes it feels like it’s just… overwhelming. Like that first, insistent snow notification in January – do I *really* need to know it’s -15 degrees out?  I found myself obsessing over a neighbor’s un-shoveled sidewalk, mirroring that anxious feeling of wondering when it’s “too late” to say something.  Maybe we need a digital reset, a way to prioritize what truly matters.

**The Ghost in the Smart Fridge**

It’s 2024, and my fridge just suggested a recipe for my wilting kale. A year ago, I’d have been amazed. Now, it just feels… intrusive. This tech was meant to simplify my life, but it’s created a new chore: managing the manager. I spend more time tweaking privacy settings and dismissing notifications than I save on grocery trips. The convenience is real, but so is the quiet hum of a system that’s always watching, always calculating. I miss the silence of my old, dumb appliance.

## The Ghosts in My Old DVD Collection

Remember DVDs? Dusting off a box the other day, I was flooded with memories – movie nights, quirky packaging, even briefly considering using cereal boxes to bundle them! It’s wild how quickly physical media faded. Now, everything’s streaming. It’s convenient, sure, but I miss the tangible connection, the little ritual of popping in a disc. A digital ghost of a simpler time, perhaps?

## The Algorithm of My Life (and Why It Feels a Little Off Lately)

It’s wild to think we’re already halfway through 2024. Remember when the idea of AI art felt like science fiction? Now it's… everywhere. And honestly, sometimes it’s a bit overwhelming. Lately, I've been thinking about how much our lives are governed by algorithms – from the playlists curated just for us to the news feeds that shape our perspectives. 

It’s fascinating, sure. But I also miss the serendipity of stumbling upon something unexpected. Like, discovering a new favorite band on the radio, not because some algorithm *told* me I'd like them. Maybe I need to intentionally introduce a little chaos into my digital routine. Anyone else feeling this?

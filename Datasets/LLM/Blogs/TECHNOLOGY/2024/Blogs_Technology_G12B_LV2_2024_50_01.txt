## The Echo of Nostalgia in My Smart Home

It's funny, isn't it? We chase seamless technology in 2024, voice assistants anticipating our every need. Yet, I find myself oddly drawn to the *imperfection* of older gadgets. Just listening to Bats for Lashes’ "Laura" – that gorgeous, swirling reverb – made me think about it. There’s a warmth, a human touch, often lost in the sterile efficiency of our smart homes. Maybe embracing a little delightful glitch is the key to feeling truly connected.

## The Echo of a First Phone

Remember that first flip phone? Mine was a Motorola Razr, a sleek, silver brick I practically cherished in 2007. It wasn’t about endless apps or streaming; it was about *connection*. A simple call, a text message – it felt momentous. Now, staring at my overflowing smartphone, I sometimes miss that focused simplicity. We’re so tethered, so bombarded. It makes me wonder: are we truly more connected, or just… louder? It's a strange echo of a quieter time.

**The Quiet Worry About Perfect Answers**

Remember when searching the web felt… messy? A delightful tangle of links and opinions? Lately, it feels like we’re being gently nudged towards something cleaner, more curated – thanks to AI tools like Perplexity. It’s incredibly useful, honestly. But I’m starting to wonder if this ease comes at a cost. 

If AI can deliver perfectly synthesized answers, what happens to the serendipity of stumbling upon a brilliant, slightly flawed, original thought? And what about the websites that built communities around sharing those very imperfections?  It’s a small worry, maybe, but a big one for the future of online content and how we *find* things in 2024.

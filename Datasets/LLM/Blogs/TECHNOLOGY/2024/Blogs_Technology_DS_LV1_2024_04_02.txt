**The Real Cost of Cashing Out**

It hit me yesterday as I went to sell a single bitcoin: the final step is still the hardest. In 2024, we talk about global adoption and digital borders, but my simple goal—turning 1 BTC into spendable GBP—felt like navigating a maze.

I wasn't just looking for the best exchange rate; I was hunting for the most cost-effective path, the one with the lowest transaction fees. Because if we want this technology to truly go mainstream, it can't just be a high-tech vault. The journey from blockchain to bank account needs to be as simple and cheap as sending an email. We’ve solved the "store of value" part, but the "medium of exchange" part? We're still getting there.

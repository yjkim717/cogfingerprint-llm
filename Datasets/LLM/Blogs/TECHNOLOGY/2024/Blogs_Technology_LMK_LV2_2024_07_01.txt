As I scroll through my social media feeds in 2024, I'm struck by the overwhelming presence of sustainable clothing brands. Many of these eco-friendly labels have built their marketing strategies around Facebook ads, but I've noticed some are starting to diversify. One brand caught my eye, seeking alternative channels to drive organic brand searches. It's no secret that relying too heavily on paid advertising can be costly and unsustainable in the long run.

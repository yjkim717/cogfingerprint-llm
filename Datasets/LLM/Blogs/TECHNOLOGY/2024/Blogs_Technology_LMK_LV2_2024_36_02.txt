"Boosting GIMP on Linux: A Graphics Card Conundrum (2024)"

As I upgraded my Linux machine, a colleague suggested I prioritize a powerful graphics card for smoother GIMP performance. But others countered that more RAM was the real bottleneck. I was torn. In 2024, with demanding image editing tasks, I realized that both were crucial. A strong graphics card accelerates rendering, while ample RAM ensures seamless multitasking. I opted for a balanced upgrade, and my GIMP workflow transformed. It was a valuable lesson in optimizing my Linux setup for creative pursuits.

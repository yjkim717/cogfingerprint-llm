## The Data Privacy Tightrope in the Age of LLMs

It's 2024, and the power of Large Language Models is undeniable. But lately, I've been wrestling with a question: how do we leverage these incredible tools while safeguarding our data? I'm searching for a cloud LLM that lets me analyze my documents – a thousand public ones, in my case – without the nagging worry of my queries being used to train the model further.

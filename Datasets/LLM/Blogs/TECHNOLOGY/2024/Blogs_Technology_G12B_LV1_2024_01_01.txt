## The Unexpected Kindness of Algorithmic Reminders

It’s funny, isn’t it? How technology, often blamed for isolating us, can sometimes nudge us back towards connection. This winter, my smart assistant started reminding me to check in on my elderly neighbor, based on the gloomy weather reports. It felt…oddly thoughtful. A little digital prompting led to a warm cup of tea and a lovely chat. Proof that even algorithms can spark a little human kindness.

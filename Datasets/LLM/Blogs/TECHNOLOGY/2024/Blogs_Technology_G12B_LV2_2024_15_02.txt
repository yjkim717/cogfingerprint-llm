## The Unexpected Comfort of Digital Glitches

Isn't it funny how we chase seamless tech experiences? Yet, sometimes, the little hiccups are what stick with you. I was wrestling with Fallout 4 mods last night – America Rising, specifically – and battling constant instability despite all the fixes. It felt frustrating, sure, but then I realized: these quirks, these digital stumbles, remind me technology isn’t perfect. It’s *built* by humans, and that’s a strangely comforting thought in our hyper-connected, always-on world of 2024. A reminder to embrace the imperfect.

"LeetCoding: A Productivity Paradox"

As I sit here in 2024, reflecting on my coding journey, I'm reminded of the countless hours I've spent on LeetCode. The rush of solving a tricky problem is exhilarating, yet the sheer time invested often leaves me wondering if I'm being productive or just productive-looking. Have I made progress or just spun my wheels? As I navigate this dichotomy, I'm forced to confront my relationship with technology and ask: what's the true cost of honing my skills in a world where time is money?

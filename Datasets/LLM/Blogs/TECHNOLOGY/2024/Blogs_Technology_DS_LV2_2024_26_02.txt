It’s funny how a single piece of hardware can shift your entire build vision. Just last week, I was all set on a sleek ITX setup—compact, efficient, and minimalist. Then, this gently used RX 5600 came along, with a cooler so beefy it felt like overkill. I couldn’t resist the deal on the hardware swap, but now it’s sitting here, a beautiful beast that just doesn’t fit the tiny case I had in mind.

Sometimes, tech reminds us that our best-laid plans need room to breathe—or in this case, a bigger chassis. Here’s to embracing the unexpected in 2024.

## The Ghosts of Missed Deals

Remember that feeling? Scrolling, searching, *needing* that game, only to see it vanish from the sale just as you turned away? It's a uniquely modern frustration, isn't it? In 2021, with endless digital storefronts, it's easy to miss out. I’ve started using price trackers – a little digital guardian angel preventing those phantom "what ifs" from haunting my wallet. Anyone else relate?

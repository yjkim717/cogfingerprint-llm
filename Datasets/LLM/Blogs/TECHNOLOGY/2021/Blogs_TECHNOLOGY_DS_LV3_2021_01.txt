Remember that 2021 gaming obsession, tweaking custom resolutions for that perfect edge? I spent hours converting widths and heights into hex values, convinced I’d cracked the code. Then I noticed it: 7680x4320 and 3840x2160 gave the same hex result. The math was identical—just scaled. It hit me: sometimes, in tech and life, different paths can lead to the same destination.

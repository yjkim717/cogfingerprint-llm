## The Agony of the Missed Deal

It's a uniquely modern frustration, isn’t it? You've been eyeing that indie RPG for months, patiently waiting for a sale. Then, *bam!* A flash deal pops up, but you missed it. The feeling of digital disappointment is real. I’ve been on the hunt for a good price alert system for video games – something that doesn’t just notify me, but *remembers* what I want. It feels silly, but in a world overflowing with fleeting deals, a little tech to help you snag that perfect price feels essential. Anyone else feel me?

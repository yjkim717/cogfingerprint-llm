## The Unexpected Lesson From a Returned Sweater

Remember the early days of online shopping? It still feels relatively new, doesn't it? Recently, I had a return from the UK – a slightly-too-big sweater. The customs fees were a *shock*. Then, I stumbled upon a Reddit tip: mark the package "returns" on the invoice. It worked! A small tech tweak saved me a surprising amount. It’s a reminder that even in our digital world, clever solutions can still pop up in unexpected places.

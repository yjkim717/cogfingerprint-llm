**Lost in the Numbers: Why Games Need to Speak *Our* Language**

Remember that feeling? You’re totally engrossed in a game, maybe Baldur’s Gate 3, and then… *bam*. A complicated dice roll, a confusing proficiency bonus, and suddenly you’re staring blankly at the screen. It’s frustrating, right? 

I was there in 2021, wrestling with these mechanics. It felt like the game was speaking a language I hadn’t learned.  We need better tutorials – not just walls of text, but actually *showing* us. And honestly, a more intuitive feedback system would have made all the difference. Tech should enhance, not hinder, our enjoyment.

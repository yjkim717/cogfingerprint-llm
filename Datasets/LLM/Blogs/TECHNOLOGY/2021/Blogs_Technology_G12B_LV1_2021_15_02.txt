## The Endless Scroll and the ERP Rabbit Hole

It's 2021, and I’m deep in the trenches of a university project. We’re exploring ERP systems – specifically, SAP Business ByDesign. Simple enough, right? Except finding straightforward pricing and module breakdowns online feels like searching for a unicorn. Hours spent clicking links, wading through dense documentation… it’s a frustrating reminder that even with all this digital access, clarity isn't always guaranteed. Makes you wonder if the information overload is, ironically, hindering understanding.

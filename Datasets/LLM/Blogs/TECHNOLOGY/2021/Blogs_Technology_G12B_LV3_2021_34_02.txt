## My Digital Detox & Rediscovering Calm in 2021

Remember 2021? It felt like we were *all* glued to our screens. I was definitely guilty! Lately, I've been experimenting with mindful moments – even just five minutes of meditation a day. It sounds simple, but honestly, it’s helped quiet the constant buzz of notifications and eased my anxiety. It's a small shift, but feeling more relaxed amidst the digital noise is a huge win.

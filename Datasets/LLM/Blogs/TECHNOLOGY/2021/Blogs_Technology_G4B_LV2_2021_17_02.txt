I’ve been wrestling with a Raspberry Pi Zero, trying to wrangle a bunch of WS2812B strips into a cohesive light show, and honestly, it’s been… rewarding. There’s something incredibly satisfying about taking a pile of bits and creating something that *glows*. 

Right now, I’m obsessing over grounding – a surprisingly complex topic! – and figuring out the best way to mount everything securely. I’ve even invested in an aluminium plate to help with heat dissipation – because let’s be real, these LEDs get warm.

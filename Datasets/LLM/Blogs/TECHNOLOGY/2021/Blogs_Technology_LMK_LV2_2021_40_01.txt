"Finding the Perfect Tiling WM: My Journey to a More Efficient Linux Experience. As a Linux enthusiast in 2021, I was on the hunt for a modern tiling window manager that could simplify my workflow. I turned to dwm, a minimalist WM that requires manual configuration. While it offered flexibility, I found myself missing a built-in status bar to display essential information like the system clock. After some trial and error, I discovered a few clever workarounds to create a seamless experience. In this post, I'll share my findings and the lessons I learned along the way."

## Is Anyone *Really* Here? A Late-Night Scroll and a Strange Feeling

It’s late, and I’m doing that thing we all do – endlessly scrolling. Instagram, Twitter, Reddit… a highlight reel of everyone else’s “best” lives. And tonight, it hit me with a weird wave of disconnect. 

We curate these online personas, right? Carefully chosen photos, witty captions, thoughts strategically shared. But are we *really* presenting ourselves? Or are we building characters, like crafting a video game avatar? 

I saw a thread today about gender-swapped Mortal Kombat characters – wild, imaginative stuff. It made me think: aren't we all, to some degree, playing a role online? It’s not necessarily a bad thing, but I wonder if we ever pause to consider who we're *not* showing, and what's being lost in translation. Just a random thought, fueled by late-night internet wanderings. Anyone else feel this sometimes?

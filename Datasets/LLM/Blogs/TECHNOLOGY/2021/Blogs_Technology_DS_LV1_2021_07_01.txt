Back in 2021, I was wrestling with international returns for my small e-commerce shop, convinced I’d be stuck paying customs fees forever. Then I discovered the power of proper documentation. By ensuring every return included a clear commercial invoice and proof of original shipment, the customs puzzle solved itself. It wasn’t a tech glitch or a policy loophole—just good old-fashioned paperwork. Sometimes the simplest solutions hide in plain sight, saving us from overcomplicating things with tech.

"Gaming Frustration: When Teamwork Fails" - I've spent countless hours playing Guns of Boom in 2021, but nothing drains my enthusiasm faster than teammates who don't pull their weight. Capturing objectives is key, yet some players seem to zone out, leaving others to do all the work. Where's the strategy? Effective zone control and coordination are crucial in KOTH gameplay, but when teammates fail to contribute, frustration takes over, and the game loses its thrill.

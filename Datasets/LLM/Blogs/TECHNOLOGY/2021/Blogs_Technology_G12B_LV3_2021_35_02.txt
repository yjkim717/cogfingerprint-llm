## Lost in the Algorithm? Finding Myself in a Digital World

Remember reading *Invisible Man* by Ralph Ellison? It’s been swirling around in my head lately, especially with everything going on. It struck me – aren't we all, in a way, experiencing a modern version of that feeling of invisibility? Scrolling through endless feeds, curated realities, and targeted ads, it’s easy to feel like just another data point, lost in the algorithm.

2021 has been a year of intense reflection, and for me, it’s led to questioning how much of my identity is being shaped *by* technology, rather than *in spite* of it. Are we building walls of personalized content that keep us from truly seeing – and connecting with – the messy, beautiful, contradictory world around us? Maybe it's time to log off and look up.

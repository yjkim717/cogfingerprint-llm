"Flipping the Script: Exploring Mortal Kombat's Characters through a Different Lens"

In 2021, I stumbled upon an intriguing Reddit post that challenged my perception of the iconic Mortal Kombat characters. The user creatively reimagined the fighters with inverted sexuality, swapping their genders and sparking a fascinating discussion. This thought-provoking exercise got me thinking about the versatility of characters in gaming and how a simple twist can breathe new life into familiar figures, inviting us to reevaluate our assumptions and biases.

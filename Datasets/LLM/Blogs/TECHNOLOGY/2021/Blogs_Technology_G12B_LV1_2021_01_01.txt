
## The Homescreen Hustle: Why I'm Obsessed with App Search Widgets

Okay, I’ll admit it, I’m a little chaotic. My phone’s app drawer is a *landfill*. Finding what I need sometimes feels like an archaeological dig. Then I discovered homescreen app search widgets. Seriously, a game changer! Now, a quick tap and I’m instantly locating that obscure note-taking app I haven’t used in six months. It’s brought a surprising amount of calm to my digital life – and made me wonder, are we all just battling app clutter?

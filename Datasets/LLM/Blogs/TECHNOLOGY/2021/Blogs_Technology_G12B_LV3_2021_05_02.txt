## The Weight of Algorithms: Thinking About What We Don't See

It’s 2021, and algorithms are woven into *everything*. We scroll, we click, we’re shown… something. Lately, I’ve been wrestling with a disquieting thought: how much are these invisible forces shaping our understanding of the world? Seeing a master’s thesis on euthanasia – a deeply complex ethical debate – become a video, sparked something. It highlighted how even challenging topics are being mediated, potentially simplified, and presented through a lens we may not fully recognize. Are we truly engaging with nuance, or just consuming carefully curated perspectives? It’s a question I think we all need to be asking ourselves.

**The Ghosts of Yosemite**

It’s weird, isn’t it? I was helping a friend this week wrestle with an old Macbook – a Yosemite (10.10.5!) machine. He desperately wanted to upgrade to macOS 10.11, but the App Store just wouldn’t cooperate. It felt like trying to drag a stubborn ghost into the 21st century.  You don’t see these systems around much anymore, and it really highlighted how far Apple – and tech in general – has come.  Still, there's a certain nostalgia for those simpler days, right?

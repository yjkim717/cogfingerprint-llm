**The Azure File Share Puzzle**

Remember 2021? I was wrestling with a particularly thorny problem: trying to centrally manage Azure file shares with Group Policy. The idea of using GPO for drive mapping and enforcing NTFS permissions felt brilliant, but the authentication – especially with AzureAD – threw me for a loop. It highlighted just how complex hybrid environments could be.  Ultimately, while GPO isn't a silver bullet, it’s a piece of the puzzle when combined with the right Azure tools.  It’s a reminder that sometimes the most elegant solutions require a bit of creative troubleshooting!

"Rolling with Uncertainty: How D&D's Magic 8-Ball Inspired My Warlock Build. In 2021, I dove back into Dungeons and Dragons, experimenting with unique character builds. One peculiar combination caught my attention - pairing a Warlock with a Wizard and incorporating a Magic 8-Ball for decision-making. This unorthodox approach not only added a fun twist to gameplay but also mirrored real-life uncertainty, making me reflect on how we navigate unpredictability in our own lives."

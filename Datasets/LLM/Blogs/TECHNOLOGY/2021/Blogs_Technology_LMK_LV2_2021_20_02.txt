"Uninvited Digital Makeovers: When Tech Surprises Us". I still recall the 2021 news about smart home devices getting hacked. It hit me - just like Belle's shocked reaction to the Beast's surprise library in Beauty and the Beast. We love tech for convenience, but what happens when it starts to feel invasive? Are we comfortable with our digital spaces being transformed without consent? This got me thinking about the fine line between tech's helpfulness and its potential to invade our personal spaces.

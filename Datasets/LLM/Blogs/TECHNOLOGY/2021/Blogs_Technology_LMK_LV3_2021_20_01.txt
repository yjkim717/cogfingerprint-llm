"The DMV Debacle: A Digital Identity Crisis". In 2021, I witnessed firsthand how a bureaucratic error can snowball into a nightmare. A simple mix-up at the DMV led to my identity being tied to a stranger's car - and their bad driving habits. Suddenly, I was on the receiving end of a flurry of traffic tickets. It was a frustrating wake-up call to the limitations of our digital systems. As we increasingly rely on technology to manage our lives, it's clear that even the most seemingly straightforward processes can go awry, with chaotic consequences.

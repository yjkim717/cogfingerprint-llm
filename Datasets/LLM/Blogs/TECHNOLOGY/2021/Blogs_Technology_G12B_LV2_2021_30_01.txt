## The Hum of Progress, and a Little Bit of Longing

Remember the thrill of dial-up? The screech, the promise of connection? 2021 feels a million miles away from that now, doesn't it? We're swimming in bandwidth, instant everything. Sometimes, though, I find myself missing that patient anticipation. There was a ritual to it, a focused waiting that feels lost in this hyper-connected world. A quiet hum, before the digital flood.

That dizzying rush when my penny stock picks shot up 200% last month—it felt like winning the lottery. But 2021’s meme-stock chaos had me wary. Were these genuine gains or just another pump-and-dump scheme fueled by online hype? I replayed past losses in my head, the sharp rises and brutal crashes.

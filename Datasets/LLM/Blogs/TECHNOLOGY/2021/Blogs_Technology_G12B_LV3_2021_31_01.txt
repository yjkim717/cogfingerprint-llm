## The Allure of the Tentacle Robot (and My Latest Obsession)

Okay, I'll admit it. I've been completely consumed by the idea of building a tentacle robot. Seriously! It started as a late-night rabbit hole dive into soft robotics, and now I'm sketching designs fueled by copious amounts of coffee. The goal? A pneumatic, air-powered arm that can grip and retract – think octopus, but engineered.

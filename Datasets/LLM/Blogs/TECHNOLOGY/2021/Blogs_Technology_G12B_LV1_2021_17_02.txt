## The Grounding of My Ambition (and a Few LEDs)

I know, grounding seems basic. But when you’re dealing with a boost converter, a logic level shifter (because the Pi's 3.3V just *wasn't* cutting it for the WS2812B LEDs), and the delicate dance of electronics, it's suddenly not so straightforward. I’d been wrestling with whether to connect the DC negative to earth, and honestly, the internet was a mixed bag of opinions.

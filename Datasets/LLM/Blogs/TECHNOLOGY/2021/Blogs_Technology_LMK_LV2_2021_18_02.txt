"Stuck in the Past: My MacBook Upgrade Struggle" - As I stared at my trusty MacBook in 2021, I realized it was still running Yosemite. I wanted to upgrade to El Capitan, but the App Store came up empty. Hours of searching later, I discovered that Apple's 'hidden' update archives were the answer. Now, my MacBook's newfound speed is a testament to the power of perseverance - and a little tech know-how.

## When Tech Gets Too Real

Remember that feeling of a fantasy being perfectly realized? A Reddit post recently struck a chord: a woman felt strangely unsettled when her partner built her a *Beauty and the Beast*-style library, a dream she’d cherished. It made me think about how technology can sometimes blur the lines. We build our ideal digital worlds, then find reality catching up – and it’s not always as magical as we imagined. A little mystery can be nice, right?

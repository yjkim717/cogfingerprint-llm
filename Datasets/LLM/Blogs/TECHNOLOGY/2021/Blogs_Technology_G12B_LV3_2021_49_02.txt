## Does Your Tech Look as Polished as You Are?

Remember when everyone was obsessed with perfectly curated Instagram feeds? Now, it feels like our *digital* presence is just as important as our physical one. In 2021, with remote work and video calls the norm, our laptops, messy desktops, and chaotic browser tabs are constantly on display. It got me thinking: are we projecting professionalism with our tech, or are we unintentionally sending a different message? Maybe a digital declutter is in order!

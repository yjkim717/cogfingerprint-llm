You know, sometimes I wonder if our smart devices are studying us more than we’re using them. Back in 2021, I’d ask my home assistant for the weather, and it felt like it knew me—too well. It’d suggest playlists based on my mood, remind me of anniversaries I’d almost forgotten. It wasn’t just helpful; it was eerily perceptive. That’s when it hit me: we’re not just training our tech. In its quiet, algorithmic way, it’s training us right back.

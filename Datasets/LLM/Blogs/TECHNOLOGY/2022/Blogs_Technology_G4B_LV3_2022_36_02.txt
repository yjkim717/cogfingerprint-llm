I spent a ridiculous amount of time, scouring forums, reinstalling, praying to the tech gods – you name it. It felt like staring into a digital void, utterly unable to access a simple video. 

Honestly, it was more frustrating than it should have been. It reminded me that even in 2022, with all our supposed tech savviness, things can still go sideways.  Sometimes, the simplest connections – like watching a silly cat video – can feel like a monumental victory.

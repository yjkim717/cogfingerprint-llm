## The Little Things That Really Get Under Your Skin

Isn't it funny how tech frustration often boils down to the *smallest* details? I was just reading a Reddit thread about a guy trying to replace the carpeting in his 2015 Honda Accord – a seemingly simple DIY project derailed by the wrong size rivets! It’s a reminder that even with all our fancy gadgets, sometimes it's the analog, the utterly basic, that throws a wrench in the works. Anyone else relate?

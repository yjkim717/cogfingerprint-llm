## The Unexpected Comfort of Analog

It's 2022, and we're drowning in screens. But lately, I've found myself strangely drawn to the tactile. Fixing a loose piece of carpeting in my car – needing to find the right rivets, the satisfying *thunk* of the hammer – it felt… grounding. Maybe it’s a reaction to constant digital stimulation, a quiet yearning for something real and solid. Who knew automotive repair could be so soothing?

I’m chasing that elusive Twitter/Telegram feel – those subtle left and right margins that give everything a clean, spacious look. It shouldn’t be this hard, right? I’m searching for a standard, reliable way to achieve this. Anyone else battling similar width issues with React Native Web? Let's share strategies – I'm all ears!

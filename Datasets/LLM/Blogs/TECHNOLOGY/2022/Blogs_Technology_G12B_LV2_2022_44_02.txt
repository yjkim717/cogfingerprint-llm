## Remember Dial-Up? A Little Tech Nostalgia

It's wild to think how much has changed, isn't it? Just last year, I was wrestling with buffering videos – a *constant* battle. Now? Streaming is seamless, and I can video call my niece across the country without a single glitch. 

I remember the agonizing wait of dial-up internet back in the late '90s. That screeching sound! The franticness of someone picking up the phone! It feels like a different lifetime. Seeing how far we’ve come in such a relatively short time is both exhilarating and a tiny bit… humbling. Makes you wonder what the next big shift will be, doesn't it?

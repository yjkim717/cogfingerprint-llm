## Remember When Tech Felt… Simpler?

It’s 2022, and I’m staring at my phone, feeling a strange kind of exhaustion. Not the battery kind, but a mental one. It feels like just yesterday, the internet was this exciting new frontier. Remember dial-up? The glorious, screeching sound of connection? Now, it’s just…constant. 

Every app wants my attention, every platform demands I curate a perfect version of myself. I miss the days when checking email felt like a conscious decision, not a compulsive twitch. Even something as simple as ordering groceries online feels like navigating a small obstacle course.

I’ve noticed it in others too. A vague sense of overwhelm, a longing for a slightly less connected existence. We’ve built this incredible, intricate web, but sometimes I wonder if we’ve lost something in the process – a little bit of quiet, a little bit of presence. Maybe it's time to consciously disconnect, just for a little while, and remember what it felt like before the world lived in our pockets.

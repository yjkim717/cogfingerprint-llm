

## That Feeling When Tech *Almost* Gets It Right

Remember that brief, frustrating period in 2022 when every app seemed to have a slightly-off recommendation engine? Like, suggesting I needed *another* sourdough starter kit? It felt like technology was so close to truly understanding me, but just…missing. It’s a peculiar feeling – a blend of hope and mild annoyance – that reminds me even the smartest systems are still learning. And maybe, just maybe, that’s okay.

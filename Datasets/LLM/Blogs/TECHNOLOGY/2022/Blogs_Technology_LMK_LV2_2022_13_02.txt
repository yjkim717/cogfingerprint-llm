"Lost in the Digital Haze: My Blockfolio Data Dilemma"

I'll never forget the sinking feeling in 2022 when my Blockfolio app crashed, taking my manually tracked investment data with it. I had spent hours logging purchases and monitoring my portfolio, only to see it all vanish. Desperate to recover my data, I searched for solutions, learning the hard way about the importance of backing up app data. A cautionary tale of the fragility of our digital lives.

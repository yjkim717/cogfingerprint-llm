**The Frustration of String Glue**

Remember those late nights in 2022, wrestling with Rust and WebAssembly? I do. I was chasing a safer way to build Wasm modules, desperately trying to ditch the dreaded string concatenation. It felt… fragile.  Like building a house with sticky tape. 

I stumbled upon the idea of a DSL – a dedicated scripting language – and the promise of tools like `pest.rs` to parse it.  It’s a reminder that sometimes, the simplest solutions are the most complex to implement, and a good library can really simplify things.

## The Blink of an Eye in the Digital Market

Something strange happened yesterday. I was watching the market—not investing heavily, just observing—and around 2:22 PM, things *moved*. A flurry of activity, a green spike that vanished as quickly as it appeared. It was gone in what felt like a blink.

It got me thinking about how we experience so much of our lives online now. Information, trends, opportunities… they flash past us, demanding attention, then disappearing. We’re constantly reacting, chasing the fleeting "green candle" of the moment. Are we truly absorbing anything, or just perpetually chasing the next fleeting trend? It's a dizzying pace, isn't it? Maybe a little mindful pausing is in order.

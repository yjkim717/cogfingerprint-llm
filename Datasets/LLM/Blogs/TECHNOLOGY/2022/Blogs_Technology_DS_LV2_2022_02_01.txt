Remember that satisfying tap to see all your voicemails laid out? On my old iPhone, visual voicemail felt like magic. Now, with my shiny new iPhone 13 on Verizon, that little red dot just mocks me. I’m back to the digital dark ages, dialing in and listening to robotic prompts. In 2022, you’d think an upgrade would bring more features, not fewer. It’s a strange, quiet loss in a world that’s supposed to be getting smarter.

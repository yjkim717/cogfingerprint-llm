**The Little Pause That Could**

Remember those moments when online ordering just…stopped? A frustrating freeze mid-checkout, a little digital stutter that left you staring at a paused screen? It’s oddly familiar, isn’t it? Like a tiny hiccup in the otherwise seamless flow of 2022 tech.

Sometimes, a brief moment of reflection, a chance to correct customer information, is exactly what prevents a bigger problem down the line.  It’s a reminder that perfect isn’t always immediate.

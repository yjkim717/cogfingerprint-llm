It was just a simple tap on the tablet screen to sign up for a Walgreens membership in 2022. Before I knew it, I’d accidentally applied for their credit card, too. My heart sank a little—I’d just handed over my SSN for a card I never intended to open. Now I’m stuck wondering: should I close it immediately, or does the fact there’s no annual fee make it worth keeping open? In our digital world, a single mis-click can lead to surprisingly personal financial dilemmas.

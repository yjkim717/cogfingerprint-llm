## The Ghosts in My Tablet Drawer

Remember that feeling of boundless possibility tech used to unlock? I was reminded of it recently when someone asked about getting Pokemon Go to work on an old Samsung Galaxy Tab Pro 8.4, running LineageOS. A valiant effort to breathe new life into a device most people have long forgotten.

It got me thinking about all the tech graveyard items I've got tucked away. Devices that *could* do amazing things, if only… if only they were still supported, if only the software caught up. There's a quiet sadness in that, isn’t there? A longing for the days when a little tinkering could keep something alive.  Maybe it’s a metaphor for something bigger, but right now, I'm just appreciating the ingenuity – and the slightly wistful hope – behind wanting to make something old *new* again. Anyone else got a drawer full of digital ghosts?

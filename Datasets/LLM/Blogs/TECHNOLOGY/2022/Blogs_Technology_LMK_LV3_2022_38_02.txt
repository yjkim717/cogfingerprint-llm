As I planned my 2022 trip to the Netherlands from the US, I found myself navigating a maze of COVID-19 entry rules. Do I need to show vaccine records alongside a negative test result? I realized that staying updated on travel restrictions was crucial. This experience made me appreciate how technology can simplify travel planning. With online resources and travel apps, staying informed about COVID test requirements and vaccine mandates is easier than ever, allowing us to focus on the excitement of exploring new destinations.

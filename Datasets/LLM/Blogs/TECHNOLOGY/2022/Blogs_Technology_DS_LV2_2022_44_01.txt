Of course, here is a reflective blog post from 2022.

***

Remember that frantic, hopeful search for a puppy in 2020? I do. After weeks of scrolling, we found "Buddy" from a seller with glowing reviews. He was perfect.

But our first vet visit revealed a chilling secret: two microchips. One was ours. The other, an old registration to a different name in another state. Our beloved new family member was likely stolen, his history erased and rewritten by a stranger.

In that moment, technology’s promise of connection felt hollow. The same digital marketplace that brought us together was a tool for heartless fraud. It was a stark lesson that behind every online profile—and every pet—is a real, fragile story we have a duty to protect.

"Getting Lost in the Scroll: My Mindless Social Media Marathon"

The more I scrolled, the more I felt like I was falling behind. It dawned on me that my perfectionism was seeping into my online interactions, making me feel like I needed to present a flawless digital persona. I realized that it's okay to not have it all together and that my worth isn't measured by my online presence.

**The Silent Struggle of the Server Farm**

There’s something deeply unsettling about a system that just…doesn’t *understand*. I was helping a colleague troubleshoot a weird ADFS issue last year – a frustrating certificate renewal error. The whole thing boiled down to the system failing to recognize other servers in the farm. It felt like shouting into a void! Adding the list of member servers manually – a simple step – was the fix. It reminded me that even the most complex tech needs a little human connection, a little patience to truly “see” what’s going on.

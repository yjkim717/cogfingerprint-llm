## The Ghosts in the Pixels

Remember chasing the perfect screenshot? Back in 2022, I was obsessed with finding a crisp, high-res image of Lilith from *Tera Online*. It felt silly, really – a digital ghost hunt. But it highlighted something fascinating: how much emotional weight we invest in these virtual worlds and the characters within them. A single, beautiful image became a symbol of a cherished online memory. It’s a strange, pixelated nostalgia.

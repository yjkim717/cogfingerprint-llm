"Snipe Camping: A Symptom of Gaming's Lone Wolf Problem"

As I dove back into my favorite multiplayer game in 2022, I couldn't help but feel frustrated with teammates who'd rather snipe from a distance than contribute to capturing points. It's a common issue - "snipe camping" can be a real team buzzkill. Effective teamwork requires coordination and strategy, not just individual skill. When players prioritize personal glory over teamwork, the whole team's gameplay suffers. Is it time to rethink our gaming strategies?

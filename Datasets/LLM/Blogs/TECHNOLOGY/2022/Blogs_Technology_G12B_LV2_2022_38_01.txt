## The Little Things That Frustrate (and Delight!)

Isn't it funny how tech frustrations often boil down to the *smallest* details? I was wrestling with a slightly crooked video the other day – just a 12-degree lean! – and realized how much I rely on simple rotation features in video editors. Turns out, finding genuinely *free* software that handles those tiny adjustments smoothly can be a surprisingly tricky quest. It's a reminder that even the most powerful tools are only as good as their ability to handle the everyday quirks.

It was 2022, and I’d spent months meticulously logging every crypto trade in Blockfolio. Then, the dreaded app error hit. My stomach dropped—all that manual entry, my entire purchase history, suddenly at risk. It wasn’t just data; it was my financial journey, a digital diary of decisions. In our tech-reliant world, we trust these apps with our stories. Losing them feels like losing a part of our own narrative, a quiet lesson in digital fragility.

## The Frustrations of Retro Gaming on the Go (2022)

Anyone else wrestling with getting classic games running smoothly on mobile? I’m currently battling crashes trying to play Majora’s Mask 3D on Citra via my Android – specifically, it seems my Snapdragon 8 Gen 1 is having a *time* with it. Decrypted files and all! It's a reminder that even with incredible tech, retro gaming on the go can still be a bumpy road.

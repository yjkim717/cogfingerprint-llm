## The Algorithm Knows Me Better Than I Do, Sometimes

It’s weird, isn’t it? How in 2022, my phone anticipates my needs before *I* do. Spotify knows exactly the low-key, slightly melancholic playlist I crave after a long day. Amazon suggests books I’d genuinely enjoy. It’s efficient, sure, but also… a little unsettling. 

I find myself wondering, are these algorithms truly understanding me, or just expertly mirroring patterns? Sometimes, I feel like I'm reacting *to* the recommendations, rather than following my own intuition. Maybe we all are, a little. It makes you pause and think: who’s really driving the bus?

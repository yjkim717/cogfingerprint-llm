## The Weight of Unread Emails (and Why I Finally Did Something About It)

Remember that feeling of accomplishment when you finally cleared out your closet? That satisfying *whoosh* of lightness? I just experienced something similar, but digitally. And honestly, it felt…bigger.

For years, my inbox has been a monster. A polite, digital monster, sure, but a monster nonetheless. Thousands of emails, newsletters I vaguely remember signing up for, promotional offers, and forgotten notifications all piled up, silently judging me. It felt overwhelming, like a constant low-level anxiety.

Then, sometime last fall (2022 felt like a blur, didn’t it?), I just snapped. I spent a weekend ruthlessly unsubscribing, deleting, and archiving. It was surprisingly emotional! Each click felt like releasing a tiny bit of mental baggage.

Now? My inbox is…manageable. And with that digital decluttering came a strange sense of calm. It’s a small thing, I know, but it’s a reminder that even our digital lives need a good spring clean. What’s one digital space *you* could clear out today?

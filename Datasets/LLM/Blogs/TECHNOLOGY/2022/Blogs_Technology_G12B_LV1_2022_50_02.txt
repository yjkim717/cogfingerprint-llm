>## The Little Things That Annoy (and Inspire)

It’s 2022, and we’re surrounded by polished, intuitive interfaces. Yet, sometimes the little frustrations stick with you. I was recently wrestling with VLC, trying to replicate the effortless skipping and pausing I’m used to on Netflix. Ten-second jump buttons! A picture pause! Why isn't this standard? It made me realize how much we take those design choices for granted, and how even simple tweaks can dramatically improve the viewing experience. Maybe it's time to embrace a little more user-driven customization.

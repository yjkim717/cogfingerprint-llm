>## The Unexpected Joy of Slow Mornings

It's 2022, and we're all chasing "hustle," right? I definitely was. Then, burnout hit. Hard. I started experimenting – deliberately waking up *later*. Just 30 minutes. It felt…wrong at first. But now? Those quiet moments with a cup of tea, reading a real book (not scrolling!), feel like a lifeline. It's a tiny rebellion against the constant pressure. Turns out, slowing down isn't laziness; it's a recharge. And honestly, I'm a much better human – and coder – for it. Who knew stillness could be so powerful?

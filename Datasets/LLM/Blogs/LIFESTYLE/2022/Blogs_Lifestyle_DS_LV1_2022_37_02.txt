**Finding My Character, One Stitch at a Time**

It was 2022, and my D&D group was moving our sessions online. Stuck at home, I felt disconnected from my new Elvish rogue. So, I picked up my knitting needles. As I searched for a cardigan pattern with elvish-wizardy flair, I realized I wasn't just making a cozy sweater; I was crafting her essence. Now, wearing it, I feel her cunning spirit woven into every stitch, a quiet magic for my everyday life.

It’s 2022, and I’ve been chasing a feeling—that genuine, skin-crawling terror only the best horror stories deliver. Podcasts became my refuge, but so many leaned on humor. Then I found "Video Palace," an audio drama that plunged me into a chilling tribulation. No jokes, just dread. It reminded me that in our curated lives, sometimes we need to embrace the dark, to feel truly alive. Now, I seek out those unsettling stories, finding clarity in the fear.

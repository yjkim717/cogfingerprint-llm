## The Unexpected Curveball

Remember when life felt predictable? Like, you had a plan, a roadmap, and you were mostly sticking to it? 2022 threw that out the window for me. We’d made some big decisions, felt settled, and just…assumed things were on a certain track. Then, bam! A complete surprise. It’s a bit like Sookie St. James discovering she's pregnant – you’re left reeling, reassessing, and trying to figure out how to bake a cake with a whole new set of ingredients. Life, huh?

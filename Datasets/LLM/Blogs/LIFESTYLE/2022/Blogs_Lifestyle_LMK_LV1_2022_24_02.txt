"The Vegan Vogue: When TV Chefs Cash In"

As I flipped through the channels in 2022, I was struck by the sudden proliferation of vegan TV chefs. It seemed like every other cooking show was touting the benefits of a plant-based diet, but beneath the trendy Instagram-friendly dishes, I sensed a lack of authenticity. Many of these celebrity chefs were promoting veganism as a lifestyle, yet their enthusiasm felt manufactured. To add insult to injury, they often presented others' innovative vegan recipes as their own creations. Is it time to call out these "vegan influencers" for their lack of sincerity and originality?

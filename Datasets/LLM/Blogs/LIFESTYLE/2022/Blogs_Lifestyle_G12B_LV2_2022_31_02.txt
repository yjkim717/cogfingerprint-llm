## Letting Go (and the Unexpected Heartache)

It’s funny how your heart expands in ways you never anticipated. This past week, one of my foster rabbits, Pip, found his forever home. Seeing him hop into his new carrier, surrounded by a family clearly bursting with love, was genuinely joyful. Like, *really* joyful.

But then? The quiet settled back in. And with it, a surprising wave of…missing him. I knew this day would come, of course. That's the whole point of fostering! Yet, the little thumps of his feet, the way he’d nibble my fingers – those tiny routines leave a bigger mark than you realize. It’s a bittersweet feeling, a beautiful reminder of the connections we make, even in the short time we have. Sending Pip all the good vibes for a happy life!

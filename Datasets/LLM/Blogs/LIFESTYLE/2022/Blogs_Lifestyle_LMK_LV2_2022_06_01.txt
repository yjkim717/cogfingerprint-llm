"The Surprise on My Flight: A Lesson in Mindful Spending. Last year, I flew with JetBlue and was surprised to see a charge for drinks on my credit card statement - or so I thought. It turned out, I had been promised a charge that never went through. As I reflected on the experience, I realized it's a reminder to stay on top of our in-flight purchases and track our expenses, even when they're 'free'."

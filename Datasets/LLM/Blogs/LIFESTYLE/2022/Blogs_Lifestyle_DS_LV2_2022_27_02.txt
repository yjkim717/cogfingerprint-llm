That familiar knot tightened in my stomach every time my parents mentioned marriage. In 2022, living authentically felt more urgent than ever, yet the weight of my conservative Indian family’s expectations was a heavy cloak. How do you tell the people you love most that you’re living a truth they’ve been taught to fear? The pressure to conform was immense, but the pressure to finally be *seen* was even greater. It was a terrifying, necessary step toward my own freedom.

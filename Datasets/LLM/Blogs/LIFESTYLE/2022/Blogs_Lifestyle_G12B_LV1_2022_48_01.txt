## The Unexpected Comfort of "Gently Used" Dreams

2022 felt like a pressure cooker. Suddenly, everyone expected me to *have it figured out*. Career? Finances? Adulting? Overwhelming. Then, staring at the mountain of bar exam prep materials, I hesitated. Could I really afford all this new stuff? A quick eBay search led me to someone selling Barbri materials from last year. It felt… comforting. Someone else had already walked this path, wrestled with these concepts. Maybe adulthood wasn’t about starting pristine, but about building on what’s already been. A little worn, a little loved, and perfectly ready for a new chapter.

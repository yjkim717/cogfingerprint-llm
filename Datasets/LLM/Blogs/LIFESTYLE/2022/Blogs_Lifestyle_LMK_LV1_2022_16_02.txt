As I sat at my desk, surrounded by screens and strategy guides, I found myself pondering the quirks of Hearts of Iron 4. I'd been trying to mod the game to let me control the Soviet Union as it took on the world - a thrilling prospect. But the game's historical settings had a mind of their own, and before I knew it, the UK had turned fascist. I laughed at the absurdity, wondering what my grandfather, a history buff, would make of it. It was a strange reflection of our world's unpredictability, and I was hooked.

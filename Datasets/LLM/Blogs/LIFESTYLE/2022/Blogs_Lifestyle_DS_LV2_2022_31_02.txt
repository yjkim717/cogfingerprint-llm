There’s a special kind of ache that comes with saying goodbye to your first foster bunny. Just this morning, I watched him hop into his new forever home—a perfect match after weeks of careful bunny bonding. My heart swelled and shattered all at once. In 2022, it’s these small, raw moments that remind me what love is: sometimes it’s holding on, but often, it’s letting go. Here’s to new beginnings, even when they tug at your soul.

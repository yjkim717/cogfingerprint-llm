As 2022 unfolded, I found myself caught between my truth and my traditional Indian business family’s expectations. The pressure to marry weighed heavily, but so did the silence around who I truly am. Coming out felt like navigating a minefield of cultural duty and personal fear. My tip? Start with one trusted relative—it creates a bridge. It’s not about one grand moment, but gentle steps toward being fully seen, even when it’s hard. Your peace is worth it.

"Navigating University Hopes: Will Predicted Grades Hold Me Back?

As I sat in sixth form, filling out my UCAS application in 2022, a nagging worry crept in - would my predicted grades dictate my university fate? I had worked hard, but I knew I was capable of more. What if my actual exam grades surpassed predictions? Would I be stuck with limited university choices? I wasn't alone in this anxiety. Many students face the same uncertainty. In this blog, I'll explore the impact of predicted grades on university aspirations and whether they truly limit our potential."

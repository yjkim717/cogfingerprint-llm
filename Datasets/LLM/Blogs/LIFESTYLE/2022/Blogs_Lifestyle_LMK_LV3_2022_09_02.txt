"Escaping to the Regency Era: A Haven for Romance Readers"

In 2022, I'm not alone in craving a break from the chaos of modern life. For many, Regency romances offer a tranquil escape. Fans of Jane Austen and Barbara Cartland know the joy of being swept away to a bygone era. If you're seeking authentic historical fiction beyond contemporary adaptations, look to authors like Elizabeth Rolls. Her meticulously researched novels transport readers to the Regency period, where social etiquette and swooning romances reign. Let's revisit the charm of Regency romances and discover new favorite authors, perfect for a cozy afternoon escape.

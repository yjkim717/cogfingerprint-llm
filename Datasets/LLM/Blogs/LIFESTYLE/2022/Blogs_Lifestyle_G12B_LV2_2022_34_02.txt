## That Feeling When… Everything’s Riding on One Deal

Remember that breathless feeling? The one where you’ve poured your heart and soul into something, and it *finally* feels like it might actually work? I had it a few weeks ago. My little business, which I bootstrapped from my kitchen table, was on the cusp of landing a seriously big deal – a product acquisition for a government contract, no less. We’re talking $285,000!

It was exhilarating, sure, but also terrifying. Suddenly, I was wrestling with things I hadn’t even considered before – things like managing a credit limit that felt enormous, and understanding these “net30 terms” everyone kept throwing around. Late payments are a real fear, especially when you’re relying on that income.

2022 has been a wild ride for small businesses, hasn’t it? Constant pivots, supply chain headaches… This felt like a chance to breathe, to actually invest in growth. It’s a lesson, I think, about chasing opportunities while also acknowledging the practical realities. Now, I'm just holding my breath, hoping everything goes smoothly. Wish me luck!

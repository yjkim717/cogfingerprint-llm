**Title: The Unseen Tether: A Reflection on Staying Connected**

It’s 2022, and my phone, a trusty Galaxy S9, suddenly feels like a brick. Verizon’s 3G shutdown left my "unlocked" variant struggling to find a 4G signal, severing my digital lifeline. In the sudden quiet, I noticed the world more—the way light hit my kitchen wall, the sound of actual birds. It was a forced, frustrating detox, but it made me wonder: when our primary connection fails, are we forced to find a better one with the world right in front of us?

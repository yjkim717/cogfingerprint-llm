## The Hidden Elo of Online Friendships

It's 2022, and even online gaming throws curveballs. Lately, I’ve been noticing a strange phenomenon in Age of Empires 4 – players intentionally hiding their match history to create "beginner-only" lobbies. It got me thinking: how much do we *really* trust someone's self-proclaimed skill level? It’s a digital echo of real-life, isn't it? We all curate our online personas, and sometimes, transparency feels… vulnerable.

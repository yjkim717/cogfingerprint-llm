## The Unexpected Reset

It’s funny how life can mirror video games sometimes. I was playing Dying Light 2 last week, and kept getting frustratingly respawned *behind* a moved wardrobe in my safe house. Talk about disorienting! It made me think about my own routines. Lately, I've felt like my life’s been a bit…shifted. Old habits, like my evening walks, have fallen by the wayside. Maybe it’s time to consciously rearrange my "wardrobe" of daily rituals and find a fresh starting point. 2022, let’s recalibrate.

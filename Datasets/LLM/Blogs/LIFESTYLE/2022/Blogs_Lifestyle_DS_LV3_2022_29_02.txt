It’s 2022, and my bipolar diagnosis still feels like a lonely island sometimes. I take my medication faithfully, but when old friends drift away or don’t get the rollercoaster, the silence is deafening. The few who also lived with it? Our connection faded, and that loss stings the most. So here I am, putting this out into the void—a small vent to anyone listening who gets it. You’re not alone in feeling alone, and sometimes, just saying that is a start.

## When Your Life Feels Like an Empty Notebook

Remember 2020? It felt like *everything* was shifting, right? For me, it hit in a weird, unexpected way. I was wrestling with a frustrating tech issue – my OneNote 2010 desktop notebook, usually brimming with ideas and to-do lists, suddenly appeared completely blank. The online version? Perfectly fine. Go figure.

It got me thinking. Sometimes, don’t we all feel a bit like that notebook? Like the *real* substance of our lives, our passions, our purpose, feels lost somewhere, even when we’re outwardly “functioning.” We’re showing up, posting the highlight reel online, but internally… empty.

This year has been a massive reset. Maybe the blank notebook was a sign to really examine what I was filling it with. To reconnect with the *source* of my inspiration, instead of just the digital reflection. Anyone else feeling this disconnect, and finding ways to bridge the gap?

Remember those endless 2020 lockdown hours? My escape was chasing the platinum trophy in Madden 21. But one trophy, the head-to-head win, was a ghost. The moment I’d pull ahead, *poof*—my opponent would quit, voiding the game. It became a bizarre lesson in digital sportsmanship. In a year where we all craved connection, even a virtual football game mirrored the frustration of unfinished business and elusive victories. Some wins, it turns out, aren't just about the scoreboard.

**The Spreadsheet That Broke My Heart (2020)**

Honestly, 2020 was a weird year for numbers. I was meticulously tracking everything – groceries, bills, that impulse Amazon purchase. But my budgeting spreadsheet kept flashing a red “AtoB” – meaning the money *going in* didn’t match the money *coming out*. It was baffling! Turns out, a forgotten credit card transaction was the culprit. It highlighted how easily we can lose sight of the details, even when we think we're being careful.  It was a humbling reminder to actually *look* at my transactions.

**The Year My Car Refused to Cooperate**

Honestly, 2020 felt like a year of little frustrations piling up. And my 2004 Ford Fiesta? It seemed determined to be the biggest one. Suddenly, starting it in the rain became a monumental task – a sputtering, reluctant battle. It ran like a grumpy beast until it warmed up, mirroring my own mood sometimes. 

It made me realize how reliant we are on things working smoothly, and how a small mechanical issue can suddenly feel like a huge obstacle.  Maybe it was a gentle reminder to appreciate the simple things, and to be patient with myself, just like coaxing that old Fiesta to life.

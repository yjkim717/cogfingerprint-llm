

## The Unexpected Comfort of Routine in a Wild Year

2020… what a year, right? Between the chaos, I found myself strangely comforted by simple routines. Making my morning coffee, a daily walk, even just watering my plants. It felt like anchoring myself amidst a world that felt constantly adrift. Sometimes, finding peace isn't about grand adventures, but appreciating the quiet, steady rhythm of everyday life. It’s a lesson I’ll carry forward.

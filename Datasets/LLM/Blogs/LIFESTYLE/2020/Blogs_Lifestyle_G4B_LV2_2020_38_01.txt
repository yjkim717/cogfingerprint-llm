**A Quiet Worry in Delhi, 2020**

Remember 2020? It felt like we were all just *trying* to figure things out. Here in Delhi, there was a nagging worry – a feeling that the “virus situation” was slipping away from our attention. I watched people, rushing past, forgetting the simple act of distancing. It felt frustrating, a quiet disappointment.  Social distancing wasn’t about restriction; it was about looking out for each other.  It’s a reminder that even in the midst of chaos, small acts of care matter.



## Finding My North Star (and Maybe Yours Too)

2020 has been… a lot, hasn't it? Like many, I’ve felt adrift, questioning everything. Lately, I've been diving into Dr. Jordan Peterson's work, and it's sparked something in me - a desire to actively shape my life instead of just reacting to it. This isn’t a sudden transformation, but a slow, messy climb. I’m sharing this because maybe, just maybe, knowing someone else is wrestling with feeling lost can be a little comfort. Let's figure this out together.

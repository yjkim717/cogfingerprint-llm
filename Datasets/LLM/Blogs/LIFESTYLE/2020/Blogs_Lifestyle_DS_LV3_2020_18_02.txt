In 2020, my world shrank to the size of my apartment, but my imagination found freedom in audiodramas. While many podcasts start strong, few sustain that magic. For me, anthologies often fizzle, but then I found Bronzeville. Its rich storytelling was a complete escape. Video Palace hooked me, too, even if the ending divided fans. In a year of uncertainty, these rare, start-to-finish gems weren't just entertainment; they were a lifeline to other worlds, proving that the most powerful journeys happen with your eyes closed.

Let’s be real—2020 had us all rethinking our TV habits. When my friends teased me for binging *Outer Banks*, calling it a “chick show,” I almost caved to the pressure. But you know what? Sarah Cameron’s magnetic presence aside, it was the high-stakes adventure and loyal friendships that hooked me. Sometimes, the shows we’re quick to judge end up reminding us to embrace what we love, labels be damned. Life’s too short for guilty pleasures.

It’s 2020, and my old Ford Fiesta has become a barometer for my mood. On sunny days, it purrs to life—just like my optimism. But let the rain fall, and it sputters, hesitating like my own doubts in tough times. We both struggle with a wet start, a rough idle mirroring those uncertain days. Funny how a car’s quirks remind you: even when things feel stalled, clearer skies always return.

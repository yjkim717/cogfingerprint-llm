Overcoming Procrastination and Stage Fright: My GCSE English Language Triumph (2020)

As I received my GCSE results in 2020, I was thrilled to have achieved a distinction in my English Language speech. Despite struggling with public speaking and putting off my preparation until the last night, I pushed through my fears and nailed it. This experience taught me that with determination and last-minute effort, I can overcome procrastination and achieve my goals, even when faced with daunting tasks like public speaking.

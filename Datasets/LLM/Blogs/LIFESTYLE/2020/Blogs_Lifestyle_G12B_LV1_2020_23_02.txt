>## The Unfinished Quest of 2020

This year felt a lot like a Destiny 2 quest gone wrong. I unlocked the "potential" chest – the shiny new job, the ambitious project – *before* truly committing to the necessary steps. Suddenly, the path forward was blocked. It’s a strange feeling, having the reward in sight but the journey incomplete. 2020 taught me that sometimes, the best thing you can do is revisit the starting point and lay the groundwork, even when the finish line is tempting.

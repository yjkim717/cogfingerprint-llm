## Finding Little Wins When Everything Feels…Heavy

2020, right? We all feel it. The weight of… *everything*. News cycles, uncertainty, the sheer strangeness of it all. I've found myself needing to actively seek out small pockets of joy, little wins to counteract the overwhelm. 

For me, that’s been diving into *Forager*. Sounds silly, I know, a pixelated game. But the simple act of gathering resources, expanding my little world, even just trading with the travelling merchant for a better deal… it's surprisingly grounding. It’s a tiny, contained space where I *can* make progress, where I *can* build something. 

It’s a reminder that even when the bigger picture feels chaotic, there's still room to create, to collect, to find a little bit of beauty. And honestly, sometimes that’s enough.

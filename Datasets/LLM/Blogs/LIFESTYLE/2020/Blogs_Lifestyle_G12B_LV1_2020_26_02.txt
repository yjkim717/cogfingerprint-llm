## The Unexpected Joy of Digital Scavenging

Okay, so 2020 has been… a lot. Lately, I've found myself sinking into the bizarre comfort of online communities. Specifically, I’m building an *Among Us* montage – don’t judge! – and it's been surprisingly therapeutic. Hunting for cursed images and amazing fan art feels like a little digital treasure hunt, a silly distraction from the world’s chaos. Who knew finding the perfect meme could be so… grounding?

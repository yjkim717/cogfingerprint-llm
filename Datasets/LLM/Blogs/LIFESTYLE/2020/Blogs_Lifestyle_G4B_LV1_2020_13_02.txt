**Disconnected, But Still Connected**

It’s weird, isn’t it? 2020. Everyone glued to screens, yet feeling increasingly…disconnected. I found myself obsessing over network configurations – setting up Hyper-V guests to talk to my local network, even using Wake-on-LAN to gently rouse my laptop. It felt oddly symbolic: wanting to control the connections, to be reliably reachable, even when everything felt chaotic outside. Maybe it’s just a desire for order in a strange new world.

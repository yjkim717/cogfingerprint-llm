>## The Unexpected Calm of Routine

2020 threw a lot at us, didn’t it? For me, the initial chaos eventually morphed into a strange, comforting routine. Suddenly, the pressure to constantly *do* something evaporated. I found myself relishing the simple things: early morning walks, meticulously planned grocery trips, even just watering my plants. It wasn't about productivity; it was about finding peace in predictability. Who knew a global pandemic could teach me to slow down and appreciate the quiet rhythm of everyday life? It’s a lesson I’m carrying forward.

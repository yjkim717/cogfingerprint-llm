## The Weight of the World (and a Punching Bag)

Remember *Rocky IV*? All that Cold War chest-thumping and Ivan Drago's icy stare? It always struck me as a really… intense distillation of fear. And lately, rewatching it feels even *more* resonant. 2020 has been a year of its own kind of chilling. Not a single, looming enemy like the Soviet Union, but a creeping uncertainty, a sense of fragility exposed.

We've all felt that weight, haven't we? Like we're training for a fight we don't fully understand. I started hitting a punching bag in my garage a few weeks ago. Sounds silly, I know. But there’s something oddly cathartic about channeling that anxiety into physical exertion. It’s not about winning or losing, it's about showing up, putting in the work, and facing down whatever comes next, one punch at a time. Maybe we all need our own makeshift training montages right now.

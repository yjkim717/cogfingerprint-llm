"In 2020, I adopted intermittent fasting, but struggled with dry mouth while on medication. Sugary candies weren't an option, so I discovered some effective coping strategies. Staying hydrated before and after fasting, using a humidifier, and chewing sugar-free gum brought relief. By experimenting with these simple hacks, I was able to continue my fasting journey comfortably, prioritizing my overall health and well-being."

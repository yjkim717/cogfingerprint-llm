As we navigated the challenges of 2020, staying productive became more crucial than ever. With the lines between work and personal life increasingly blurred, it was easy to get bogged down in a sea of tasks and lose motivation. That's when I discovered the power of 'Work Cycles' - a simple yet effective technique that transformed my workday. By breaking down my tasks into focused 90-minute cycles, followed by short breaks, I was able to stay energized and engaged. This technique, combined with discipline and effective time management, helped me tackle my to-do list with renewed enthusiasm. As I adopted this approach, I noticed a significant boost in my productivity and motivation. By incorporating 'Work Cycles' into my daily routine, I was able to achieve a better work-life balance and make the most of my time. It was a game-changer.

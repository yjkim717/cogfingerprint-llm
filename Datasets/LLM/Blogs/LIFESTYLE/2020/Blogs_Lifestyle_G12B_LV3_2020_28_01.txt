## The Boomerang in Space and Finding Your Way Back

Remember 2020? Everything felt… adrift. Like a spaceship, maybe? I was pondering that feeling the other day, and it oddly led me to thinking about a boomerang in zero gravity. If you throw it, does it come back? Physics says yes, even without gravity pulling it down. It got me thinking – even when life feels weightless, a little momentum, a little intention, can still guide you home.

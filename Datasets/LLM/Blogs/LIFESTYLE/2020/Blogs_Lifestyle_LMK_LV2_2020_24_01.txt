"Learning in Isolation: A Pandemic Reality Check"

As we navigated the uncharted territories of 2020, many of us found ourselves learning new skills in solitude. I began coding, but motivation was a struggle. That's when I stumbled upon a Reddit post proposing an app to connect learners with similar interests. It sparked an idea - what if we could learn together, even from afar? The concept of online study buddies and accountability partners resonated deeply, and I was inspired to take my programming skills to the next level.

## The Bagel Quest & the Beauty of Slow Dough

2020 has been all about embracing the simple things, right? Lately, I've been obsessed with bagels – that chewy, satisfying goodness. A Reddit thread popped up asking about no-knead bagels *without* a mixer. Could you really get away with hand-kneading that dough? It got me thinking about the joy of slowing down, of putting in the effort, even if it’s just 10 minutes of hand-kneading. Maybe the best things *are* the ones that require a little more patience.

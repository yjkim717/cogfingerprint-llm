## The Ghosts in the Game (and Life Lately)

Remember 2020? Everything felt…fragile. I was chasing that Platinum trophy in Madden 21, a silly goal, I know. But then, constant disconnections plagued my online matches. It mirrored how I felt – constantly losing connection with plans, with people, with a sense of normalcy. Frustrating, right? It made me pause. Maybe striving for some arbitrary “win” wasn't the point. Maybe it was just about showing up, even when the connection was flickering. A small lesson learned amidst the chaos.

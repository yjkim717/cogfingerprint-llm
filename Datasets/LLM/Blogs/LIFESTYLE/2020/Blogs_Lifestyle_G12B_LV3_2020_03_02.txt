## Finding My Fold in a World on Pause

Remember 2020? The world felt…still. Stuck. I stumbled upon a Reddit thread in my usual ultralight backpacking group, but the conversation wasn't about gear. It was about *hobbies*. Suddenly, everyone was searching for indoor activities. I dove into origami – surprisingly meditative! – and even dusted off my old policy debate skills. It was a reminder that connection and joy can be found in unexpected places, even when van life and mountain trails are temporarily off the table. What did *you* discover during those quiet days?

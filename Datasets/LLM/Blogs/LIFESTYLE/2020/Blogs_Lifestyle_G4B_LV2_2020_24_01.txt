**Remembering the Power of "We" in 2020**

Honestly, 2020 felt like a year of isolation, right? I was staring at my laptop screen, trying to learn a new coding language – a *really* ambitious goal. It hit me that I needed more than just tutorials. That’s when I started thinking about a simple idea: a virtual “study buddy.” Connecting with others struggling with the same challenges? Suddenly, it felt less daunting, more… human.  It reminded me that learning, and life, is always better with a little “we.”

## The Unexpected Joy of Slowing Down

Remember March 2020? The world shifted. Suddenly, my frantic, always-on lifestyle screeched to a halt. Initially, I panicked. All those plans, the hustle… gone. But then, something quiet started to bloom. I rediscovered the joy of making sourdough (disasters and all!), of long walks without a destination, of actually *reading* a book instead of just adding it to a towering pile. It hasn't been easy, the uncertainty lingers, but this forced pause has reminded me that sometimes, the richest experiences aren't found in the relentless pursuit of *more*, but in simply *being*. And that’s a lesson I’m carrying forward.

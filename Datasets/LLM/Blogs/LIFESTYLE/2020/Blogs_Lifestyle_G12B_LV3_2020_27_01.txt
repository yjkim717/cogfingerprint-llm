## The King's Bathroom Break & Our Own Power Struggles

Remember binging *Succession* back in 2020? There's a scene that's stuck with me – Logan Roy, unexpectedly, in Kendall's office. Was it a calculated move, a display of dominance? Or something else entirely? It got me thinking about how we all navigate power dynamics, especially within families. Sometimes, those seemingly random acts are just… complicated. Aren't we all a little like Logan sometimes, wrestling with our own vulnerabilities and trying to maintain control?

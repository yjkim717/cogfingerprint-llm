## Little Shifts, Big Feels

Remember 2020? A year that felt… a lot. I stumbled into manifestation then, desperately seeking a little control. It started small: listing goals, genuinely feeling gratitude for the everyday, and even whispering self-compliments in the mirror. Sounds silly, right? But those tiny shifts, that intentional focus on the good, genuinely eased the weight. It wasn’t magic, but it *was* empowering.

## The Unexpected Joy of Imperfect Baking

Remember 2020? Everything felt… uncertain. I found solace in baking, attempting elaborate cakes I’d see online. My first few attempts? Let's just say they were *creative*. A sunken middle here, a collapsed layer there. But you know what? Those imperfect treats tasted like love and resilience. It wasn’t about perfection, it was about the process, the small victories, and finding joy in something tangible during a very strange year.

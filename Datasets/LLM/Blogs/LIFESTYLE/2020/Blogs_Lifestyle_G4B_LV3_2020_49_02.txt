**The Spreadsheet Shame of 2020**

Remember 2020? It felt like everything was a little… off. I was meticulously tracking every expense in a spreadsheet, trying to build a budget – and utterly baffled when ‘AtoB’ kept flashing red, a negative number despite my bank balances looking solid. It was a tiny, frustrating glitch, but it highlighted how easily we can lose sight of the bigger picture when focusing on the details.

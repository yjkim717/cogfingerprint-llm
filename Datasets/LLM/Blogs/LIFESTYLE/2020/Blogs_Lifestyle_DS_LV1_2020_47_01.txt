Looking back on 2020, I felt completely adrift. That’s when I stumbled upon Jordan Peterson’s advice: start by cleaning your room. It seemed too simple, but I did it. Then I tackled my closet. This step-by-step approach became my anchor. I’m sharing this not as an expert, but as a fellow traveler. If you're feeling lost, just start with one small, ordered corner of your life. The journey begins there.

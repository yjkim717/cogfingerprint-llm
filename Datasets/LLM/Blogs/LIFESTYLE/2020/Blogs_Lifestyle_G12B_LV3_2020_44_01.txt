## The Unexpected Joy of Pixelated Problem-Solving

Remember 2020? Stuck inside, searching for little escapes? I found mine revisiting *Pokemon Emerald* via VisualBoyAdvance. Sounds silly, right? But wrestling with those cheat codes – specifically, figuring out the master code format – became a strangely satisfying puzzle. It was a tiny, digital challenge that offered a welcome distraction and a surprising sense of accomplishment. Anyone else find solace in unexpected places back then?

**The Day My Fiesta Refused to Cooperate**

It’s funny, isn’t it? How a little thing – a car – can suddenly feel like a massive frustration. Last October, my trusty (mostly) Ford Fiesta decided it *loved* the rain. Dry weather? Perfect starts. Rain? Just a stubborn refusal to turn over. 

It made me think about how easily we get used to things going smoothly, and how quickly we can become annoyed when they don’t.  Like a sudden, unexpected hurdle in your day.  Sometimes, you just need to wait it out, right?

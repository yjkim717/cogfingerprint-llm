You know that moment when someone asks what you do? In 2021, working from my kitchen table, I’m tempted to say “world-famous author and illustrator.” Never mind that my day job involves spreadsheets, not crayons, and the only thing I’m mining is data, not coal. But isn’t our identity more than our 9-to-5? Maybe our passions—not our paychecks—tell the real story. Who’s with me?

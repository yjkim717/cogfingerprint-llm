"City Life: The Uninvited Guest - Noise in Jersey City" 
As I strolled through downtown Jersey City in 2021, a deafening roar shook the air, leaving me wondering - what was that?! Many of my neighbors echoed the same question. Some blamed low-flying aircraft, while others speculated about construction. Whatever the source, the noise was a jarring reminder that in our bustling city, serenity is often the uninvited guest. Has the constant din become our new normal?

Living downtown was supposed to be the ultimate luxury upgrade. But in 2021, the soundtrack isn't chic cafes; it's a sudden, deafening roar that shakes the windows. I rushed outside, expecting catastrophe, only to see fighter jets carving through the sky. It’s a stark reminder that no amount of polished concrete and designer finishes can drown out the realities of the world—or the persistent hum of car exhausts below. A strange new definition of city living.

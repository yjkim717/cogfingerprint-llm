There I was in my 2021 home studio, chasing that perfect tone on my Katana 50MK2. I’d set both foot switches to latch mode, ready to seamlessly jump between channels. But only the 'b' button worked, toggling between banks while 'a' sat silent. It was a small, stubborn reminder that sometimes the gear—like life—has its own plans, and the solution is often simpler than the frustration it causes.

It’s funny how small changes can shift your whole perspective. Last year, I was obsessing over optimizing my VMware Workstation – painstakingly setting static IPs on my Windows 10 VMs, wrestling with DHCP. It felt… controlling, almost. Now, realizing how much time I wasted tweaking, I’m trying to embrace the flow. Letting go of needing *everything* to be perfectly defined, like a neatly configured network.  It’s a surprisingly liberating shift.  Maybe life’s less about the perfect IP address and more about just… connecting.

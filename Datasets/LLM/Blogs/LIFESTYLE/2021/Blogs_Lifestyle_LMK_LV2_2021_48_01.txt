"Tempted by the Market: A FIRE Journey Crossroads"

As I sat down to review my latest mortgage statement in the spring of 2021, I couldn't help but feel a thrill at the news: my home's appraisal value had skyrocketed by 40-45% in less than a year. It was a staggering gain, one that made my head spin with the possibilities. The real estate boom was in full swing, and I was caught in its midst. As someone striving for financial independence through the FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) movement, the temptation to cash in and accelerate my journey was strong. Should I refinance and tap into the equity, or take the leap and sell? The thought of shaving years off my timeline was tantalizing. As I weighed my options, I couldn't help but wonder: was this a chance to speed towards my dreams, or a siren's call to financial recklessness?

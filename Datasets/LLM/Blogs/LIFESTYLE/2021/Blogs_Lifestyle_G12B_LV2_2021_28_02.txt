## Letting Go of the "Perfect" Project

It’s funny how things shift, isn’t it? Lately, I’ve been realizing I get so caught up in *perfecting* hobbies. A knitting project has to be flawless, a recipe meticulously followed. But 2021 has taught me a lot about embracing the imperfect.

I started a little pottery project, and the first few attempts were… well, let's just say they looked like abstract art. But instead of frustration, I felt a strange sense of freedom. It’s okay if it’s not "perfect." It's *mine*. And sometimes, the unexpected beauty lies in the flaws.

**The Day My House Numbers Almost Fell Off**

2021 felt like a year of small, persistent annoyances, didn’t it? Mine manifested in the form of a ridiculously flimsy house number plaque. It just… wouldn’t stay put on my siding. I spent a ridiculous amount of time wrestling with it, trying every hook, every nail, feeling increasingly defeated. 

It wasn't about the numbers themselves, really. It was about the feeling of things not being secure, not being *right*.  It made me realize how much we fixate on the little details – and how important it is to find a solid, reliable way to anchor them, both literally and figuratively, in our lives.

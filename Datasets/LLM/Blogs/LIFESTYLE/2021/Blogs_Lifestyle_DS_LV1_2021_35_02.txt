**Finding the Right Difficulty in Life**

Remember in 2021, when I was obsessed with that baseball video game? I’d get so frustrated making "perfect" contact, only to have the ball caught every time. I was playing on a veteran-level difficulty that just didn't fit my style. It hit me recently—I was doing the same thing in my own life, pushing for a career pace that looked good but felt impossible. I’ve finally started adjusting my own "sliders," prioritizing peace over perfection, and suddenly, everything feels less like a grind and more like play.

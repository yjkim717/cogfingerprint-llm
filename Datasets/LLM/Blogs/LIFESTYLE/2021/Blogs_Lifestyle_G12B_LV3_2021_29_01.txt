## Late Nights & Pixelated Thrills: Finding Escape in Tech-Noir

Remember those nights in 2021, scrolling through Netflix, desperately seeking something *different*? I was on a serious kick for tech-inspired thrillers – shows like *How to Sell Drugs Online (Fast)*, movies like *Searching* – anything with a clever premise and a slick UI. It wasn't just the plot; it was the visual language, the way technology became a character itself. It's amazing how a well-designed interface can amplify the suspense, isn't it?

"Missing Out on Campus Life: A Commuter's Regret. As I navigated college in 2021, I chose to commute instead of dorming on campus. Living close by seemed convenient, but it came at a cost. Reflecting now, I wish I'd taken the leap to dorm, traded convenience for connection, and immersed myself in the vibrant campus life that I often took for granted."

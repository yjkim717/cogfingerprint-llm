## The Weight of Passion (and My Telescope)

It’s 2021, and I’m still wrestling with the same problem: how to safely transport my passions. My Celestron telescope, a beautiful piece of equipment allowing glimpses of distant galaxies, is a prime example. The OTA and fork mount *together* are a beast, and finding a decent carrying case? Forget about it! Existing options are just…wrong. 

It’s more than just the gear, isn't it? It’s the commitment. The desire to explore. But that exploration feels a little hampered when the logistics are such a headache. Maybe that’s a universal truth for anyone chasing a hobby – balancing the joy with the practicalities.

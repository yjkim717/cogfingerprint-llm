## The Crushing Weight of Digital Loss (and Unhelpful Help)

It's 2021, and even our hobbies can feel… weighty. Recently, I lost a *ton* of crafting materials in Apex Legends – a frustrating setback after hours of grinding. But honestly, the real gut punch was EA support. Their response? Accusatory and dismissive. It made me realize how easily we invest emotional energy in digital spaces, and how disappointing it is when those spaces don't reciprocate. It’s a small thing, but it stings.

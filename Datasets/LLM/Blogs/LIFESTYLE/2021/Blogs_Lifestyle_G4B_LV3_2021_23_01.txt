**Chasing That Skateboarding Vibe**

Like ‘Stuntin’ by The Floozies’, but without the heavy electronic beat. I craved upbeat guitar riffs and lyrics that fired on all cylinders. It’s about capturing that feeling of freedom, that fast-paced, exhilarating vibe you get when you’re cruising on a skateboard.

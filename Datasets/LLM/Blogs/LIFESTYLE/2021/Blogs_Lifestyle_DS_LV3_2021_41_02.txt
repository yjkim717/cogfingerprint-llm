There’s something bittersweet about revisiting old favorites—like firing up Rainbow Six Vegas in 2021, hoping to relive those tense terrorist hunt sessions with friends. But instead of nostalgia, I’m met with the same stubborn uPlay login error, a ghost from Ubisoft’s past that just won’t quit. It’s a reminder that some doors close for good, pushing us to find new adventures. Maybe it’s life’s way of nudging us forward, even when we’re clinging to the comfort of what used to be.

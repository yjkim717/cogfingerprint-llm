)**The Static IP Struggle & Finding Stability in a Chaotic Year**

2021 felt like a constant juggling act, didn't it? Trying to keep everything running smoothly, but things shifting beneath our feet. I recently wrestled with VMware Workstation, trying to assign static IPs to my VMs – a surprisingly apt metaphor for the year! It's frustrating when things aren't consistent, when that reliable foundation you build just…moves. Ultimately, finding that stability, whether in virtual networks or life itself, requires a little troubleshooting and persistence.

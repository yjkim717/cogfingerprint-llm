## The Grind and the Reset

Ugh, 2021 has been *a year*. Lately, it felt like everything was a losing streak. Not in a dramatic, life-altering way, but a slow, frustrating chipping away. My Valorant rank plummeted from Plat to Gold – a 24-game slide! It was a weird mirror for how I felt generally. Maybe it’s time for a reset, a little less chasing wins, and a little more focusing on just… playing.

## The Beauty of Assigned Addresses

Remember 2021? Everything felt…unstable. Like a network trying to auto-configure itself. I was constantly chasing after things, reacting to whatever popped up. My to-do list was a sprawling, unmanaged DHCP server, assigning priorities willy-nilly.

Then I started thinking about VMware Workstation – specifically, setting up static IPs for VMs based on MAC addresses. It seemed so… deliberate. A system where *I* controlled the assignments. 

It hit me. Maybe life doesn't need to be a constant scramble for the next thing. Maybe a little structure, a little pre-planning (assigning those “static IPs” to my priorities), could actually bring a sense of calm. Not rigidity, but a knowing that *some* things are firmly in place, allowing the rest to breathe and shift organically. It’s a work in progress, of course, but embracing a bit of managed chaos feels a whole lot better than just letting everything ping wildly.

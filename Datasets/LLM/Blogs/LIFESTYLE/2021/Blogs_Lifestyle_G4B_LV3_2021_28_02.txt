**Tiny Metal, Big Ideas**

Remember 2021? I was obsessed with 3D printing, specifically experimenting with pewter and gold alloys – it felt wonderfully futuristic.  There were frustrating moments with heat and print quality, but honestly, it was a lesson in patience.  Trying to coax these inexpensive metals to behave on an FDM printer made me realize that even the smallest challenges can spark huge creative ideas. It’s about the process, not just the perfect print.

## The Unexpected Joy of Letting Go (and Fragrance Swaps!)

Isn't it funny how possessions can sometimes feel…heavy? I’m currently parting with a beautiful bottle of Roja Oceania – nearly full! It’s sparked a surprisingly lovely realization: joy isn’t always about *having*, but about the connection and discovery that comes from sharing, trading, or letting go. Maybe a fragrance swap is just a metaphor for decluttering the soul.

"Designing Life, One Slide at a Time: When Tech Gets in the Way

As I sat staring at my screen, frustration mounting, I couldn't help but think of all the times tech has hindered my creative flow. In 2024, you'd think our tools would be more intuitive, not less. My Canva slideshow refused to save, despite a stable internet connection. It was a minor setback, but it made me realize how much we rely on these platforms. Is it time to explore alternatives like Google Slides? Or should we be advocating for better customer support? Sometimes, it's the little tech tantrums that make us rethink our design – and life – choices."

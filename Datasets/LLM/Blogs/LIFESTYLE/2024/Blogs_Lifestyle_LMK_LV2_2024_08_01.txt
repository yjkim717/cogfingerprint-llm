"Getting Around Belfast: A Traveler's Tale. As I stepped off the bus in Belfast in 2024, I was hit with a familiar travel dilemma - how to get to my destination. The city is beautiful, but navigating it can be tricky. I weighed my options: walking through the historic streets, hailing a taxi, or using a ride-hailing app. Data roaming was a concern, but a quick pre-trip download of maps saved the day.

**The Edit Button & My Brain**

It’s funny, isn’t it? In 2024, we have these amazing “WYSIWYG” apps – you see what you’re creating *as* you create it. Like a live editing dream! But I’ve realized it’s also made me a little anxious about every little tweak.  I used to just *go* with it, but now I’m constantly sending out “patch requests” to my own thoughts, trying to perfect everything.  Maybe I need to embrace the beautiful, messy process, like relying on session storage for a good idea – let it sit and grow.

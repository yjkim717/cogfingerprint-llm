## The Algorithm Knows Too Much (Sometimes)

Anyone else feeling a little overwhelmed by their phone lately? I’ve been noticing the Reddit app’s ‘Latest’ feed is…suggestive, let’s say. It’s 2024, and I'm trying to curate a calmer digital space! Blocking specific subreddits seems like the obvious solution, but it’s a reminder of how much these algorithms learn about us. A little digital decluttering feels surprisingly good for the soul.

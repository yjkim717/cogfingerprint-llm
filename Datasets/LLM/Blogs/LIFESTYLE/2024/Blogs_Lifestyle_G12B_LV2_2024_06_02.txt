## The Unexpected Cost of Paradise

Cape Cod in July. Just *thinking* about it conjures sunshine and salty air. But this year, planning our week-long rental in Provincetown threw a curveball: parking. Apparently, even paradise has fees! A Reddit thread popped up about the $30 daily maximum – a surprising sting after budgeting for lobster rolls and beach days. It’s a reminder, isn’t it? Even the most idyllic escapes require a little extra planning (and maybe a hefty parking budget!).

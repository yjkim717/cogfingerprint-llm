The familiar hum of the highway is our family’s soundtrack. When we decided to upgrade our 5th wheel, the biggest debate wasn't about towing capacity—it was about the floorplan. Do we prioritize a dedicated bunk room for the kids, giving them a private space to call their own, or opt for the more common, open layout? In 2024, RV camping is less about the destination and more about creating a home on wheels where everyone has a little room to breathe.

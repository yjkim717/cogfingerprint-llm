**The Tiny Text Trap**

It’s weird, right? I was wrestling with a design project today – resizing text in a graphics tool – and it felt like a total frustration. It just shrunk the box, but the actual words stayed stubbornly the same size. It’s a small thing, but it highlighted how easily we can get caught up in the *presentation* of things, forgetting the core message.  Sometimes, it's about clarity, not just clever visuals, isn’t it?

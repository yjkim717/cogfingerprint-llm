**The Unexpected Joy of a Little Imperfection**

I’ve been spending a lot of time tackling small home projects this year – mostly because adulting feels a bit overwhelming sometimes! Right now, I’m laying LVP flooring in my tiny bathroom, and I’m wrestling with whether to just lay it all straight or stagger the boards. There’s a certain ease to perfection, but honestly? Staggering feels more… intentional. It’s a tiny rebellion against rigidness, a little visual interest. Maybe it’s about embracing a touch of imperfection in a space that needs it.

### The Digital Declutter: Finding My Cache in 2024

This year, I promised myself less digital clutter. I was that person with a thousand browser tabs and a desktop littered with files. My computer’s performance, much like my focus, was sluggish. Then I learned about a simple trick: using a small, speedy NVMe drive as a cache for my main storage. It invisibly prioritizes what I use most. It got me thinking—what if we applied this to our lives? Automatically caching our energy for what truly matters, and letting the rest just… load in the background. A quieter mind, a faster machine. Perfection.

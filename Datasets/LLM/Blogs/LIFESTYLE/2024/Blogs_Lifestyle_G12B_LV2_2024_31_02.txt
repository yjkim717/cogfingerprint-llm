## The Gentle Art of Letting Go (Especially This Thanksgiving)

Okay, let's be real. Thanksgiving in 2024 feels… weighty. Between everything swirling around, the pressure to create a picture-perfect holiday can be overwhelming. This year, I'm actively practicing the art of *letting go*. Less striving, more savoring. Maybe that means simpler recipes, accepting help, and truly *being present* with loved ones. It’s not about flawless execution; it’s about connection. What small shift are *you* making this year?

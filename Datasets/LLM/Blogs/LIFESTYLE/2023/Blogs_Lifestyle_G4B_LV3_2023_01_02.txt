**The Quiet Thrill of a Fragrance Swap**

There’s something wonderfully specific about the world of fine fragrances, isn’t there? It’s a deeply personal thing – a scent that instantly transports you, or evokes a particular memory. Lately, I’ve been noticing a trend, a little corner of the internet bubbling with excitement: fragrance swaps.

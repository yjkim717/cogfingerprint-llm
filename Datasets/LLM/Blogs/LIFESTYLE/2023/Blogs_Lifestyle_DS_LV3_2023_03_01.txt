There’s a special kind of disappointment that comes with unboxing a sleek new M2 MacBook Air, only to have it stumble right out of the gate. I was so ready to dive back into the galactic politics of Stellaris, a game I’d always associated with Paradox’s solid reputation. But there I was, staring at the same loading screen crash at 80%, over and over. It was a stark 2023 reminder that sometimes, the shiniest new tech and beloved classics just don’t get along, leaving you in a frustrating limbo.

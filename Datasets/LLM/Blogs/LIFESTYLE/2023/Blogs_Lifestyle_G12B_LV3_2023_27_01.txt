## Is "Community" the New Brand? A Late-Night Marketing Rabbit Hole

Okay, so I had one of *those* nights. The kind where you’re scrolling through social media (ironic, right?) and suddenly find yourself spiraling into a deep question. We launched our e-commerce super app a few months ago, and I’ve been wrestling with something: should we be pouring all our energy into building a thriving online community, or laser-focusing on brand association?

It feels like the old playbook – build a strong brand, then cultivate a following – is getting flipped on its head. Everyone’s talking about genuine connection, about fostering spaces where people feel *seen*. But is that sustainable for a new business? I’d love to hear from fellow marketers – are you prioritizing community building, or sticking to the tried-and-true brand-first approach? Let’s chat!

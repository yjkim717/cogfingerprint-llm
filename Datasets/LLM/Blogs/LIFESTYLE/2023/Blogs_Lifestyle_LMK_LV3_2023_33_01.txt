"Blending Passions: How Video Production Meets CS". As a self-taught programmer with a background in video production, I've often wondered how to merge my creative and technical skills. In 2023, I'm not alone in seeking a career that combines both. With the rise of video-centric platforms, video processing and programming are becoming increasingly intertwined. By leveraging tools like FFmpeg, I'm now exploring video-adjacent CS roles that bring together my passions. For those looking to bridge their own diverse interests, I share my journey and insights on turning a unique blend of skills into a fulfilling career.

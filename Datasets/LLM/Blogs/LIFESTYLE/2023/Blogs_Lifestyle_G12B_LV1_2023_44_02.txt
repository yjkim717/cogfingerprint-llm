>## The Resume Shuffle and the Fear of the Blank Space

It's 2023, and the job market still feels… precarious. I've been hunting for a while now, and lately, I’ve been doing a weird dance with my resume. There's this nagging fear, you know? The fear of the blank space, the visual representation of unemployment. 

So, I considered adding a short-term gig, something I did mostly to keep busy – not exactly a career highlight. Was it better to show *something* than nothing? It felt a bit dishonest, but then again, isn’t everyone just trying to present their best selves these days? It’s a messy feeling, this pressure to appear perpetually productive. Still wrestling with that one…

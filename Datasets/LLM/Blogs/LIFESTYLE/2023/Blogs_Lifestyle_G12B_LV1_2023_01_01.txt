
## The Unexpected Joy of Letting Go (and Finding New Scents)

There's something liberating about decluttering, isn't there? I recently realized my cherished 2016 Creed Royal Mayfair just wasn't getting the love it deserved. So, I’m embarking on a fragrance swap adventure! Hoping to find a new signature scent – maybe something intriguing from a niche house or a bold Tom Ford – it’s a reminder to embrace change and discover something fresh.

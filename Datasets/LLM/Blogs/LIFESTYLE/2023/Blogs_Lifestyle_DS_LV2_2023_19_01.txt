Last year, I decided to tackle my first big DIY project—rehanging drywall to move some outlets. I was so nervous, wondering if just mudding over the old screw holes would come back to haunt me. But you know what? It held up perfectly. That little leap into the unknown taught me that sometimes, the fixes we fret over the most are the ones that make us proudest. Here’s to trusting our hands and our hearts in 2023.

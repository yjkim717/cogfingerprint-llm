"The White Explorer Menace: A Rant on Road Safety and Entitlement". The issue isn't the vehicle itself, but the entitled behavior of some behind the wheel. It's a microcosm of our society, where individuals believe they're above the law. Let's take a step back and reassess our driving habits - and our lives. Are we prioritizing safety, or just our own convenience?

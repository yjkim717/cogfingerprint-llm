### The Two-Month Resume Dilemma: To List or Not to List?

You landed a new role just two months ago, but it’s already clear it’s not the right fit. Now, as you discreetly update your resume, you face a modern career conundrum: do you include this fleeting chapter?

Leaving it off avoids questions, but creates a confusing employment gap. Including it, however, requires a confident narrative. Frame it as a strategic, short-term role that provided crucial insight, reinforcing your decision to seek a better-aligned opportunity. In today's fluid job market, a brief stint can be a story of proactive career management, not a red flag.

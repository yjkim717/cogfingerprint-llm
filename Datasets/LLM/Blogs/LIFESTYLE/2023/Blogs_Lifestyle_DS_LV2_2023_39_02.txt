That first “yes” after a job hunt feels like a finish line. You celebrate, breathe a sigh of relief, and wait for the promised onboarding details. But when the follow-up from your new manager doesn’t come, the silence is deafening.

Suddenly, the excitement curdles into anxiety. Was the offer real? Did I do something wrong? In 2023, this hiring limbo is an oddly common part of the professional lifestyle, a strange purgatory between the old you and the new role. You’re hired, but you don't feel it, left wondering what comes next.

It’s 2023, and I still remember the knot in my stomach during my daughter’s first school lockdown drill. As a parent, you want to believe in safety, but the reality is heavy. Now, a researcher wants to photograph one for a study on stress—how it shapes our kids and us. It’s a stark reminder that these drills, while necessary, leave invisible marks. We navigate this tension daily, hoping to protect their innocence while preparing them for a world we wish were simpler.

The gentle hum of the coffee shop is my office soundtrack. Between wireframes for a new UX project, I feel a familiar pang of loneliness. I glance around, wondering if anyone else here is a kickboxing enthusiast or has ever had their tarot cards read. My current read is good, but I’d trade it in a second for a conversation about the last great movie someone saw or a life-changing book recommendation. It’s 2023, and we’re all so connected, yet sometimes the deepest connection starts with a simple, “What are you working on?”

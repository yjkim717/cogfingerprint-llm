## The Unexpected Hiccup in My Mac Gaming Dreams

So, I finally took the plunge and upgraded to an M2 Macbook Air this year – sleek, fast, everything I'd hoped for! But then I tried to fire up Stellaris, one of my favorite strategy games, and… frustration. Apparently, even with all the chatter about Mac gaming improving, compatibility isn't always a guarantee. It's a little reminder that even shiny new tech can throw you a curveball. Anyone else battling unexpected quirks lately?

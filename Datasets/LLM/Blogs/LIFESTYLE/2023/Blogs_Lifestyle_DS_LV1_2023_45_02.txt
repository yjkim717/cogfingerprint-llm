It’s 2023, and I’m knee-deep in a home refresh, staring at my dated vinyl floors. Empire Carpet claims they’re the only ones who can lay new carpet over it without a hitch—really? I got a second quote, and the installer laughed. “Preserving old flooring is common practice,” he said. It reminded me: in life and renovations, it pays to question the “only way” and trust your own instincts.

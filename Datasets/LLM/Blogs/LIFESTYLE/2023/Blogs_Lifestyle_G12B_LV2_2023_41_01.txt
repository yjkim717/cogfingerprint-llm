## The Disappearing To-Do List & Letting Go

Anyone else feel like their to-do list has a glitch? You’re *right* there, about to tackle that mountain of laundry or finally respond to those emails, and…poof! The motivation vanishes. It’s bizarre, right? 

I was thinking about it the other day, remembering a frustrating bug in Spider-Man PS4 where crimes would just…disappear as you got close.  It felt like a waste of time.  And honestly? Sometimes life feels like that too. We set intentions, build momentum, and then *bam* – distraction, exhaustion, or just plain overwhelm.

Maybe it's not about chasing everything relentlessly. Maybe it’s about recognizing when something’s fading and accepting that some things just won't get done today. Letting go can be surprisingly freeing.

## Seeing It To Believe It: The Eagles, Goddard, and My Own Little Dreams

It's been incredible watching the Philadelphia Eagles this year, hasn’t it? Seeing them make it to the Super Bowl really got me thinking. Coach Nick Sirianni has spoken about visualization, about imbuing his team with the feeling of victory *before* the game even begins. And it struck me – that’s essentially what Neville Goddard talked about decades ago.

Goddard’s teachings on manifestation, the power of feeling as if something is already true, are timeless. He emphasized the importance of vivid mental imagery paired with positive affirmations – a kind of auditory fuel for your visualizations.

It sounds a little "woo-woo," I know. But lately, I've been consciously trying to apply this to my own life. Small things, really. Visualizing a productive workday, feeling the satisfaction of finishing a project. It's a reminder that our mindset shapes our reality. Maybe the Eagles are onto something bigger than just football. Maybe we all are.

**The Weight of “Why?”**

There’s a particular kind of vulnerability that comes with being a therapist – sometimes feeling like you’re wading into a client’s deepest fears. Recently, I’ve been reflecting on conversations about the ‘meaning of life’ and ‘self-worth’ with someone struggling with suicidal thoughts. It’s a tightrope walk. 

How do you offer a lifeline without inadvertently adding to the pain?  Creating a safety plan felt like the most immediate step, but truly connecting with their sense of self… that’s where therapy’s power lies.  It’s a reminder that seeking support isn’t a sign of weakness, but a courageous act.

## The Unexpected Flooring Question and Selling Our Home

You wouldn't believe the rabbit hole I fell down last week! We're prepping our house for sale, and a friend asked a surprisingly specific question: "Can you put carpet *over* vinyl?" Apparently, it’s a trickier install than it sounds. It got me thinking about all the little details that go into selling a home – things you never consider until you're knee-deep in paint samples and flooring options. It's a whirlwind, but a strangely satisfying one, revealing so much about what we've built here.

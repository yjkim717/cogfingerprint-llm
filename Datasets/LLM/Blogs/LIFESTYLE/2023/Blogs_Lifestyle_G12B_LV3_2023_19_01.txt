## The Unexpected Lesson in Drywall (and Life)

Okay, confession time: this weekend I dove headfirst into a home improvement project – moving some outlets. Turns out, drywall isn’t as forgiving as I’d hoped! Now I’m staring at a wall full of patched screw holes, wondering if those mud lines will *ever* disappear. It's a surprisingly relatable feeling, isn't it? Like those little imperfections we try to cover up in our lives. Maybe it’s okay to let a few show. After all, they’re proof we tried, we learned, and we’re still building.

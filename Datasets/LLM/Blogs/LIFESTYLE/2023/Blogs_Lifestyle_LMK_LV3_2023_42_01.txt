"Behind the Wheel: Unpacking the Psychology of Aggressive Driving" 

Have you ever felt intimidated by a vehicle on the road? For many, the white Ford Explorer sparks unease, often mistaken for a police car. But is that the only reason? Perhaps our vehicle choices reflect our driving habits. Aggressive driving is a growing concern for road safety. As we navigate the roads in 2023, let's consider how our driving styles impact those around us. By being more mindful of our behavior, we can create a safer, more considerate driving culture, one road at a time.

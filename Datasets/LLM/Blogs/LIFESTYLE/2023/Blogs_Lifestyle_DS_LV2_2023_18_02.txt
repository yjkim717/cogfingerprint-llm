This summer, my five-year-old decided our sea glass collection needed purpose. Armed with glue and childlike wonder, we crafted simple suncatchers and tiny mosaics. It wasn’t about perfection—it was her proud smile as we arranged them on a wobbly plant stand. In a world of complex hobbies, these moments reminded me that joy lives in creating together, with little more than nature’s treasures and minimal tools. Our tiny business bloomed alongside the basil.

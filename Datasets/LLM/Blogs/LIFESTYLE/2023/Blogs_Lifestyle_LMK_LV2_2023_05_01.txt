As I wander through my suburban California neighborhood, I'm reminded of the hidden potential in every log and tree stump. It's a journey that's not just about foraging for fungi, but about connecting with nature and rethinking my relationship with the land. As I source logs for my mushroom production, I'm discovering a new sense of purpose and harmony with the world around me.

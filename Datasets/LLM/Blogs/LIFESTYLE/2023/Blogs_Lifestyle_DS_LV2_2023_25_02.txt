Last week, a client asked me, "What’s the point of it all?" My heart sank—not because I didn’t have an answer, but because I feared my words might deepen their despair. It reminded me how fragile our sense of meaning can be. In 2023, with so much noise, finding purpose isn’t about grand answers, but small, daily anchors—like a warm cup of tea or a kind text—that quietly affirm we matter.

It’s funny how life mirrors sports sometimes. I used to think my dad’s way of doing things was outdated—his rigid routines, his insistence on writing lists by hand. But lately, I’ve been feeling overwhelmed by the endless apps and life-hacks promising efficiency. In our 2023 world of digital noise, I’m starting to see the wisdom in his simpler, more deliberate approach. Maybe the old playbooks, in life as in football, hold a clarity we’ve been complicating away.

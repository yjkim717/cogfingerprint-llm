I was determined to build this custom data visualization – a little word cloud of my reading habits, a bar chart of my daily walks, even a network diagram of my friendships! Sounds intense, right?

I dove into a library called visx, thinking I'd build something beautiful and bespoke. But it was… a lot. Too many layers, too much low-level control. And I realized, I was spending more time *building* the tool than actually *using* it.

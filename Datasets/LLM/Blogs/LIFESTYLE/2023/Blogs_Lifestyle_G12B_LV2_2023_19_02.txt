## The Unexpected Beauty of Imperfection

Isn't it funny how life throws little curveballs? I was wrestling with a woodworking project the other day – an L-shaped shelf, specifically – and got totally hung up on the *direction* of the screws. Sounds silly, right? But it mirrored something bigger. I realized I'm often striving for flawless, perfectly aligned, *invisible* results.

But maybe the slight imperfection, the visible screw, is where the character lies. It’s a reminder that things are made, built, *real*. And honestly, embracing those little quirks? It feels a lot less stressful and a whole lot more beautiful. 2023 has taught me that.

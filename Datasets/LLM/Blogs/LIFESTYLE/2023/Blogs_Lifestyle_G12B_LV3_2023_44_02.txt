)_The Resume Gap Anxiety of 2023_

Okay, real talk: job searching in 2023 feels…weird. I saw a Reddit post today that really resonated. Someone was agonizing over whether to put a short-term, unremarkable job on their resume, terrified of looking like they’d been unemployed. We’ve all been there, haven't we?

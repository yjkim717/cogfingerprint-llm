## The Instant Pot and the Lost Art of Patience

Anyone else find themselves chasing kitchen shortcuts these days? I dove headfirst into Instant Pot cooking this year, envisioning cozy weeknight dinners. Let’s just say my first attempt at chicken and dumplings was… a learning experience. My dumplings dissolved into a starchy soup! It was a humbling reminder that sometimes, the slow simmer, the patient wait, still yields the best results. Maybe embracing that slower pace extends beyond the kitchen, too.

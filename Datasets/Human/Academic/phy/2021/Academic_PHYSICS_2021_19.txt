Author: Jihua Zhang
Title: Single-shot integrated multi-photon split state tomography
Paper ID: 92aa8ff1ecff0cfdb525c53807df01ba7534cd9e
Year: 2021
Author Index: 19

Abstract:
We propose a segmented coupled waveguide array as a new form of compact optical quantum circuit and apply it for the on-chip multi-photon split state tomography with optimized performance and no need of reconfigurability.

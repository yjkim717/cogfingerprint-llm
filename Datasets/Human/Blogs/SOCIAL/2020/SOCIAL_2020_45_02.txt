I was recently travelling and discovered that the magnetic lid of my AirPods case can easily deprogram your hotel card key. So when you get your iPhone 12, I'd be cautious about putting it in the same pocket with any card with a magnetic stripe, unless the phone has that shielded wallet covering up the magnet.

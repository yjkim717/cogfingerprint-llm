So... I'm guessing we all know by now the trick to increase your max health to insane levels, via a Master of Wounding + a little Ambrosia? If you use the Master of Wounding, then drink a flask of blood or cast a new wand, when his effects wear off that tiny little health boost will turn into a massive one.

"Doctor I want to have a baby but my husband is adamant that he doesn't want any children. What can I do?" The doctor tells her that the next time she goes to have sex with her husband, take a sewing needle and poke holes in the tip of the condom. The next day the woman goes back to the doctor and says, "Doctor it didn't work, after I poked holes in the condom my husband didn't want to have sex anymore, but I'm sure he didn't see me do it." The doctor says, "Yes I saw him earlier. Next time do it before you put the condom on him."
